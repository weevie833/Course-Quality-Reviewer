Mainely Kid Adventures

Mainely Kid Adventures is an organization dedicated to serving the youth of rural Maine, just outside Lewiston. Our mission is to provide enriching experiences and opportunities for growth to children who face challenges across all domains of their lives. To accommodate a larger number of children from low-income families, Mainely Kid Adventures plans to increase the frequency and duration of our After-School Adventure Program sessions.

Our organization is committed to developing experiential learning opportunities that not only meet but exceed state educational standards. We recognize the importance of hands-on, experiential learning in fostering curiosity, critical thinking, and creativity in young learners.

Investing in the professional development of our staff and board has been crucial to ensuring the delivery of high-quality programming that meets the diverse needs of our participants. Mainely Kid Adventures continues to provide ongoing training opportunities for our staff that cover many areas.

Mainely Kid Adventures recognizes the importance of actively involving parents, caregivers, and community stakeholders in our programs, too.
