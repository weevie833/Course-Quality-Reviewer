The objective here is to produce a completed grant proposal that includes the following sections:

  • Organizational Background
  • Organizational Goals/Objectives
    • Organizational Capacity
  • Project/Program Description
    • Goals/Objectives (Project/Program)
  • Statement of Need
  • Request for Support
  • Outcomes
  • Conclusion

Part of the objective here is to conduct your own research to help frame how you might approach these sections. For instance, new grant writers often must find information about the organization from the executive director. Grant funders may also need to contact funders for clarity on grant instructions.

Believe it or not, what you see up at the top of this document may have most of what some instructions contain as guidance from some funders, so to some extent you are flying blind here. With that said, I (the instructor) can serve as your point of contact for this grant. Email me with your questions, and I will provide you with guidance and role play as executive director and/or funder to provide you with a real-world experience of what it might be like to write a grant as a grant writer.

You can email me up to TWO times for assistance, so make good use of your communications. Better questions will lead to better responses from me and, in turn, a more compelling grant narrative.
