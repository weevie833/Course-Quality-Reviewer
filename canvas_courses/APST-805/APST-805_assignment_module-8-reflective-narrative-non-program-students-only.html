Consider everything you have experienced in this course and up to this point in your program.  Connect your course work, real world experiences, professional standards and what you have learned from your colleagues in this 3-5 page narrative using APA style. Use your readings to ground your reflection.  For example, you may want to cover:

  • What are the key lessons learned from this course?  From your program up to this point?
  • Reflect on the learning outcomes of this course and their importance to the overall program.
  • Describe your successes and how you achieved them.
  • Describe your challenges and what you learned from them?
  • Describe how you have grown professionally as related to the programmatic standards.
  • Reflect and describe how the skills learned from this course can or were be applied in your professional life.
  • Describe your next goals in your professional development.  Use the standards as a guideline.  

This assignment is designed to assist you in developing your Capstone e-Portfolio.  The process of reflecting on the past, present and future, is creating documentation of your professional growth over time which will be used in your final project.  You will be asked to integrate and build on your prior course work and experiences and demonstrate your professional skills through a Capstone e-Portfolio which you will create and present at the end of your program.
