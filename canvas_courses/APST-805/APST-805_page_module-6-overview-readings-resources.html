Overview

In this module, you will submit your completed grant narrative. As you vicariously experienced through this assignment, grant writers—while likely not trained as strategists—often find themselves on the front lines of an organization or deep within its very heart in order to write a grant. 

The degree to which a grant writer can understand the larger picture that surrounds the need for funding could mean the difference between a successful ask and one that remains at the proverbial bottom of a grant reviewer’s desk.

As you reflect on this process, consider what you think are the biggest challenges in not only writing a grant, but supporting it with documentation and assurances that a successful grant has the intended impact (the outcomes).
