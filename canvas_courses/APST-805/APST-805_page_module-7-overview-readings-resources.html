Overview

In this module, you will turn in your completed chapter contribution for the OER. You are also invited to reflect on the grant narrative writing process. What have been the key takeaways for you?

As you reflect, please consider that well-executed grants are not merely written documents that make linear sense on a page, but a blueprint for real activities that involve real people with real outcomes in the world—all of which is made possible by a funder. Involving various inputs and stakeholders, grant management and stewardship includes consistent and mindful engagement with the funder.
