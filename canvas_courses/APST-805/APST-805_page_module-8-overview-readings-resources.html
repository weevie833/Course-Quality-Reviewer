Overview

In this last module, you will reflect on the open educational resource/open pedagogy experience and provide a few different assessments about your experience.  

While working on your self-assessment, consider the importance of what it means to make a compelling statement for support within a grant narrative. This statement is, to some extent, a story. How might a consideration of storytelling influence your grant writing strategy? 

In this last module, you may also continue to work either as a class or in small groups to finalize your content for the OER, which will be published at the conclusion of class.
