Assignment: Contracts and SLAs in Your Hypothetical IT Department

For this assignment, you will develop a detailed section of your business proposal focused on external support needs for your hypothetical IT department. This will be Section 2: Contracts in your proposal. Your task involves analyzing your IT operations, identifying external support requirements, and providing clear justifications for your decisions. Follow the structured outline below to ensure clarity and depth in your work:

Instructions

  1. Identify External Support Needs (1–2 pages):

  • Clearly outline the areas of your IT department that require external support (e.g., cloud hosting, cybersecurity, software development, hardware maintenance, or network administration).
  • Explain the specific need in each area (e.g., limited in-house expertise, cost-efficiency, scalability).
  • Provide a rationale for seeking external contractors rather than relying solely on internal resources.

  1. Define Contractor Engagement and Justification (1 page):

    • For each identified need, describe the nature of the engagement you would establish with the contractor. Address questions such as:

      • What tasks or responsibilities will the contractor handle?

      • How will their role align with your department's overall strategy and objectives?

    • Justify your choice of contractor(s) by considering factors like expertise, cost, reputation, or alignment with organizational goals.

  2. Specify Contract Type or SLA (1–2 pages):

    • For each external support need, specify the type of contract you would use (e.g., fixed-price contract, time-and-materials contract) or whether an SLA would be more appropriate.

    • If using an SLA, outline key elements such as:

      • Service performance metrics (e.g., uptime, response times).

      • Penalties or recourse if the SLA terms are not met.

    • Explain why your chosen contract type or SLA is the best fit for that particular engagement.

  3. Integrate the Assignment into Your Proposal:

    • Ensure this document is seamlessly incorporated into your broader business proposal. It should form Section 2: Contracts of your proposal.

    • Use headings, subheadings, and bullet points to organize your content for clarity and professionalism.

Formatting Requirements

  • Length: 3 to 4 pages, double-spaced.
  • Format: Use a professional tone and structure with proper headings and subheadings.
  • Citation: If referencing any sources or examples, provide appropriate citations in APA format.
  • Submission: Include this section as part of your comprehensive business proposal.
