Assignment: Developing a Preliminary Budget for an IT Department

For this assignment, you will create a preliminary budget for your hypothetical IT department. This document will serve as Section 4: Budget in your business proposal. It should detail salary costs, hardware, and software infrastructure expenses and distinguish between capital and operational expenses. Use Excel for the budget breakdown and provide a Word document to explain the estimation process.

Assignment Components and Steps:

1 - Introduction to the Budget (Half a Page):

  • Begin your Word document with a brief introduction explaining the purpose of the preliminary budget in supporting the IT department's operations and strategic goals.
  • Highlight the key components of the budget (e.g., salaries, hardware, software) and the importance of distinguishing between capital and operational expenses.

2 - Budget Breakdown in Excel (2 Pages):

  • Create an Excel spreadsheet that organizes your budget into the following categories:
    • Salary Costs:
      • List each projected role (e.g., IT Manager, System Administrator, Network Engineer).
      • Include headcount for each role and estimated annual salaries or hourly rates.
    • Hardware Infrastructure:
      • Provide preliminary costs for servers, networking equipment, workstations, and other physical IT assets.
    • Software Infrastructure:
      • Estimate costs for software licenses, cloud services, and IT management tools.
    • Capital Expenses:
      • Highlight one-time purchases, such as new hardware or setup fees.
    • Operational Expenses:
      • Include ongoing costs, such as subscriptions, maintenance, and salaries.
  • Ensure totals for each category and an overall total are clearly displayed.

3 - Description of Budget Development Process (1–1.5 Pages):

  • Write a Word document to explain the process you used to estimate costs. Include:
    • Salary Costs: Describe how you determined the headcount and salary estimates (e.g., market research, average industry salaries).
    • Hardware Costs: Explain how you estimated hardware needs based on the department's projected workload and infrastructure requirements.
    • Software Costs: Discuss considerations for licensing, scalability, and alignment with the department's goals.
    • Capital vs. Operational Expenses: Provide a brief justification for categorizing each expense as capital or operational.

4 - Integration into the Business Proposal:

  • Ensure that the Excel budget and the accompanying Word document explaining your methodology are cohesive and professional.
  • Insert this content into the Budget Section (Section 4) of your business proposal, using consistent formatting and structure.

Formatting Guidelines:

  • Length: Combined, the Excel spreadsheet and Word document should be 3 to 4 pages.
    • Excel should account for 1 to 2 pages, including the budget breakdown.
    • The Word document should account for 1 to 2 pages, describing the estimation process.
  • Professional Presentation: Use clear labels, formatting, and structure in both documents for easy readability.
  • Tone: Maintain a professional and formal tone in the Word document.
  • References: If any data sources (e.g., salary ranges, hardware pricing) were used, include citations in APA 7th edition format in the Word document.
Note: This assignment is designated Level 1 - No AI. The assignment must be completed entirely without AI assistance. The assignment must be produced relying solely on conventional research and study methods. AI must not be used at any point during the production of the assignment.
