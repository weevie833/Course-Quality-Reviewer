Scenario:
You have just been hired as the IT Manager for a fast-growing e-commerce startup that is expanding its operations across multiple regions. The company currently runs its operations on basic off-the-shelf software, scattered spreadsheets for inventory and customer data, and aging desktop computers. There is no centralized database, and cybersecurity protocols are minimal.

As part of your new role, you've been asked to propose a scalable, secure, and integrated IT infrastructure that will support the company's future growth.
