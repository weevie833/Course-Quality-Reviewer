Scenario:
You are the IT Manager for a health and wellness company that collects sensitive personal data from users through a mobile app. This includes names, addresses, fitness data, and some health-related information. The marketing department wants access to this data to build targeted campaigns, including using third-party analytics platforms.

You are aware that while sharing the data might help the company grow its customer base, it could raise serious privacy, ethical, and legal concerns, especially in light of data protection regulations like GDPR or HIPAA (depending on your location and industry).

Additionally, you recently discovered that one of your IT staff members has been accessing user data outside of work hours without clear justification. You're unsure whether it's curiosity or something more serious.

Discussion Prompt:

  3. Policy Development: What policies or safeguards would you implement to balance data utility with user privacy and regulatory compliance?

Support your response with relevant laws, industry guidelines, or examples from professional experience.
