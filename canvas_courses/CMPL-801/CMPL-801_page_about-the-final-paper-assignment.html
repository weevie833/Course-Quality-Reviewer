In this course, you will develop a business proposal to establish a new IT department within an organization. You will be asked to populate a new segment of that proposal every week, reflecting that week’s learning. The proposal will include a discussion of the governance of the new department, including its position within the organization’s structure.  It will also be aligned with ITIL best practices. 

The final proposal should highlight the technology needed, financial implications, risk assessments, compliance and security, and technology adoption guidelines. It should also explain the proposed department’s structure and identify the various levels of support it will provide to the overall organization.

The completed proposal will be submitted in Module 7.
