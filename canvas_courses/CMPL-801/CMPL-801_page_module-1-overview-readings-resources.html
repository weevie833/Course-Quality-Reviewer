Overview

This chapter defines strategic planning and outlines an effective process for accomplishing this critical activity. It also clarifies why the information technology (IT) organization plans must align with the values, mission, and vision of the larger organization. Additionally, this chapter shows how organizations can prioritize the initiatives and projects presented to meet those objectives. Today’s organizations need people who can develop strategic plans and use technology to realize corporate benefits.

Module Objectives:

  • Discuss the traditional and contemporary view of the role that information systems play in an organization’s value chains.
  • Identify three services the IT organization provides to an organization.
  • Describe the structure of a hypothetical IT department.
