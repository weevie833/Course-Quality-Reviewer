Overview

A significant portion of a business’s financial assets are consumed by technology. In this week’s readings, you will be introduced to the development and management of an IT budget. You will learn of different categories of expenses, such as capitalized or operational, and the tax implications associated with each.

Module Objectives:

  • Discuss the various components of an IT department budget.
  • Describe the cost savings benefits associated with server virtualization.
  • Identify five common major IT Budget mistakes and how to correct them.
  • Produce a defensible budget for your hypothetical IT department.
