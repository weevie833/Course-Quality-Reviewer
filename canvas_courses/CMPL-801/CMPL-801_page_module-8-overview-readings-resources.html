Overview

In this module, you will explore methodologies and strategies to foster ongoing improvement in IT processes and systems. The focus will be on aligning IT initiatives with business objectives to maximize value, using frameworks like Lean, Six Sigma, and Agile. Practical case studies will illustrate how continuous improvement can lead to enhanced efficiency, innovation, and competitive advantage in the business landscape.

Module Objectives:

  • Identify the primary difference between business process reengineering and continuous improvement.
  • Describe the two types of innovation.
  • Identify and explain the differences between 3 popular continuous improvement methods.
