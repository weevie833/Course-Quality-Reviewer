Assignment: Reflection Paper – Emerging Technologies

Length: 3–4 pages (excluding title page and references)
Format: APA 7th Edition
Due: End of Week 1

Assignment Overview

In this first assignment, you will reflect on the key concepts introduced in the chapter "Introduction to Emerging Technologies." The chapter provides a foundational understanding of what emerging technologies are, how they have evolved, and their transformative effects across different business sectors.

This paper is your opportunity to demonstrate initial insight into the current landscape of emerging technologies, analyze major trends, and consider how these innovations might influence IT management and strategic planning in both the short and long term.

Assignment Objectives

  • Reflect on the relevance and potential impact of selected emerging technologies
  • Identify and analyze current technology trends that influence IT leadership and management
  • Begin developing a critical perspective on how businesses can adapt to and leverage emerging tools

Your Paper Should Include the Following Sections

1 - Introduction

  • Briefly introduce the concept of emerging technologies as discussed in the chapter and readings
  • Mention why understanding emerging technologies is important for IT managers

2 - Summary of Key Concepts from the Readings

  • Highlight 2–3 key takeaways from the chapter and readings
  • Discuss how these concepts set the stage for understanding the broader role of innovation in business

3 - Current Trends in Emerging Technologies

  • Identify and describe at least two current trends in emerging technology (e.g., AI and automation, quantum computing, blockchain, augmented reality, edge computing, etc.)
  • Reference course materials or external sources to support your descriptions
  • Connect these trends to how they may influence IT management or business strategy

4 - Your Perspective: What's Next?

  • Share your thoughts on where you believe emerging technology is heading
  • Choose one emerging technology that you believe will be most impactful in the short term (1–3 years) and explain why
  • Consider factors like scalability, market demand, ethical or legal concerns, or cross-industry relevance

5 - Conclusion

  • Recap your main points
  • Offer a closing thought on why it's essential for IT managers to stay informed about emerging technologies

Formatting Guidelines

  • Length: 3–4 pages (not including title or references)
  • Font: 12-point Times New Roman, double-spaced
  • Margins: 1 inch on all sides
  • APA 7 formatting: Title page, in-text citations, and a reference page
  • Cite at least two sources (course textbook or scholarly/industry resources)
