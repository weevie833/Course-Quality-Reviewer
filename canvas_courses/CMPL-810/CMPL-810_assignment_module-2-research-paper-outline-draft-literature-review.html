Assignment: Research Paper Outline & Draft Literature Review

Due: End of Week 2
Length: Up to 3 pages
Purpose: To establish the structure of your final research paper and begin developing the literature review section.

Assignment Overview

As you prepare for your final research paper, this assignment asks you to complete two critical components:

  1. A detailed outline of your planned paper structure
  2. A draft literature review based on your selected topic

This submission will help ensure you are on track with your research focus and provide an opportunity for early feedback.

Assignment Instructions

Your submission should include the following two sections:

1 - Research Paper Outline

  • Create a structured outline for your final paper that includes:
    • Working title of your research paper
    • Introduction and thesis statement
    • Main sections and key points you plan to cover in each (e.g., background, analysis, methodology, implications, conclusion)
    • Preliminary ideas for data or evidence you will use (if applicable)
    • This does not need to be fully developed prose — bullet points or brief descriptions are acceptable

2 - Draft Literature Review

  • Conduct a preliminary literature review of your selected topic using a minimum of five reputable external sources (not including the course textbook). Acceptable sources include industry publications (e.g., McKinsey, CIO), scholarly books, peer-reviewed journals, and credible websites.

For each source, provide:

  • Author name(s) and publication date
  • Title and source (e.g., journal name, website, book title)
  • Access information (URL for web articles, ISBN for books)
  • A brief summary of key findings
  • A justification for including the source (e.g., relevance, credibility, insight it provides on your topic)

Formatting Guidelines

  • Length: Up to 3 pages total
  • Font: 12-point Times New Roman
  • Spacing: Double-spaced
  • Use APA 7th edition for in-text citations and references (if applicable)
  • Submit as a Word or PDF document
Note: This assignment is designated Level 1 - No AI. The assignment must be completed entirely without AI assistance. The assignment must be produced relying solely on conventional research and study methods. AI must not be used at any point during the production of the assignment.
