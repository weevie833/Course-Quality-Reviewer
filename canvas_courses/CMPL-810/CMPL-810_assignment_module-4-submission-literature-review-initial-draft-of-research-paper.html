Assignment: Literature Review & Initial Draft of Research Paper

Length: First 10 pages of your research paper, including the literature review
Purpose: To provide a substantial draft for review and feedback ahead of your final 20+ page research paper submission

Assignment Overview

As you progress toward your final research paper, this assignment requires you to submit the first 10 pages of your paper, which must include the completed literature review section. This draft will allow your instructor to provide meaningful feedback on the structure, depth of analysis, and use of sources before the final version is submitted at the end of the course.

Assignment Requirements

Your submission should include:

  1. Title Page (APA 7 format)
  2. Introduction
    • Clearly present your topic, purpose, and research question(s)
  3. Literature Review Section
    • A fully developed literature review that synthesizes relevant sources and establishes the scholarly or industry context for your topic
  4. Body Content
    • Additional sections of your paper (methodology, background, analysis, etc.) that demonstrate the structure and direction of your argument
    • Your total draft should include at least 10 full pages of written content, not including the title or reference pages
  5. Reference Page
    • Properly formatted in APA 7 style

Formatting Guidelines

  • Minimum 10 pages of written content (excluding title and reference pages)
  • Font: 12-point Times New Roman
  • Spacing: Double-spaced
  • Citations and references in APA 7th edition format
  • Submit as a Word or PDF document
Note: This assignment is designated Level 1 - No AI. The assignment must be completed entirely without AI assistance. The assignment must be produced relying solely on conventional research and study methods. AI must not be used at any point during the production of the assignment.
