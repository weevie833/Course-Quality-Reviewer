Overview

In the first week, participants are introduced to the concept of emerging technologies, focusing on current trends and their historical impact on various business sectors. This foundational knowledge sets the stage for deeper exploration in subsequent weeks. Discussion will center on the transformative power of technology and initial reflections on its business implications.

Module Objectives:

  • Develop a perspective on the relevance and impact of selected emerging technologies
  • Develop a perspective on emerging technology key trends that impact IT management.
