Overview

Building on the introduction in Week 1, Week 3 dives into AI and ML, examining their principles and real-world applications. Participants will analyze successful business implementations, connecting AI/ML capabilities to business innovation. Reflections will help solidify understanding of how these technologies can drive efficiency and growth.

Module Objectives:

  • Compare and contrast the methods of AI deployment.
  • Develop a perspective on the role of IT management in AI innovation.

Readings

  • Read Chapter 5 and 6, Vincent and Igou (36 pp)
  • Read: Artificial Intelligence for the Real World (19 pp)
  • Read: The State of AI in 2025 (23 pp)
