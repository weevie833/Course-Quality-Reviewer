Overview

Week 7 introduces AR and VR, focusing on their business applications and potential to create immersive experiences. Participants will analyze case studies and brainstorm applications in their own industries. This builds on the previous technology discussions, highlighting how AR/VR can enhance user interaction and business processes.

Module Objectives:

  • Conduct a comprehensive analysis of the use of AR/VR in a corporate setting.
  • Produce a completed draft of your Emerging Technology Research paper.

Readings

  • Read Chapter 13, Vincent and Igou (14pp)
  • Read: Seeing is believing: How VR and AR will transform business and the economy (30pp)
  • Read: 5 Best Augmented Reality Use Cases (7 pp)
