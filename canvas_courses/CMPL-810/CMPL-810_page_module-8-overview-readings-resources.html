Overview

As businesses adopt new technologies, cybersecurity becomes crucial. Week 8 addresses advancements in cybersecurity, with case studies on breaches and responses. This week reinforces the importance of secure technology integration, relating back to previous discussions on AI, blockchain, and IoT security needs.

Module Objectives:

  • Revise and submit a final version of your Emerging Technology research paper.
  • Produce a narrative reflection on your learning experience for this course.
