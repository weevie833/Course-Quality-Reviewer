About the use of AI in this course

Discussion Topics: You will be presented with discussion topics each week that relate to the information in the chapter that we are working on, issues in the AI industry, or related to the development of your final project. You will be required to give your thorough opinion on the topic and engage with classmates by replying to the posts of at least two classmates. Initial posts are due Thursday at 11:59 p.m. ET and responses to classmates by Sunday at 11:59 p.m. ET.

Final Project-Weekly Modules: Each week, you will be required to complete a specific deliverable toward the build of your Final Project. Your completed modules should demonstrate what you have learned in this course and/or your industry experience.

Prepare a PowerPoint presentation (minimum 12 slides including your Title and References slides) with audio narration on each slide that presents an overview of your project. It is expected that you will use an attractive slide template that uses color effectively, uses bullet points rather than paragraphs of text, and includes images that illustrate the business environment and industry in which the project may be used.

Final Project Paper: The Final Project paper should demonstrate the highest quality academic writing with clear, concise, and persuasive writing with the expectation that it will be used as a guide by other industry professionals. Sections should include

      1. a high-level summary of the project topic you chose,
      2. an explanation of the business problem or purpose that the project intends to solve,
      3. an overview of the phases of the work you conducted,
      4. the metrics that should be used to evaluate the project’s success,
      5. a timeline for the development of each stage of the project,
      6. the estimated budget and projected costs for the development and deployment of the IT solution you have created.

It is expected that you will use your weekly module submissions to help build the content of your final paper.  (12-16 pages; single-spaced; APA format)
