Overview

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming corporate functions across various industries, revolutionizing how businesses operate, make decisions, and interact with customers. This module explores the integration of AI into key corporate functions such as marketing, finance, human resources, supply chain management, and customer service. By understanding the applications, benefits, challenges, and ethical considerations of AI in these areas, students will be equipped to leverage AI technologies to enhance business performance and drive strategic initiatives.

By the end of this module, students will be able to:

  • Understand the role of AI in various corporate functions and its impact on business operations.
  • Identify and evaluate AI applications in marketing, finance, human resources, supply chain management, and customer service.
  • Analyze the benefits and challenges of integrating AI into corporate functions.
  • Discuss ethical considerations and best practices for responsible AI implementation in business.
  • Propose strategies for effectively leveraging AI to achieve corporate objectives and drive innovation.
