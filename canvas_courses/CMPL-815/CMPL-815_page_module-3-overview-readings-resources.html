Overview

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is revolutionizing Research and Development (R&D) and driving business innovation across various industries. This module explores how AI technologies are transforming R&D processes, fostering innovation, and creating new opportunities for growth and competitiveness. Students will learn about the applications, benefits, challenges, and strategic considerations of integrating AI into R&D and innovation activities.

By the end of this module, students will be able to:

  • Understand the role of AI in enhancing R&D and fostering business innovation.
  • Identify and evaluate AI applications in different stages of the R&D process.
  • Analyze the benefits and challenges of using AI to drive business innovation.
  • Discuss ethical considerations and best practices for implementing AI in R&D and innovation.
  • Propose strategies for leveraging AI to achieve competitive advantage and sustainable growth.
