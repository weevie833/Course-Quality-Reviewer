Overview

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming customer service, sales, and marketing by enabling more personalized, efficient, and effective interactions with customers. This module explores the various applications of AI in these areas, highlighting how AI technologies can enhance customer experiences, drive sales growth, and optimize marketing strategies. Students will learn about the benefits, challenges, and ethical considerations of integrating AI into customer-facing functions and will develop strategies for leveraging AI to achieve business goals.

By the end of this module, students will be able to:

  • Understand the role of AI in enhancing customer service, sales, and marketing.
  • Identify and evaluate AI applications in customer engagement, sales processes, and marketing strategies.
  • Analyze the benefits and challenges of using AI in customer service, sales, and marketing.
  • Discuss ethical considerations and best practices for implementing AI in these functions.
  • Propose strategies for effectively leveraging AI to achieve business objectives and improve customer experiences.
