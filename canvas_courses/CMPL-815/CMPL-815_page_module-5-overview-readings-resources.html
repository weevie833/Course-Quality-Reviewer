Overview

As Artificial Intelligence (AI) becomes increasingly integrated into various aspects of society, the role of humans in ensuring its responsible development and deployment is crucial. This module examines the three key roles that humans play in creating and managing responsible AI: the role of the ethical overseer, the technical developer, and the regulatory enforcer. Understanding these roles and their interplay is essential for fostering AI that is ethical, effective, and aligned with societal values.

By the end of this module, students will be able to:

  • Understand the three key roles humans play in developing and deploying responsible AI.
  • Identify and analyze the responsibilities and challenges associated with each role.
  • Discuss ethical considerations and best practices for ensuring responsible AI.
  • Propose strategies for effective collaboration between these roles to promote responsible AI development and deployment.
