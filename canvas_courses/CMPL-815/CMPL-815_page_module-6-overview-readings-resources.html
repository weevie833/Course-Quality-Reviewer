Overview

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming industries by driving unprecedented levels of productivity, fostering growth, and sparking creativity. This module explores the multifaceted ways in which AI achieves these outcomes, delving into specific technologies, applications, and strategies that businesses can leverage to harness the full potential of AI. By understanding the transformative power of AI, readers will gain insights into how to strategically implement AI solutions to achieve significant competitive advantages.

By the end of this module, students will be able to:

  1. Understand the key ways AI enhances productivity, including automation and process optimization.
  2. Identify how AI drives business growth through data-driven decision-making and market innovation.
  3. Recognize the potential of generative AI and human-AI collaboration in sparking creativity and innovation.
  4. Evaluate the ethical and practical considerations of implementing AI in business.
  5. Develop strategies for overcoming challenges and maximizing the benefits of AI in their organizations.
