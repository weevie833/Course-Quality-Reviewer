Overview

In the dynamic business environment, reimagining business processes is essential for staying competitive, improving efficiency, and driving innovation. This module explores the actual steps involved in reimagining business processes and provides a strategic framework for managers to effectively lead these transformations. By understanding these steps and managerial approaches, students will be equipped to implement significant changes that align with organizational goals and market demands.

By the end of this module, students will be able to:

  • Understand the importance of reimagining business processes in the context of continuous improvement and innovation.
  • Identify and analyze the steps involved in reimagining business processes.
  • Evaluate managerial approaches for leading business process reengineering (BPR) initiatives.
  • Discuss best practices and strategies for successfully reimagining business processes.
  • Develop a strategic plan for managing business process transformations within their organizations.
