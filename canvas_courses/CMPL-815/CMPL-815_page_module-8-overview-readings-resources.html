Overview

As Artificial Intelligence (AI) and machine learning technologies continue to advance, the collaboration between humans and machines is becoming increasingly vital. This module explores how to extend human-machine collaboration through the development and application of eight critical fusion skills. These skills are essential for leveraging the strengths of both humans and machines to achieve superior outcomes in various industries.

By the end of this module, students will be able to:

  • Understand the concept of fusion skills and their importance in human-machine collaboration.
  • Identify and analyze each of the eight fusion skills, understanding their roles and applications.
  • Evaluate the benefits and challenges of integrating these skills into organizational practices.
  • Discuss strategies for developing and enhancing fusion skills within teams.
  • Propose practical applications of fusion skills to improve collaboration and productivity.
