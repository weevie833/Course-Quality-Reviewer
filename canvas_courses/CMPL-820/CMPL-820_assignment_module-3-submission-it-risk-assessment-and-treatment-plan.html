Part 1: Risk Identification and Assessment

Choose an organization (real or hypothetical) and analyze its current IT infrastructure. Identify at least five potential vulnerabilities in its information security posture.

  • For each identified vulnerability, assess the likelihood and potential impact of a successful exploitation, using qualitative or quantitative risk assessment methods.

Part 2: Risk Treatment Strategy

  • Based on your assessment, propose a detailed risk treatment plan for the organization. This should include specific recommendations for mitigating, transferring, accepting, or terminating each identified risk.
  • Provide a rationale for each recommendation, considering factors such as cost, business objectives, and industry best practices.

General requirements and instructions to complete your assignment

  • Use APA format for in-text citations and your reference page.
