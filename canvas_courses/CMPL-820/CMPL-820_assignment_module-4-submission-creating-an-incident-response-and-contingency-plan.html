Objective:
Develop a practical incident response and contingency plan for a hypothetical organization, ensuring it’s ethical, legal, and sustainable.

Instructions:

Part 1: Describe Your Organization

Start by outlining your hypothetical organization:

  • Industry: (e.g., finance, healthcare)
  • Size: (e.g., small business, large corporation)
  • IT Setup: (e.g., cloud, servers)
  • Key Assets: (e.g., customer data, intellectual property)

Identify the top threats and vulnerabilities the organization might face.

Part 2: Develop an Incident Response Plan

Create a strategy to handle security incidents:

  • Detection: How will you spot incidents?
  • Classification: How will you rate their severity?
  • Response Actions: What steps will your team take? Who’s responsible for what?
  • Communication: How will you keep everyone informed?
  • Legal and Ethical Considerations: Ensure compliance with laws and ethical standards.

Part 3: Plan for Contingencies

Prepare a backup plan:

  • Disaster Recovery: How will you recover from major failures?
  • Business Continuity: How will you keep critical operations running?
  • Testing and Maintenance: How will you ensure the plan stays effective?

Part 4: Align with IT Governance and Compliance

Explain how your plans fit into the organization’s IT strategy:

  • How does it align with overall IT governance?
  • How does it ensure legal compliance?
  • How does it support long-term growth and sustainability?

General requirements and instructions to complete your assignment

  • 6-8 pages in length.
  • Use APA v7 format for in-text citations and your reference page.
  • Type your responses in a Microsoft Word document.
