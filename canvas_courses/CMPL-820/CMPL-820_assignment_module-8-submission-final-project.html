  5. Formulate a strategic IT governance framework to ensure the organization sustains operational growth, security compliance, and ISO/IES 27001 standards (CLO 8).
  6. Evaluate the impact of emerging technologies and propose their integration to support business growth and optimize organizational performance (PLO 6).
