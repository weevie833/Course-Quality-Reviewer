Welcome to ITM 820: Information Privacy, Security, and Continuity!

This graduate-level course is designed to equip IT managers with the critical skills necessary to handle information security, privacy, and continuity in today's complex IT environments. Over the next eight weeks, you will engage with various aspects of IT security management, including threat assessment, risk mitigation, incident response, and legal and regulatory compliance.

Major Assignments

Throughout the course, you will complete a series of assignments designed to deepen your understanding and application of IT security principles:

  • Discussion Posts: Regular discussions will allow you to engage with your peers on key course topics, fostering a collaborative learning environment.
  • Written Assignments: You will work on case studies where you will evaluate security breaches, create risk assessments, and design security strategies. The final project will require you to integrate the knowledge gained throughout the course by developing a comprehensive IT security plan for an organization.
  • Final Project: Students will design and implement an integrated IT infrastructure for a mid-sized organization, utilizing virtualization, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence technologies to enhance operational efficiency and drive innovation
