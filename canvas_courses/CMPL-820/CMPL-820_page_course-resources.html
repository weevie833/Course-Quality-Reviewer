Assignments

Discussion Questions: Each week, you will be required to answer one or two discussion questions. Discussion questions are prompts designed to encourage thoughtful responses and meaningful interactions among students. These questions typically ask you to reflect on course readings, lectures, or other materials and to share your ideas, insights, or opinions in a discussion forum. In your response, you should provide a well-thought-out answer that shows your understanding of the topic, supports your points with examples, and engages with your classmates’ ideas when appropriate. Active participation in these discussions is essential for deepening your learning and building a collaborative learning environment.

Written Assignments: Each week, you will be required to complete a module assignment that focuses on a specific topic covered in the course. These assignments will typically be written essays that ask you to apply concepts learned in the course to real-world scenarios. Examples of assignments may include writing a case study analysis, developing a security plan, or creating a contingency plan.
Your submissions should demonstrate critical thinking, thorough analysis, and a clear understanding of the course material. Be sure to follow the assignment instructions closely, provide supporting evidence for your points, and meet the required formatting and submission guidelines. Weekly module assignments are designed to deepen your knowledge of key topics and give you hands-on experience with applying concepts to practical situations.

Final Project: The final project for this course will involve designing and implementing an integrated IT infrastructure for a mid-sized organization, focusing on enhancing operational efficiency and innovation through the use of virtualization, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence technologies. This project allows you to apply the concepts and skills learned throughout the course to a real-world scenario.
