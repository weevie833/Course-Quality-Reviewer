Overview

Protecting information is one of the most important tasks an organization must monitor around the clock and regardless of where personnel are located. In this module, you will study the purpose of information security and how it is needed in organizations.

Next, you will study why a successful information security program is the shared responsibility of the entire organization and not just departments that focus on technology. In the second half of the module, emphasis is placed on threats that occur to trigger information security solutions and common attacks of them. The final part of the module lists common information security issues that result from poor software development efforts.
