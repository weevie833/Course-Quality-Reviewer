Overview

In Module 3, you will discuss information security management in sustaining organizational security in a world that is becoming more technologically advanced. This entails comprehending the aspects of information security management as a guide to the protection of information through management implementation of policies, procedures, standard and practices.

Elements of security education, training of security personnel and development of a security plan are also provided in the current module but can be considered as forming a base for the subsequent higher level courses. In Module 4, risk management is discussed more specifically in terms of identification, assessment, and to an extent, risk reduction to acceptable levels, as well as security control evaluations, quantitative and qualitative risk analysis.
