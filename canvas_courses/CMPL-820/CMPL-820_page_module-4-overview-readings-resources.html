Overview

This week’s readings focus on critical aspects of information security, highlighting the importance of both contingency planning and personnel management. Module 5 addresses the inevitability of organizational interruptions, emphasizing the need for disaster recovery, business continuity planning, and incident response through digital forensics, with a focus on testing plans for preparedness. Module 6 explores the role of personnel in maintaining security, discussing the responsibilities, credentials, and employment practices that support the effort, along with the importance of personnel management in safeguarding data.

  • Module 6 - Legal, Ethical, and Professional Issues in Information Security (36 pages)
