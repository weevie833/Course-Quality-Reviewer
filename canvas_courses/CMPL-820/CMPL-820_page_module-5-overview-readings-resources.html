Overview

This week is devoted to the study of the importance of security technologies, including access controls, firewalls, and VPNs for the organization’s assets. You will take a critical step in determining whether these technologies meet standards in the industry and their effectiveness in achieving IT security and organizational goals.

Besides, the week focuses on the personnel aspect of security, especially in relation to training and readiness in handling such systems. Some of the discussions will revolve around issues such as incorporating security technology within the organization’s current structure and the consideration of new development to improve security and productivity.
