Overview

In this week, we will discuss integration of the advanced IT infrastructure solutions such as virtualization, cloud computing & artificial Intelligence, keeping security compliant to ISO/IEC 27001. You will also be able to learn the features of building a secure IT infrastructure, estimating risks, and applying cryptographic measures for data security.

This week's work includes developing IT continuity documents as part of an incident response plan, outlining an initial access control policy proposal, and proposing sustainable IT governance. We will also consider the relative potential of emergent technologies to generate business opportunities.
