Welcome to CMPL 825, Designing and Analyzing Information Systems

Systems Analysis & Design presents a comprehensive journey through the systems development life cycle (SDLC). The course is structured to support students with a straightforward, systematic approach to understanding and applying the principles and practices of systems development. It covers the essential phases from planning and analysis through design and implementation to operations and security management, enriched with real-world examples and contemporary methodologies.

Course Learning Outcomes

  1. Develop a feasible Business Intelligence Strategy according to an organization's goals and objectives.
  2. Integrate the key concepts and principles of Big Data into a Business Intelligence Strategy using the Hadoop Ecosystem.
  3. Evaluate the implications of the Software Development Lifecycle on long-term systems planning within an organization's IT department.
  4. Transform the Information System (IS) and Decision Support System (DSS) to serve the needs and requirements of an organization’s Business Intelligence Strategy.
  5. Design a data organization and processing system that serves an organization’s KPI requirements for executive decision-making.
