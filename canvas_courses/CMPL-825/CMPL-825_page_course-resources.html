Assignments

Weekly Discussion Assignment

Each week, you will participate in a discussion assignment that encourages collaboration and critical thinking on key course topics. For each discussion, you will research the topic, share your insights, and engage in meaningful conversations with your peers by responding to their posts.

Written Assignments

Each week, you will complete a written assignment that allows you to apply key course concepts in a structured format. These assignments are designed to develop your analytical and technical writing skills while reinforcing your understanding of course materials through research, analysis, and practical application.

Final Project

The final project for this course is a comprehensive culmination of the work you’ve done throughout the semester. You will be required to submit a Final Report along with a Presentation to demonstrate your understanding of the course concepts and showcase the prototype you have developed.

 

Appendices

The appendices provide additional resources on communications and finance, offering insights and tools relevant to systems development projects that are not fully covered in the main chapters. These sections guide effective communication strategies for project teams and stakeholders and financial analysis techniques for budgeting, cost–benefit analysis, and financial planning of systems projects.

Appendix A – Communications: Appendix A discusses communication tools to help the systems analyst write clearly, speak effectively, and deliver powerful presentations—in person or online. (Page 516 of Ebook)

Appendix B – Finance: Appendix B covers the financial aspects of systems analysis, including cost–benefit analysis, cost management, and financial tools for project evaluation. (Page 542 of Ebook)
