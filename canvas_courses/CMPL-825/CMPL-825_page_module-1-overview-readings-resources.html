Overview

Big data refers to large, complex datasets that traditional tools struggle to process, requiring advanced technologies for analysis. Business intelligence (BI) involves leveraging data to gain actionable insights and drive decision-making.  This wee we explore the core components of Big Data and Business Intelligence (BI) and their impact on organizational analysis and decision-making. It examines the differences between Big Data, BI, and traditional data analysis, highlighting the advantages and applications of modern approaches.

The role of Big Data and BI in influencing decisions across finance, marketing, and operations is analyzed, showing how they drive better outcomes. Case studies of successful Big Data and BI implementations illustrate their benefits, revealing how organizations have achieved business goals and performance improvements.
