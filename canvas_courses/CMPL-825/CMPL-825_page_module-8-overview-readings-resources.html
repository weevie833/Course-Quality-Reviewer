Overview

Risk identification involves steps like asset identification, threat assessment, and vulnerability analysis. assess key aspects of a project or report. Depth of Analysis measures the understanding of big data, BI strategies, Hadoop tools, and SDLC methodologies. Reflection and Insight gauge how well the decision-making process, challenges faced, and lessons learned are articulated.

Technical Accuracy focuses on the correct description of technical components and their integration within the system. Organization and Clarity ensure that information is presented in a clear, logical, and structured way. Use of Evidence evaluates the ability to support reflections and analyses with data, examples, and references. Finally, Professionalism emphasizes a formal tone and adherence to submission guidelines.
