Purpose: As you begin your capstone journey, this assignment gives you the opportunity to explore and share a few early ideas you’re considering for your project. The goal is not to finalize your topic, but to begin thinking critically about the direction you might take based on your professional interests, prior coursework, and career goals.

Assignment Instructions:

  • Write an informal, 1-page (max) paper describing 2–3 project ideas you are considering for your Capstone.
  • For each idea, include:
    • A brief summary of the project concept.
    • Why this idea interests you.
    • How it might relate to the field of IT leadership or your current/future career.
    • Any early thoughts about scope or challenges.
  • This is not a formal proposal—no APA formatting or citations are required at this stage. However, your writing should still be clear, professional, and well-organized.
  • Submit your document as a Word or PDF file by the due date.

Sample Topics May Include:

  • Improving a cybersecurity policy within an organization
  • Evaluating the integration of AI tools in IT operations
  • Designing a cloud migration strategy for a small business
  • Developing a plan to implement an IT governance framework

Instructor Feedback:

Your instructor will review your ideas and provide guidance on which direction may be best suited for the capstone project. You’ll refine and develop one of these ideas into your full project proposal in the coming weeks.

Using AI:

You are free to use any AI tool to help you to brainstorm ideas for your project.

Note: This assignment is designated Level 2 - AI-Assisted Idea Generation and Structuring. AI can be used in the assignment for brainstorming, creating structures, and generating ideas for improving work. However, no AI content is allowed in the final submission.
