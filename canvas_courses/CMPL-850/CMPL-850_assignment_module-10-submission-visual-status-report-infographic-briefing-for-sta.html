In this assignment, you will present your project status update in a visually engaging and professionally synthesized format. Building on your earlier stakeholder communication experience, this assignment uses visual storytelling to deliver key project updates in a concise, at-a-glance format. This two-part assignment simulates a real-world progress briefing through an infographic and brief voiceover.

Assignment Components

Part 1: Project Status Infographic (1 Page)

Design a one-page infographic that communicates your current project status clearly and visually. Use tools like Canva, PowerPoint, Google Slides, Piktochart, or another platform of your choice. Your infographic must include the following components:

      • Stage of Work – Use a visual indicator such as a progress bar, timeline, or project phase graphic.
      • Tasks Completed – Represent completed tasks using icons, charts, or concise visuals.
      • Problems Encountered and Resolutions – Highlight any key challenges and how they were addressed using symbols or minimal text.
      • Next Steps – Show the upcoming tasks or milestones using arrows, checklists, or timeline markers.

Part 2: Audio Voiceover (1–2 Minutes)

Record a brief voiceover to walk stakeholders through your infographic. This should be a concise verbal explanation that adds context or emphasis to the information shown visually. The audio-only format simulates real-world asynchronous status briefings and should be delivered in a professional tone.
