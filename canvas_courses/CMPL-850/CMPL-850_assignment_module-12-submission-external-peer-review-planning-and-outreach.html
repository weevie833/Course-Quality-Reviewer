As part of this module’s focus on collaboration and feedback, you will conduct an external peer review to gather valuable insight into your Capstone project. The purpose of this activity is to seek feedback from someone with relevant professional or subject matter expertise outside of this course, someone who can offer informed, constructive perspectives aligned with your project’s topic and goals.

Assignment Instructions:

Project Summary: 

Begin by writing a clear and concise explanation of your Capstone project. Include: 

  • Project title 
  • The problem or opportunity being addressed 
  • Your proposed solution or approach 
  • Target audience or stakeholders 
  • Any tools, frameworks, or methods being used 
  • Expected outcomes or deliverables 

Peer Review Questions: 

Create four thoughtful and specific questions you would like your external reviewer to answer. These questions should prompt feedback that is meaningful and actionable. Consider asking about: 

      • Clarity and relevance of your problem statement 
      • Strength and feasibility of your proposed solution 
      • Overall structure or organization of your project 
      • Practicality, innovation, or real-world application of your ideas 

Select Your Reviewer: 

Identify and contact an external reviewer with professional or academic experience relevant to your capstone project. This could be: 

      • A colleague or supervisor 
      • A professional working in the same field or industry 
      • A mentor or academic with domain expertise 

Ensure the reviewer understands their role is to provide honest, constructive feedback based on your summary and questions. 

Submission Requirements: 

In this module, you will submit a single document that includes: 

      • Your project summary 
      • Your four peer review questions 
      • The name, title, and professional background of your external reviewer

Note: In Module 13, you will submit the completed peer review responses you receive. 

Submit your External Peer Review Planning document by Sunday at 11:59 PM ET. 

Note: This assignment is designated Level 1 - No AI. The assignment must be completed entirely without AI assistance. The assignment must be produced relying solely on conventional research and study methods. AI must not be used at any point during the production of the assignment.
