In this assignment, you will submit the completed version of the External Peer Review you initiated in Module 12. The purpose of this assignment is to gather and reflect on constructive feedback from an external reviewer with relevant knowledge or professional experience connected to your capstone project topic. 

Submission Requirements:

  • Capstone Project Overview 
  • Project Title 
  • Brief description of your capstone project, including its goals, scope, and intended impact. 
  • Peer Review Questions 
  • The four questions you crafted in Module 12 to guide your reviewer’s feedback. 
  • External Peer Responses 
  • The responses provided by your chosen external peer reviewer. 
  • Responses should appear directly below each question. 
  • Responses may be typed by the reviewer or transcribed by you (if the reviewer provided them verbally or in handwritten format). 

Submission Format:

  • File type: Microsoft Word (.docx) or PDF (.pdf) 
  • Submit your document via the LMS assignment portal in Module 13 
  • File name format: LastName_FirstName_ExternalPeerReview.docx 

Grading:

This assignment will be graded on a Pass/Fail basis. To receive a passing grade, all sections must be completed, and responses from an appropriate external reviewer must be included. 

Note: This assignment is designated Level 2 - AI-Assisted Idea Generation and Structuring. AI can be used in the assignment for brainstorming, creating structures, and generating ideas for improving work. However, no AI content is allowed in the final submission.
