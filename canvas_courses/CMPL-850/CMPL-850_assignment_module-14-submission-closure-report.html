The Closure Report marks the formal conclusion of your Capstone project. In this assignment, you will summarize the final testing and deployment outcomes and reflect on the overall experience through a post-mortem review. The goal is to document how your project came to completion, what you learned, and what insights you can carry into future work as a professional in the field of Information Technology Management.

Instructions

Prepare a written Closure Report that includes the following two sections:

Final Testing and Deployment Summary

Describe how the final stages of testing were conducted, any issues that arose, and how they were resolved. Summarize the deployment process, including who was involved, how the deployment was executed, and whether it met expectations.

Include the following details where applicable:

      • Testing methods and final outcomes
      • User acceptance feedback (if applicable)
      • Technical issues resolved during deployment
      • Confirmation of operational readiness

Post-Mortem and Lessons Learned

Conduct a reflective analysis of the project’s overall execution. Highlight what worked well, what could have been improved, and any key lessons you’ve learned about leadership, adaptability, collaboration, or technical execution.

Consider the following questions to guide your reflection:

      • What were the project’s greatest successes?
      • What were the most significant challenges or surprises?
      • What would you do differently in future projects?
      • How did this project influence your leadership or management style?
