Your project proposal should be 3–4 pages, double-spaced, and written in APA 7 format. It must include:

  • Project Title and Executive Summary – A brief, high-level summary of the proposed initiative and its value.
  • Project Scope, Objectives, and Deliverables – Define what the project will accomplish and the expected outputs.
  • Alignment with Program Learning Outcomes – Identify which PLOs are addressed and how they will be demonstrated.
  • Rationale and Relevance – Support your project with at least three peer-reviewed or authoritative industry sources that justify its need, feasibility, or significance.
  • Preliminary Timeline and Milestones – Outline the major phases and estimated completion points for each.
  • Anticipated Challenges or Risks – Briefly identify any concerns related to feasibility, resources, or scope.
  • Reference Page – Cite all sources using APA 7th edition formatting.

This proposal serves as the roadmap for your Capstone projct. Feedback from your instructor will help refine your direction before the project is formally approved and launched in subsequent modules.

Note: This assignment is designated Level 1 - No AI. The assignment must be completed entirely without AI assistance. The assignment must be produced relying solely on conventional research and study methods. AI must not be used at any point during the production of the assignment.
