Overview

In this assignment, you will use an AI chatbot to simulate a stakeholder role-play session focused on your draft project charter. You will configure the AI to adopt the persona of an organizational executive and engage in a conversation where the AI only asks clarifying or critical questions about your charter. This activity is designed to help you anticipate stakeholder feedback, identify project gaps, and improve your ability to defend your project decisions.

Instructions

  • Use an AI chatbot such as ChatGPT or another approved tool to simulate an executive stakeholder.
  • Develop a prompt that instructs the AI to adopt the persona of a specific executive relevant to your project (e.g., CIO, Operations Director, Chief Security Officer).
  • Clearly instruct the AI only to ask clarifying questions about your project charter, not to complete any portion of it, or provide solutions.
  • Share your draft charter content or a summarized version with the AI and initiate the role play.
  • Allow the AI to respond with stakeholder-style questions about your project. Respond briefly if needed to keep the dialogue flowing.
  • Copy and paste the entire conversation into a Word or PDF document.
  • Include a short summary (1–2 paragraphs) at the top of your document reflecting on the most valuable questions or insights you gained from the AI interaction.

Submission Guidelines

  • Submit your final document as a Word or PDF file.
  • The document should include your summary at the top, followed by the full AI conversation transcript.
  • Filename: YourLastName_AI_Stakeholder_Feedback

Sample Prompt

Below is a sample prompt with annotation that explains each component of the prompt. Develop your own prompt that is specific to your project.

You are an Operations Director at a medium-sized company that manufactures equipment for craft beer production. [This tells AI to assume a specific persona and position as an expert]. You are a stakeholder in a charter for a new IT project that I am proposing. I am the IT Manager and I am proposing a charter for a new data server implementation [This explains the situation and the context of your engagement]. Ask me questions about it from the standpoint of a stakeholder [This explains what you want AI to do]. Include clarifying questions about any aspect of the project charter proposal. [This explains how you want AI to engage with you]. Do not recommend solutions and do not complete any portion of the charter [This tells AI what not to do]. Wait for me to upload my charter before engaging with me in the role play [This tells AI to behave according to the sequence of action you want. Otherwise, it might start a dialog before you have uploaded your Charter].

Note: This assignment is designated Level 5 - Full AI. AI can be used as a collaborative assistant to meet the assignment requirement, with no restrictions. AI can be used throughout the assignment production without specifying which content is AI-generated.
