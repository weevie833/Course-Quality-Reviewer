Overview - In this assignment, you will submit the first draft of your Capstone Project Charter. This document is a key component of your project planning process and will help solidify the foundation for your work moving forward. The charter should clearly articulate the purpose, scope, and structure of your project and demonstrate alignment with both your professional goals and the program learning outcomes.

You will receive feedback from your instructor on this draft, which will help guide your revisions and preparation for the final version due in Module 4.

Charter Requirements

Your project charter should include the following four elements:

Needs Assessment - Describe the organizational or professional problem, gap, or opportunity your project addresses. Explain why this need is significant and how your project will respond to it.

Business Case - Justify the value of your project. Describe the anticipated benefits (e.g., strategic alignment, process improvement, cost savings, innovation, risk reduction).

Identify Stakeholders - List the key stakeholders (real or hypothetical) who would have an interest in, or be impacted by, the project. Include internal and external parties where appropriate (e.g., clients, IT teams, executives, vendors).

Scope, Goals, Constraints, and Risks - Clearly define what is and is not included in your project. State your primary goals and deliverables. Identify any known limitations (budget, time, technology) and potential risks.
