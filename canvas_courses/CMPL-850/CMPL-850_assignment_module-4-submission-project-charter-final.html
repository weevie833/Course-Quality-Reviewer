What to Include in the Final Project Charter

Refinement of Existing Sections

  • Clarify and strengthen the needs assessment, business case, stakeholder list, and scope based on instructor feedback.

Expanded Stakeholder Analysis

  • Include a brief description of each stakeholder’s role, expectations, and potential influence on the project.

Timeline and Milestones

  • Add a high-level timeline or milestone table that outlines major project phases and estimated completion points.

Project Governance or Communication Plan

  • Describe how communication with stakeholders will be managed throughout the project.
  • Include reporting frequency or channels if applicable.

Risk Mitigation Strategies

  • List your key project risks and describe how each will be addressed or minimized. Show evidence of planning and foresight.

Assumptions and Constraints

  • Provide a clear list of assumptions your plan depends on and outline project constraints (e.g., time, resources, budget, or organizational policies).

Formatting and Professionalism

  • Ensure the document is well-organized, free of errors, and clearly structured using headings.
