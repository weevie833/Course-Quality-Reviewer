In this assignment, you will begin developing the planning roadmap for your capstone project. This roadmap should demonstrate your ability to apply IT management best practices in preparation for implementation. Use this opportunity to lay out the technical and functional foundation for your project using realistic assumptions and professional-level planning tools.

This draft will be submitted for review, and your instructor will provide feedback to support your final submission next week.

Assignment Requirements

Your roadmap document should include the following components:

  • Define Functional and Technical Requirements – Clearly outline what your project must accomplish functionally (what it will do) and technically (how it will work).
  • Work Breakdown Structure (WBS) – Break your project into manageable components, tasks, and sub-tasks. A visual WBS or hierarchical list is acceptable.
  • Estimate Resources, Time, and Budget – Provide estimated staffing needs, project duration, and anticipated costs, even if hypothetical.
  • Project Schedule – Outline a high-level project timeline or Gantt chart showing major milestones and deliverables.
  • Roles and Responsibilities – Define the roles involved in the project and assign responsibilities (e.g., project manager, analyst, developer).
  • Develop Risk Management, Communication, and Change Management Plans – Briefly describe how your project will manage risk, stakeholder communication, and change requests.
  • Compliance Check – Confirm how your project aligns with relevant standards such as IT Governance frameworks, cybersecurity requirements, or legal compliance (e.g., NIST, GDPR, HIPAA, etc.).

Submission Guidelines

  • Format: 3–5 pages, double-spaced, professional tone.
  • Include tables, visuals, or charts where helpful (WBS, timeline, roles grid).
  • Cite any external frameworks or references using APA 7th edition format.
  • Submit as a Word or PDF file.
