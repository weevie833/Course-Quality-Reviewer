In this assignment, you will investigate how organizations in your industry, or a related industry, use artificial intelligence (AI) to support project management functions such as scheduling, resource optimization, risk mitigation, or communication. This activity encourages critical thinking about AI’s strategic role in planning.

Instructions

  1. Choose an Industry or Sector
    • Select an industry that aligns with your Capstone focus (e.g., healthcare IT, cybersecurity, business operations, education technology, etc.).
  1. Use AI as a Research Assistant
    • Using an AI tool (e.g., ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot), ask the AI to help you identify:
      • At least one real-world example of how AI is used in project planning or management in that industry.
      • Key benefits and challenges associated with that implementation.
      • Trends or tools (e.g., predictive analytics, natural language processing, automation platforms) that are shaping the future of project planning.
  1. Write a 1–2 Page Brief
    • Summarize your findings in a professional write-up that includes:
      • A short description of the industry example(s)
      • A discussion of how AI contributed to planning or execution
      • A critical reflection: Would this use of AI translate well to your field or future career? Why or why not?
  1. Include a Screenshot or Transcript of Your AI Interaction
    • Provide evidence of your AI conversation as an appendix (just one page is fine).

Learning Goals

  • Understand real-world applications of AI in project settings
  • Analyze AI’s strengths and limitations from a strategic standpoint
  • Practice ethical, transparent use of AI as a research support tool
Note: This assignment is designated Level 1 - No AI. The assignment must be completed entirely without AI assistance. The assignment must be produced relying solely on conventional research and study methods. AI must not be used at any point during the production of the assignment.
