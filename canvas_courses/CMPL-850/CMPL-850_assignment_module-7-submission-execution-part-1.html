In this assignment, you will submit a draft of your project execution plan. This plan bridges your roadmap with the implementation phase and outlines how you intend to carry out your project successfully. Your draft should demonstrate how key aspects of project execution will be addressed, including development methodology, communication, quality control, and performance measurement.

Instructions

Your submission should address the following six criteria. Each section should include clear, detailed descriptions of how you will manage these components in your Capstone project:

  1. Proposed Development Lifecycle – Describe the development methodology you plan to follow (e.g., Agile, Waterfall, Hybrid, DevOps) and explain why it is appropriate for your project.
  2. Coordination with Partners – Identify any internal or external partners involved in your project and describe how coordination, collaboration, and communication with these partners will be managed.
  3. Quality Assurance and Change Control – Outline your proposed methods for ensuring quality throughout the project. Include how you will handle change control, testing, validation, and documentation.
  4. Communication Methods – Explain how you will maintain regular, effective communication with your project stakeholders. Include the format, frequency, and tools (e.g., meetings, reports, email updates).
  5. Monitoring Progress, Budget, Scope, and Change Requests – Describe how you will track project milestones, manage scope creep, monitor the budget, and document or respond to change requests.
  6. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) – Select meaningful KPIs to assess your project's performance. Justify your choices and explain how you will measure and report on them.

Submission Guidelines

  • Length: 3–5 pages, double-spaced
  • Format: Professional tone and structure
  • References: Cite any frameworks, models, or sources used (APA 7th edition)
  • File Type: Word or PDF
