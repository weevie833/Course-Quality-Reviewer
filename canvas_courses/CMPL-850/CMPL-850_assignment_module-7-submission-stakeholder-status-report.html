In this assignment, you will prepare a short presentation that simulates a professional project status update to your stakeholders. The presentation should provide a snapshot of your project’s progress, highlight completed work, address any issues encountered, and outline next steps.

Instructions

Create a 5–7 minute PowerPoint presentation with voiceover narration. Your presentation should be titled 'Stakeholder Debrief on Project Status' and should clearly and concisely address the following four areas:

  1. Stage of Work – Briefly describe where you are in the project execution process. Include your development phase and current progress toward roadmap goals.
  2. Tasks Completed – Provide an overview of the major tasks or deliverables you have completed since the planning phase.
  3. Problems Encountered, Assessment, and Proposed Resolutions – Discuss any issues or roadblocks that have impacted your progress. Share how you assessed the problem and what solutions or adjustments you have proposed or implemented.
  4. Action Items for the Next Stage – Identify what comes next. Outline the upcoming deliverables, responsibilities, or milestones for your next phase of work.

Submission Guidelines

  • Presentation Length: 5–7 minutes
  • Format: PowerPoint slideshow with voiceover (narrated slides) and webcam video.
  • Tone: Professional and stakeholder-focused
  • File Type: Upload a video recording.
