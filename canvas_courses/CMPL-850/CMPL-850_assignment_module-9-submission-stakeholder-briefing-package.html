This assignment is designed to simulate a real-world stakeholder status update. Instead of a traditional slide-based presentation, you will communicate your project's current progress using a two-part approach: a concise written memo and a short executive video clip. This format helps build your professional communication skills in both written and verbal formats while maintaining a focus on real-time project execution.

Assignment Components

Part 1: Stakeholder Status Memo (Written – 1 page)

Draft a one-page professional memo addressed to a key stakeholder (e.g., CIO, Project Sponsor, Department Head). The memo should clearly and concisely summarize the current status of your project, using the four categories below:

      • Stage of Work – Briefly describe the current phase of execution.
      • Tasks Completed – List the major milestones or deliverables completed since the last report.
      • Problems Encountered and Resolutions Proposed – Summarize one or two key challenges, your assessment, and any action taken or proposed.
      • Action Items for the Next Stage – Outline the key tasks and responsibilities for the next phase of your project.

Part 2: Executive Video Clip (2–3 minutes)

Record a short, professional video summarizing the content of your memo as if presenting to stakeholders in a virtual setting. Use the Kaltura resources for recording a webcam video of yourself. Slides are not required. Focus on clarity, tone, and professionalism in your delivery.

Submission Guidelines

  • Video Length: 2–3 minutes
