To kick off our learning community and help us get to know one another, please introduce yourself in the course discussion forum.

In your post, please include:

  1. Your name and current location.
  2. Your professional background and current role (if applicable).
  3. What area of IT leadership or management are you most passionate about?
  4. An initial idea or interest area you’re considering for your capstone project.
  5. One fun or surprising fact about yourself outside of work or school.

Feel free to respond to at least one classmate to help build connection and community in our virtual space. Even though we are online and asynchronous, collaboration and support are key to a strong capstone experience!
