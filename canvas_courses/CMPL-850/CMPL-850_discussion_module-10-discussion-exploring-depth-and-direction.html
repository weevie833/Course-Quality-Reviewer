Now that your project is well into the execution phase, it’s a good time to explore some of the deeper layers of your work. This discussion invites you to reflect on the direction your project is taking and examine the strategic, technical, or organizational factors that may be influencing its development. Choose one of the following prompts to explore a unique aspect of your project, and engage with your peers to share perspectives and learning across different domains.

Discussion Prompt

Choose one of the following to respond to in your initial post. Copy/Paste your selected option into your post so that your classmates know which option you selected.

Option 1 – Design Decisions: What was one key design or planning decision you made that significantly shaped the direction of your project? What alternatives did you consider, and why did you choose the path you did?

Option 2 – Organizational Fit: How does your project reflect or challenge the culture, workflows, or structure of the organization or user base it was designed for? What adjustments have you made to ensure alignment or successful implementation?

Option 3 – Technology in Focus: What role does a specific technology, tool, or platform play in your project? Discuss how its capabilities (or limitations) have affected your progress, and whether it’s meeting your expectations so far.

Peer Response Requirement

After posting your initial response, reply to at least one classmate’s post. Offer a thoughtful question, a related experience, or feedback on how their insight might apply to other contexts. Aim to deepen the conversation and share useful strategies or perspectives.
