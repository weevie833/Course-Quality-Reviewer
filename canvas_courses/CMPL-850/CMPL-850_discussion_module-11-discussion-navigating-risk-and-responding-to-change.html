As your project progresses, the ability to respond to uncertainty becomes just as important as planning. This discussion invites you to reflect on how you’ve handled risk and change so far, and how your leadership and problem-solving skills are evolving in real time. Choose one of the prompts below that best aligns with your experience, and share your insight with peers navigating similar challenges.

Discussion Prompt

Choose one of the following to respond to in your initial post. Copy/Paste your selected option into your post so that your classmates know which option you selected.

Option 1 – Unexpected Challenge - Describe a recent obstacle or risk that was not part of your original planning. How did you approach it, and what was the outcome? What would you do differently if faced with it again?

Option 2 – Leading Through Change - Think about a moment during your project where change was necessary. How did you lead yourself or others through that shift, and what leadership qualities did you rely on most?

Option 3 – Evolving Risk Strategy - How has your approach to risk evolved since the start of your project? Have you become more proactive, reactive, flexible, or cautious? Provide a specific example that illustrates your shift.

Peer Response Requirement

After posting your initial response, reply to at least one classmate’s post. Offer a constructive question, affirmation, or example from your own experience that builds on their insight. The goal is to share real strategies and strengthen each other’s adaptability.
