In this module, you will complete a structured self-review using the guiding questions below and respond to instructor-provided feedback.

This activity is designed to give you the opportunity to present a key component of your project, receive constructive feedback, and engage in a shared learning process. The peer review format simulates professional team collaboration, strengthens communication skills, and helps refine your project before final submission.

Part 1: Peer Review Presentation or Demo (3–5 Minutes)

Record a 3–5 minute video presentation or demo of a major component of your Capstone project. You may focus on a feature, system design, data analysis, user interface, implementation method, or any other meaningful piece of your project. Your goal is to clearly communicate what you've developed so far and highlight how it supports your overall project goals.

Your presentation or demo should include:

      • A brief overview of your project and where this component fits in
      • The problem this feature/component addresses
      • A summary or walkthrough of how it works
      • Any challenges faced or decisions made in its development
      • The kind of feedback you are seeking

Submission Guidelines

  • Presentation/Demo: 3–5 minute video.
  • Submit video via Kaltura.
  • Provide feedback to at least two peers in the discussion thread.
Note: This assignment is designated Level 1 - No AI. The assignment must be completed entirely without AI assistance. The assignment must be produced relying solely on conventional research and study methods. AI must not be used at any point during the production of the assignment.
