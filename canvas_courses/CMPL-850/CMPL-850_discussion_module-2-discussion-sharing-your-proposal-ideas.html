As you begin developing your formal project proposal, use this forum to share your initial thoughts about your proposed capstone project. This is a space to reflect on where you are in the planning process, get feedback from your peers, and raise any questions or concerns you might have before submitting your draft.

In your post, please address the following:

  • A brief summary of the project idea you're currently leaning toward.
  • Why is this project important or meaningful to you professionally?
  • Any areas where you feel unsure or would like feedback (e.g., scope, alignment with outcomes, feasibility, research support).
  • One specific challenge you anticipate and how you might address it.

Reply to at least one classmate’s post with encouragement, ideas, or questions. Collaboration and peer insight are valuable parts of the capstone journey—even in an asynchronous environment!
