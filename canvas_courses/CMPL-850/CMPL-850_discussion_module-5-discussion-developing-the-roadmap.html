In this module, you will focus on developing the roadmap for your capstone project, including planning your timeline, identifying key resources, and aligning your project components with research insights. To guide your thinking and encourage meaningful dialogue with peers, please choose one of the three discussion prompts below. Your response should demonstrate critical thinking, application of course concepts, and a connection to your individual project work.

Instructions

Choose one of the following three prompts to respond to in your initial post. Your response should be thoughtful and detailed and include references to your capstone project experience. Copy/Paste your selected option in your post so that your classmates know which option you selected.

Option 1: Reflecting on the Planning Process

As you build your roadmap, what has been the most challenging part of estimating your project's timeline, resources, or budget? How are you approaching this uncertainty, and what tools or strategies have helped you most in the planning process?

Option 2: Learning from Examples

After reviewing professional or academic examples of roadmap or implementation plans, what features stood out to you as especially effective or inspiring? How are you incorporating what you've learned into your own capstone roadmap?

Option 3: Connecting Research to Action

How is the research you’ve conducted influencing your roadmap decisions (e.g., milestones, risk management, communication strategies)? Share one or two ways your findings have directly shaped your project planning.

Peer Response Requirement

After submitting your initial post, read your classmates’ responses to the other prompts. Choose at least one peer post to respond to with a constructive comment or question. Your response should aim to deepen the conversation, offer a helpful perspective, or share relevant experiences from your own project development process.
