As your project continues to take shape, this is a valuable time to step back and reflect on how your work connects to broader organizational or industry goals. Whether your focus is operational efficiency, technological innovation, or user-centered design, every project is driven by a deeper purpose. This forum is an opportunity to articulate that purpose and gather feedback from peers who may offer new insights or perspectives.

Discussion Prompt

Choose one of the following to respond to in your initial post. Copy/Paste your selected option into your post so that your classmates know which option you selected.

Option 1 – Strategic Relevance: What organizational or industry problem is your project trying to solve? How does your proposed solution align with larger strategic goals (e.g., growth, innovation, sustainability, compliance)?

Option 2 – Innovation in Action: What makes your project or approach innovative? This could relate to tools, processes, user experience, or the way you’ve framed the problem. How are you pushing beyond “standard solutions”?

Option 3 – Stakeholder Perspective: If your key stakeholder could provide you with one piece of advice or feedback at this point in the project, what do you think it would be—and how would you respond to it?

Peer Response Requirement

Respond to at least one classmate’s post by offering an idea, question, or connection to your own project. Aim to deepen the conversation or highlight something your peer may not have considered.
