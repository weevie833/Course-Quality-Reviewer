Overview

As your capstone project continues through the execution phase, Module 10 provides an opportunity to step back, synthesize progress, and reflect on the evolving direction of your work. This module emphasizes visual communication and the ability to convey complex updates clearly and concisely to stakeholders.

You will complete a two-part assignment that includes a one-page infographic summarizing your current project status and a short audio voiceover that provides context and insight. This format mirrors real-world reporting environments where leaders must present timely updates in engaging and accessible ways.

The discussion forum invites you to explore how your project has taken shape—from design decisions and organizational alignment to the role of specific technologies. As each project develops its own momentum, this is an ideal time to reflect on the deeper choices influencing your outcomes.

By the end of this module, you’ll have advanced your project’s visibility, refined your professional communication style, and deepened your awareness of your project’s strategic, technical, or organizational impact.
