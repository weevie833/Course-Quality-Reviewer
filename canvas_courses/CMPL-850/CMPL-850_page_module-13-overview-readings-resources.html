Overview

As your capstone project approaches completion, the focus of this module shifts to producing clear, professional documentation that supports both technical deployment and end-user success. Documentation is more than a final deliverable—it’s a vital tool for ensuring that your solution can be implemented, supported, and sustained beyond the scope of your individual work.

You will create two sets of documentation: one for the deployment and technical setup of your project, and another for the intended end users. These materials should demonstrate your ability to translate complex systems into clear, accessible instructions for both technical and non-technical audiences.

In the discussion forum, you’ll reflect on your documentation approach, the challenges of communicating to diverse audiences, and how well your materials convey your leadership and technical knowledge. By the end of this module, you will have created a foundation of support materials that increase the practical value and longevity of your capstone project.
