Overview

As you enter the final stages of your capstone experience, Module 14 focuses on closing your project with professionalism, insight, and clarity. Closure is not just about wrapping up tasks—it’s about documenting your final work, reflecting on your journey, and identifying the lessons that will guide your future as a leader in Information Technology Management.

This module includes two major assignments: the Closure Report, which summarizes your final testing, deployment, and lessons learned; and Reflection #2, where you revisit the program’s learning outcomes and reflect on your professional growth since Module 8. In this reflection, you’ll select two different learning outcomes than before and assess how your recent work demonstrates leadership, adaptability, and technical expertise.

By the end of this module, you will have formalized the results of your project, completed a comprehensive self-assessment, and prepared yourself to move into the final phase of presentation and portfolio submission with confidence.
