Overview

Module 15 marks a pivotal point in your capstone experience, the submission of your final project portfolio. This portfolio represents the culmination of months of planning, execution, documentation, collaboration, and reflection. It showcases your ability to lead a complex IT initiative from concept to completion and demonstrates your mastery of the program’s learning outcomes.

Throughout this module, you’ll organize and submit your final capstone portfolio, drawing on materials and deliverables created throughout each phase of the course. The portfolio should be professionally presented and accessible, providing a clear narrative of your work and the impact it aims to achieve.

In addition to submitting your portfolio, you’ll participate in a discussion forum that invites you to reflect on your growth, celebrate your achievements, and look ahead to your future goals. This is a moment to pause and take pride in how far you’ve come, both as a student and as a professional.

By the end of this module, your capstone portfolio will be ready for final review, setting the stage for your official presentation and program completion in Module 16.
