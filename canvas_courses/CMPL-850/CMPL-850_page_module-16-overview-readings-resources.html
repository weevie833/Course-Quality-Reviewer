Overview

Module 16 marks the official conclusion of your graduate Capstone experience. In this final stage, you will deliver a polished, professional presentation that showcases the full scope of your project—from initial concept through planning, execution, adaptation, and reflection. This presentation serves as both a summary of your work and a demonstration of your leadership, technical expertise, and alignment with the program’s learning outcomes.

Your presentation should be structured, visually engaging, and delivered with clarity and professionalism. It is your opportunity to communicate the significance of your work to an academic and professional audience and to reflect on the impact your project could have within your field or organization.

By the end of this module, you will have completed all major Capstone requirements and presented a comprehensive narrative of your growth as an Information Technology Management leader.
