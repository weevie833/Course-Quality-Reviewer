Overview

You will begin transforming your initial project ideas into a focused, formal capstone project proposal. This proposal will be the foundation for your work throughout the course and must clearly define the project’s purpose, scope, deliverables, and alignment with one or more Program Learning Outcomes (PLOs). You'll be guided through best practices for academic and professional proposal writing, supported by templates and research resources.

The module emphasizes the importance of setting realistic goals, understanding potential risks and assumptions, and ensuring that your project has practical relevance to IT leadership. You'll also have the opportunity to reflect on your draft proposal in a peer discussion and receive early feedback from your instructor to help refine and finalize your direction.
