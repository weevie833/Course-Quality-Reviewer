Overview

In this module, you will finalize and submit your Project Charter, incorporating feedback from previous weeks and refining the project's scope, objectives, stakeholders, and deliverables. A complete and well-structured charter sets the foundation for a successful project lifecycle.

Readings

Research and review several completed project charters online, then compare them to your own. Use what you learn to enhance the quality of your charter. Pay particular attention to the sections of the sample charters that appear to have contributed most to their effectiveness and success.
