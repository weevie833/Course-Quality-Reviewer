Overview

In this module, you will begin translating your project vision into a concrete plan by completing the Roadmap Development – Research Draft assignment. This assignment requires you to define key functional and technical requirements, develop a Work Breakdown Structure (WBS), estimate time and resources, and identify key roles and responsibilities. In addition, you will begin drafting essential project management plans, including risk, communication, and change management strategies, while considering compliance with relevant IT governance or cybersecurity standards.

By the end of this module, you should be well-positioned to guide your project from planning to execution with a clear roadmap in place.

Readings

Definition: Roadmap Development Research, in the context of a capstone project, is the process of investigating and defining the primary elements required to execute the project successfully. It bridges the gap between the conceptual planning in the Project Charter and the detailed execution of the capstone.

This week, review 2–3 professional or academic sources on Roadmap Development - Research in the context of a capstone project to guide the development of your own project. Focus your research on the structure, purpose, and essential components of effective roadmap development. Be prepared to reference what you've learned when drafting your roadmap development submission.
