Overview

In this module, you will finalize and submit your Roadmap Development – Research Assignment, incorporating feedback from your instructor to improve the clarity, structure, and feasibility of your project plan. This final version should reflect thoughtful revisions and demonstrate your ability to align project components with functional goals, resource estimates, stakeholder roles, and risk management strategies.

In addition, you will complete the AI Industry Use Case Exploration Assignment, where you will investigate how artificial intelligence is being used in real-world project planning across industries. This assignment encourages you to think critically about AI’s role in modern project management and how emerging tools may influence your field or future work.

By the end of this module, you will have completed two key deliverables that demonstrate both practical planning skills and your ability to assess the impact of emerging technologies on the project landscape.
