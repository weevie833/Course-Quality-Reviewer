Overview

In this module, you will shift your focus from project planning to the early stages of execution. This phase marks the transition from strategy to action, where planning is put into motion and progress begins to take shape. You will have the opportunity to draft your initial execution plan and practice communicating status updates to stakeholders in a professional setting.

By the end of this module, you will have taken meaningful steps toward project execution and developed communication skills essential for keeping stakeholders informed and engaged.

Readings

This week's recommended reading should focus on the following online research.

  1. Project execution and delivery methods
  2. Communication with stakeholders
  3. Quality assurance and change control
  4. KPI selection and monitoring
  5. Presentation and reporting best practices
