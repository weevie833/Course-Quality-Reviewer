Overview

In this final module of the execution phase, you will refine and submit the final version of your Execution Plan. Building on the draft you submitted in Module 7, you will apply instructor feedback to strengthen your approach to development methodology, stakeholder coordination, quality assurance, communication, and performance tracking. Your final execution plan should demonstrate a clear, actionable strategy for bringing your project to completion.

In addition to your written submission, you will participate in a reflective discussion forum that invites you to revisit the Program Learning Outcomes (PLOs). This discussion will allow you to assess how your capstone project reflects your growth as a leader in Information Technology Management and how it aligns with the strategic and ethical principles of the program.

By the end of this module, you will have completed a major deliverable and demonstrated your ability to connect your academic experience with real-world application and leadership in IT project execution.
