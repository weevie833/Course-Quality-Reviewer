Overview

In this module, you will continue executing your capstone project and begin formally communicating progress to stakeholders. The focus of Module 9 is on professional project reporting and critical reflection on your project's strategic value.

You will complete a two-part assignment that simulates a real-world stakeholder update: a one-page status memo and a brief executive video clip. This format reinforces concise written and verbal communication skills while helping you evaluate your current stage of work, assess recent challenges, and outline next steps.

Additionally, the discussion forum invites you to reflect on the broader strategic impact of your project, from innovation and stakeholder engagement to how your work aligns with real organizational or industry needs.

By the end of this module, you should be able to clearly articulate your project’s current status, assess its strategic purpose, and plan forward with confidence and clarity.
