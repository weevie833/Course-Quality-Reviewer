Application-Based Communication Assignment

This two-part assignment includes a written component and an executive summary presentation.

PART ONE: Create a crisis communication plan for your organization. Specify things that may arise, take into consideration resources you have or may need to consider obtaining. Consider how you will mentor and motivate others to act in a time of change. Length should be 4 to 5 pages in APA format. Use at least 3 references. Avoid lengthy lists of items – Write in paragraph format, not numbered or bulleted lists. Discuss how you will negotiate your oorganizations'hierarchical structure. How you will share your vision with staff and negotiate any conflict?

PART TWO: Executive Summary Presentation. Develop an executive summary presentation of your assignment. The summary should integrate one form of media (podcast, PowerPoint or Prezi, link to a website, etc.), and present a creative and compelling summary of the crisis communication plan.

The entire assignment, which includes the written component and executive summary presentation, must address each of the following:
Choose one of the following:

  1. Create or design something with multiple aspects using concepts from the text that has direct bearing on your or another student’s organization. Examples include: design a communication plan for an international organization, a social media plan, a crisis management plan, etc.
  2. Use a case study from an organizational challenge within any organization (could be one of yours, one that you have heard of in the news, or any organization) to work together on possible solutions referencing details from the text. Be sure to clearly articulate what the problem is and what your solution(s) is.
  3. Develop a response to a situation within your or another student’s organization that requires a variety of communication skills. Be specific in your response.

Make sure to review the Project Grading Rubric to see how you will be assessed.
