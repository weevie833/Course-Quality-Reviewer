Prepare a PowerPoint Presentation (Slides and Video) that reflects the contents of your research paper.

Slide requirements:

  • 20 content slide minimum with notes required (title and reference slides do not count as content slides)
  • Use only bullet points with key words/phrases on the slides and break it up with images to keep the slides visually appealing. Save full sentences for the notes section.
  • Be sure to list all sources of information at the end of the presentation. 

Video requirements:

  • Create a short video (8-10 minutes) discussing your PowerPoint. DO NOT READ FROM THE SLIDES. Notes can act like an outline, or even a script!
  • Information on how to Create and Share a Video and Presentation Tips is available in Course Resources.
  • Make sure to review the Online Oral (Recorded) Presentation Rubric located in Course Resources.

Due by Friday at midnight.
