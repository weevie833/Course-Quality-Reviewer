Think about your organization or one that you admire. Keeping our resources in mind, describe a digital strategy you recommend the organization begin to use or enhance its use. Include a rationale for why (remember to practice with in-text citations per APA formatting).

Develop your rationale using a peer-reviewed journal article in the college's library. Your search terms may be "successful digital communication strategies in a nonprofit organization." Remember to filter for peer-reviewed articles.

One aspect of your rationale is to describe the research methods used in the article you've identified and how the data was collected. (Research methods play an essential role in your capstone paper at the end of the program. See links in this module and the course resources for guidance.)

In your responses to others, share one additional digital strategy that might be considered and why.

Initial post is due by Thursday midnight, and replies to others by Sunday midnight. Please add a question to encourage a more interactive discussion experience when writing replies to others. Make sure to review the Discussion Rubric (located in Course Resources).
