After reading Edgar Schein's article on Organizational Culture, reflect on your roles and responsibilities within your organization.

  • If you could describe your corporate culture in three words, what would you say?
  • If you were going to give public tours of this company, what stops would the guide make?
  • If the local media outlet ran a story about your company's culture, what would be impossible not to include?
  • What's the best part about working in this environment?
  • What are the most common complaints employees make about your company culture?
