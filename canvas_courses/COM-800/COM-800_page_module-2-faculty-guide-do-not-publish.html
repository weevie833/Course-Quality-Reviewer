In Module 2, students will explore the relationship between organizational communication, leadership character, and communication strategies. This module includes a discussion forum focused on digital communication strategies, a paper that asks students to reflect on their core leadership competencies and areas for improvement, and an annotated bibliography assignment related to their ongoing research paper. These activities are designed to enhance students’ understanding of leadership communication and their ability to apply communication strategies effectively in organizational contexts.

Module Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will:

  1. Analyze and evaluate effective digital communication strategies used in organizations.
  2. Reflect on their core leadership competencies and identify areas for growth with leadership communication.
  3. Develop an annotated bibliography that supports their research for the final paper, critically evaluating sources relevant to their research topic.
  4. Apply communication theories and strategies to improve leadership effectiveness in various organizational settings.

  • Instructor Role:

    • Guide students to locate credible, peer-reviewed articles focusing on organizations' digital communication strategies.
    • Please encourage students to think critically about the effectiveness of digital strategies, including how they relate to leadership, transparency, and engagement.
    • Monitor the discussion for insightful engagement and ensure students apply leadership communication principles to their analysis.
    • Encourage students to reflect deeply on their leadership experiences and communication strengths—guide identifying realistic areas for improvement.
    • Offer feedback on how students can develop an actionable plan to enhance their communication competencies, emphasizing short-term and long-term goals.
    • Provide examples or resources that highlight core leadership communication skills and strategies for improvement.
    • Guide students in locating scholarly sources and evaluating their relevance and credibility. Please encourage students to select sources that directly contribute to their research topic and support their analysis.
    • Offer feedback on the quality of students’ summaries and evaluations, ensuring they engage critically with the literature.
    • Remind students that the annotated bibliography will form the foundation of their final research paper, so choosing high-quality, relevant sources is essential.

Conclusion

In this module, students will deepen their understanding of organizational communication and leadership character by exploring digital communication strategies, reflecting on their leadership competencies, and organizing research for their final paper. The discussion forum will encourage students to engage critically with digital communication strategies. At the same time, the leadership competencies paper will allow them to reflect on their communication skills and areas for growth. The annotated bibliography will provide a foundation for their research, helping them evaluate sources and prepare for the final paper. These activities will help students become more effective and reflective leaders in their communication practices.
