Overview

“Ninety percent of leadership is the ability to communicate something people want.” - Dianne Feinstein

Organizations use a variety of communication methods to attract and retain employees and stakeholders, develop employee knowledge and behaviors, and create systems of control. Policies and procedures, branding efforts, and digital communication strategies are just a few of the ways that organizations attempt to shape their culture.

In this module our resources will explore these topics, and our forums and assignments are geared to help us think about how communication strategies address organizational concerns. We’ll also explore case studies regarding digital strategies that seek to address specific organizational needs.

Our course materials, along with additional relevant resources that I share along the way, may be helpful to your paper topic and the annotated bibliography. You’ll be asked to submit a draft of the annotated bibliography at the end of this module, and you are strongly encouraged to keep working on this resource as a type of living document that might be helpful for your capstone project.

Remember: communication is key, so please don’t hesitate to ask any questions you might have about the class or assignments. Here’s to another great week together!
