In Module 3, students will explore the role of emotions in team dynamics and learn strategies for effective conflict management in leadership contexts. This module includes a discussion forum on workplace conflict, a conflict management style survey, and an infographic assignment on the relationship between mindfulness and leadership. These activities will help students better understand how emotions influence teams, develop conflict-resolution skills, and reflect on the importance of mindfulness in leadership.

Module Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will:

  1. Understand the role of emotions in team dynamics and how leaders can manage conflict effectively.
  2. Analyze their conflict management style through a survey and reflect on how it impacts their leadership approach.
  3. Create an infographic that illustrates the connection between mindfulness and leadership, highlighting the benefits of mindful leadership in conflict management and emotional regulation.
  4. Develop practical strategies for managing team emotions and conflicts, applying communication theories to real-world leadership scenarios.

Instructor Role:

  • Facilitate the discussion by guiding students to reflect on the emotional components of conflict in teams and workplaces.
  • Encourage students to apply conflict management theories and leadership strategies to the scenarios they describe.
  • Monitor the discussion for thoughtful engagement and ensure students connect their analysis to real-world leadership challenges.
  • Provide clear instructions on completing the survey and reflecting on the results.
  • Please encourage students to think critically about how their conflict management style influences leadership effectiveness.
  • Facilitate a discussion or provide feedback on their reflection, helping them consider ways to adapt or improve their approach to conflict management.
  • Provide examples of effective infographics and explain the key elements of clear and concise visual communication.
  • Encourage creativity and critical thinking, ensuring students convey the connection between mindfulness and leadership in a visually appealing and informative way.
  • Offer feedback on content accuracy, visual clarity, and the infographic's effectiveness in communicating key points.

Conclusion

In this module, students will engage deeply with team emotions and conflict management themes, reflecting on how their personal conflict management style and leadership approach impact workplace dynamics. Through the discussion on workplace conflict, the conflict management style survey, and the infographic on mindfulness and leadership, students will gain practical tools for managing emotions and conflict in teams. This module encourages critical self-reflection and the development of leadership strategies that foster emotional intelligence, effective communication, and mindfulness in challenging situations.
