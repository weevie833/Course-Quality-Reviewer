Overview

“Behind intimidating messages are simply people appealing to us to meet their needs.” - Marshall B. Rosenberg

Communication systems deeply impact the life of organizations, their culture, and how human beings within organizations relate to internal and external stakeholders.

In this module we’ll explore organizations as communications systems, workplace bullying (a form of communication), and demographic differences.

You’ll also select and describe resources of your own choosing about how communication impacts organizational culture, and you’ll develop a final paper outline to help you organize your thoughts and set yourself up for success for the final paper.

Thank you for your hard work so far, and here’s to another illuminating week together!
