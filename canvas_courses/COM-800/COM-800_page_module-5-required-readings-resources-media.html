Overview

“The difference between moral dilemmas and ethical ones, philosophers say, is that in moral issues the choice is between right and wrong. In ethics, the choice is between two rights.” - Pamela Warrick

Everyone communicates. As leaders, however, we must be mindful of how our communications impact organizational culture and the ethical considerations associated with communications.

In this module, we’ll explore ethics to create a foundation for ways leaders cultivate organizational culture. We’ll also review strategies for handling defensive communication and managing difficult conversations and climates.

This module is also an excellent time to review feedback from the annotated bibliography and outline before beginning to write your draft paper.
