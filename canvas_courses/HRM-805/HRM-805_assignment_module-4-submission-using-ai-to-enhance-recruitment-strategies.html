Objective:

In this activity, you will examine how artificial intelligence (AI) can be used to enhance recruitment strategies in your organization or any organization you choose. You will explore how AI can improve job postings, streamline candidate screening, and support compliance with employment laws, while also reducing bias and aligning hiring practices with organizational goals.

In the process below, you will create TWO documents:

  1. A "working document" to gather all of your information into an unformatted collection. Do not submit your working document.
  2. A formal AI-Enhanced Recruitment Strategy Brief according to the specifications in Step 4 using your working document as a source. This is what you will submit for this assignments.

Instructions:

Step 1 – Select an Organization and Role

In this step, you will locate a job position.

  • Go to LinkedIn and click the Jobs icon (it looks like a briefcase) to review job postings.
  • In the search box, type in a company name and review job listings. =OR= type in a position in the search box and review the jobs for the companies listed.
  • Select the position you wish to explore.
  • Click into the position details and expand the "About the job" section.

Step 2 – Evaluate the Current Strategy

Create a Word document (your "working document") and follow the instructions below:

  • Paste the details of the job posting you located.
  • Below the job posting details, indicate where else would you recommend posting this job listing as a way to increase the breadth of qualified applicants, and why?
  • Explain how these steps would ensure fairness, inclusion, or legal compliance?

Step 3 – Apply AI to Improve the Strategy

Use an AI tool to help enhance the recruitment process according to these steps:

  • Select an AI tool: You may use any general purpose AI tool to conduct this this analysis such as Open AI’s ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Anthropic’s Claude, or X’s Grok. A subscription-based account is not needed – free accounts should be sufficient for this exercise. If you find that your selected AI tool is not producing useful results, try using a different system.
  • Evaluate the job posting: Initiate a query with your selected AI tool and ask for feedback on the job post based on biased or exclusionary language. Copy the following prompt and paste it into the AI dialog box, then revise it to reflect the specific industry you are interested in.

    IMPORTANT: After Phase 2, you will revise the job posting according to the suggestions offered by the AI assistant. After Phase 2, stop interacting with the AI assistant, update the job posting in your working document, copy the revised job posting, then return to the AI assistant to complete Phase 3.

You are a Human Resources professional with expertise in job description design for recruitment. You will conduct a review of a job post according to the phases of work below. Review the phases, then ask me to paste in my job description. Wait for me to submit before starting the review.
<Phase 1: Review> You will review my job recruitment post for adherence to legal or regulatory compliance and instances of bias or exclusionary language. You may also identify any other problems with the post according to contemporary standards or conventions in the [industry]. If you need clarifications, ask me first before proceeding.
</Phase 1: Review>
<Phase 2: Offer Suggestions>
Respond with a list of suggestions that includes direct references to the original post where the problems are located. If there are any regulatory, statutory, or other compliance problems, please include a citation for the source so that I can refer to it directly.
</Phase 2: Offer Suggestions> 
<Phase 3: Develop Screening Criteria>
Then, ask me to post a revision of the job description based on your feedback. Then, after I post my revision, help me develop structured, job-related screening criteria and behavioral interview questions based on the role’s requirements. Ensure these align with equal opportunity guidelines and job relevance. Allow us to engage in dialog to improve your output until the draft meets my needs.
</Phase 3: Develop Screening Criteria>
<Phase 4: Legal Ramifications>
Ask me if the final job description, screening criteria, and behavioral interview questions meet my needs. If so, proceed with identifying any legal risks (e.g., ADA, Title VII, FCRA) if I implemented these resources. Include any ethical concerns from having used AI-assistance for developing this material.
</Phase 4: Legal Ramifications>

  • Evaluate AI output and revise the job post: After Phase 2 output, review the AI’s suggestions and evaluate its accuracy. Revise the original job post in your Word document to reflect the improvements needed to expand inclusivity and remove bias. Identify specific changes that you made and notable improvements and add those comments in your Word document under your revised job posting. Then proceed with Phase 3 in your AI tool by pasting in your revised job post into the AI dialog.
  • Evaluate AI screening criteria and integrate the results: After Phase 3 output, evaluate the screening criteria generated by AI for accuracy and relevance. Copy it all into your Word document after your revised job post. Then continue with Phase 4 in the AI tool.
  • Legal and Ethical Considerations: After Phase 4 output, copy the AI tool's output and paste it into your Word document after the behavioral interview questions. This is your completed working document - do not submit your working document.

This completes your working document.

Step 4 – Develop Your AI-Enhanced Recruitment Brief

Write a complete documentation of your work as if you were submitting to senior leadership. Include the following sections:

  • Introduction: Describe the selected organization and job posting you selected.
  • Recommendations: Describe how the original ad was problematic, including which specific areas needed attention, and why.
  • AI-Enhanced Job Posting: Present your AI-enhanced job posting and screening/interview improvements. Include a summary of the areas where AI output contributed to the final product. Include any relevant citations to support compliance issues.
  • Key legal and ethical risks: Summarize the key legal and ethical considerations proposed by AI that you affirmed were relevant and how you would address them.
  • Reflection: After your brief, create a new Reflection section of your paper. Describe your experiences using AI in this exercise from the position of a professional. Include the following:
    • What were your initial thoughts and concerns about using AI as part of a professional HR task where there are ethical and compliance issues for which you are responsible?
    • How confident did you feel about integrating AI output into a deliverable for your organization? What would make you feel more confident about using AI as an HR professional?
    • Describe whether you felt using AI added strategic value to the recruitment process. If so, how? If not, why?
    • Comment on the professional development implications of using AI as an HR practitioner.

Deliverable:

Submit your completed AI-Enhanced Recruitment Strategy Brief that showcases your ability to use AI to strengthen recruitment practices while maintaining compliance, ethics, and strategic alignment.

Length: 3-4 pages, double-spaced.

Sources: Cite course readings and at least one outside academic or industry source
