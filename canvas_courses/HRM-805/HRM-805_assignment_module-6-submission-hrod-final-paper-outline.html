This week, you are expected to submit an outline of your HROD final paper. This submission will help you organize your ideas and give your instructor an opportunity to provide early feedback before the final paper is due.

Your outline should include:

  • Working title and topic
  • Key headings or sections
  • Main points to be addressed under each section
  • Any initial references or sources
  • Specific questions or areas where you would like feedback

If you are unfamiliar with creating an academic outline, please review the 10 minute tutorial: What is an Outline?
