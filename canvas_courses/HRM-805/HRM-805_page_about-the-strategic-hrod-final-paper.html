In today’s dynamic business environment, HR&OD strategies are no longer a support function but a critical driver of organizational competitiveness and success.  This final paper explores key HR topics discussed throughout the course to examine how HR initiatives contribute to sustainable competitive advantage.

The objective is to analyze HR as a value-added function by selecting a specific topic or a combination of topics that demonstrate how effective people management directly impacts organizational performance. 

Strategic HRM Paper – Creating HR Strategies to Enhance Organizational Competitiveness.

The goal is to select a topic, multiple topics, or HR in general related to the areas covered in class. The paper should be approximately 5-6 pages (double-spaced) and include the following:

  • Reason for selecting the topic: Establish how HR is a value-added function. - State the importance and relevance of the topic in creating competitive advantages in organizations. You can also explain if you have a personal interest in the topic and how it may help in your career and education.
  • Definition of the issue/topic: Inform the readers what the topic is about, what it means, and how it is used in organizations. You should cite definitions, theories, and discussions of the topic from your text, journal articles, or other books. It is usually beneficial to cite multiple interpretations of a concept, term, etc. to illustrate its full meaning.
  • Implications for organizations: Given the findings you came across throughout the course, case study analyses, etc., what are the implications for organizations? How can organizations utilize your information to become more effective and to create a competitive advantage utilizing the HR strategies?
  • Conclusion & summary: Based on all of the above-mentioned information, state your overall conclusion. You may include the benefits, challenges, how strategies may help an organization, etc.
