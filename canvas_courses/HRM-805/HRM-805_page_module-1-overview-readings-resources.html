Overview

This module provides an integrated overview of Human Resources and Organizational Development (HR&OD), highlighting their roles in enhancing organizational performance and aligning people strategies with business objectives. It introduces key concepts in Human Resource Development (HRD), Organizational Development (OD), Human Resource Management (HRM), and Change Management. The purpose of this module is to help learners understand how these interrelated areas support workforce capability, organizational agility, and strategic transformation.

Module Objectives: 

  • Articulate the strategic value of HR according to key metrics.
