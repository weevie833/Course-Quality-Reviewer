Overview

Module 2 explores the evolving strategic role of Human Resources and its integration with organizational development (OD) and leadership practices. Key topics include models of organizational strategy, the alignment of HR and OD with business goals, and the rise of human-centered leadership. The module emphasizes how strategic HR practices and effective leadership work together to shape culture, drive performance, and support long-term organizational success. Students will examine real-world applications and frameworks that illustrate how aligning people, processes, and purpose creates competitive advantage in today’s dynamic environment.

Module Objectives:

  • Describe how HR metrics relate to organizational performance.
  • Explain how data analytics contributes to decision-making that align with strategic goals.
  • Relate relevant SHRM Competencies that demonstrate how HR supports organizational leadership.
