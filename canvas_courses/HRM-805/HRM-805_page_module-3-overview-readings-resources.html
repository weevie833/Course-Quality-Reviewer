Overview

Module 3 focuses on aligning talent strategies with organizational goals through strategic workforce planning, succession planning, and the use of analytics. Students will examine how organizations forecast talent needs, identify skill gaps, and plan for future leadership through intentional succession planning. The module also introduces workforce analytics as a tool to support evidence-based HR decisions. Additionally, the design of jobs will be explored as a strategic lever to improve performance, engagement, and adaptability. 

Module Objectives:

  • Assess how data analytics can be employed to anticipate staffing needs and succession planning.
  • Describe how data analytics contributes to organizational performance.
