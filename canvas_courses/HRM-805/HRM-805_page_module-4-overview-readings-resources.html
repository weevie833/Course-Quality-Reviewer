Overview

Module 4 examines the legal, ethical, and strategic dimensions of managing talent in today’s global workforce. Students will gain an understanding of employment law, both national and international, and how it shapes workplace policies and practices. The module emphasizes the importance of preventing workplace mistreatment, including harassment, discrimination, and unethical behavior, to foster a respectful and inclusive work environment. In addition, students will explore the full scope of talent acquisition, including recruitment, selection, and the use of analytics in sourcing.

Module Objectives:

  • Relate how HR practices assure organizational compliance with laws, regulations, and principles of fairness and equity.
  • Describe how HR practices can align with organizational goals while upholding standards for fairness and legal requirements.
