Overview

Module 5 focuses on building individual and organizational capability through learning and development, organizational development (OD), and performance management. Students will explore how effective training strategies and continuous learning foster employee growth and support business objectives. The module also examines OD interventions that drive change, improve culture, and enhance organizational effectiveness. Additionally, students will analyze performance management systems that align employee goals with strategic priorities, promote accountability, and support professional development. 

Module Objectives:

  • Describe the relationship between L & D practices and achieving organizational goals.
  • Describe how data analytics informs areas in need of development.
  • Propose L & D strategies that promote organizational agility and innovation.
