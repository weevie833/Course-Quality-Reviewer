Overview

Module 6 explores how organizations design and implement strategic compensation systems to attract, retain, and motivate talent. Students will examine the principles of internal and external equity, including job evaluation, market competitiveness, and pay transparency, and how these factors influence employee satisfaction and organizational fairness. The module also highlights the growing role of analytics in compensation, enabling data-driven decisions that align pay practices with performance, budget constraints, and strategic goals. 

Module Objectives:

  • Relate how compensation triangulates between achieving business goals and recruiting top talent.
  • Describe how equity and parity operates both internally and externally.
  • Describe how data analytics informs fairness in compensation and broader strategic decisions about performance and retention.
