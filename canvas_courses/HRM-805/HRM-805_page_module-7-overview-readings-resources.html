Overview

Module 7 explores the connection between employee engagement, retention, and positive organizational behavior. Students will examine how engaged employees contribute to organizational success through higher productivity, innovation, and commitment. The module highlights strategies to foster a positive work environment that supports psychological well-being, resilience, and meaningful work. Emphasis is placed on understanding the drivers of retention and how HR can proactively design programs that reduce turnover and build a culture of trust and purpose.

Module Objectives:

  • Describe how key metrics inform organizational performance.
  • Describe culturally responsible methods for onboarding and retention.
