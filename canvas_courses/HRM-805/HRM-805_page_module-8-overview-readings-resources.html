Overview

Module 8 focuses on the complexities and opportunities of managing Human Resources in a global context. Students will explore the role of Global HRM in navigating diverse legal systems, labor markets, and talent strategies across international boundaries. A key component of the module is assessing culture, where students examine cultural dimensions and their impact on leadership, communication, and employee expectations. The module also addresses organizational development (OD) in cross-cultural environments, highlighting how change initiatives must be adapted to different cultural norms and values.

Module Objectives:

  • Describe how data analytics informs responses to trends in organizational performance at a cultural level.
  • Propose strategies to cultivate organizational coherence and retention through cultural engagement.
  • Describe how cultural values inform workforce planning and talent development.
  • Produce a comprehensive HROD plan.
