This assignment challenges you to analyze how a business operates within the global economy while considering its ethical, social, and governmental responsibilities. You will explore how Human Resources and Organization Development (HR & OD) contribute to international business success, especially in managing cross-cultural dynamics, navigating trade complexities, and advancing corporate social responsibility (CSR).

Using your organization or a company of your choice, conduct a global business expansion analysis. Support your work with readings from Chapter 3, peer-reviewed articles, and media materials from this week’s module.

Assignment Instructions

Choose a real or hypothetical company and a product or service it currently offers. Then, develop a strategic analysis of global expansion into two international markets. Your report should include the following sections:

Part 1: Market Potential and Globalization Impact

  1. Market Assessment
    • Identify two countries where your chosen product/service has growth potential.
    • Support your rationale using data (e.g., GDP, consumer demographics, industry growth, etc.).
  2. Cultural, Legal, and Economic Adaptation
    • Compare the cultural dimensions (e.g., Hofstede’s model) of the two countries.
    • Describe how the product/service may need to be adapted for success in each country’s geography, economy, legal system, and cultural preferences.
  3. Globalization’s Influence
    • Discuss how globalization has influenced trade, investment, and workforce opportunities for this organization.

Part 2: Barriers, Entry Strategies, and HR/OD Implications

  1. Trade Barriers
    • Identify formal (tariffs, regulations) and informal (social norms, corruption) barriers to entry.
  2. Market Entry Mode
    • Recommend a market entry strategy (e.g., licensing, franchising, joint venture, wholly owned subsidiary).
    • Justify your choice based on risk, control, and resource availability.
  3. Role of HR & OD
    • Analyze the role of HR professionals in supporting the international strategy.
    • Address the following:
      • Managing culturally diverse teams
      • Ensuring compliance with labor standards
      • Supporting CSR and environmental sustainability
      • Facilitating cross-cultural training and ethical leadership

Part 3: Ethics and Corporate Citizenship

  1. Strategic CSR and Business Ethics
    • Apply Ferhani (2025) and Bawack et al. (2025) to explain how responsible leadership and CSR can enhance organizational reputation and performance in global markets.
    • Discuss the alignment of business objectives with social and environmental expectations in the selected countries.
