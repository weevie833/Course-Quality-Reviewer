As the labor market evolves due to technology, demographic shifts, policy changes, and globalization, it is crucial for business professionals, HR leaders, and educators to understand which jobs are growing, which are declining, and how to anticipate future workforce needs. This assignment will help you explore current employment trends and apply strategic thinking to future job forecasting.

Assignment Instructions:
Using the course textbook, U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) resources, and assigned readings, create a professional presentation that addresses the following:

  1. Identify Rapidly Growing Jobs:
    • What occupations are expected to grow the fastest over the next 10 years?
    • Which industries or sectors are contributing to this growth?
  2. Describe Jobs in Decline:
    • What types of jobs are projected to decrease in demand or disappear?
    • What factors (e.g., automation, offshoring, changing consumer habits) are driving these declines?
  3. Analyze Predictive Factors:
    • What key trends, technologies, or policies can be used to anticipate which job categories will grow or decline in the future?
    • How can organizations and individuals use this information for workforce planning and career development?
  4. Connect to Human Resources and Organizational Development:
    • What are the implications for HR professionals and organizational leaders?
    • How should businesses respond through re-skilling, recruitment, or workforce strategy?

Format Requirements:

  • Presentation format: PowerPoint (6–8 slides recommended)
  • Visuals: Include relevant charts, graphs, or infographics (e.g., from BLS or industry sources)
  • Speaker notes: Include brief speaker notes or a narrative transcript summarizing key points
  • References: Cite at least three sources, including the BLS, your textbook, and one scholarly or practitioner article
