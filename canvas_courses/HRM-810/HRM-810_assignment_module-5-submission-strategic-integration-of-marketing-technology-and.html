This assignment challenges you to explore how modern organizations use marketing and technology to drive business outcomes and how HR and OD professionals play a critical role in supporting these efforts. You will analyze a real or hypothetical organization’s approach to integrating marketing and technology, and evaluate how HR strategies can ensure workforce readiness, collaboration, and innovation in a rapidly changing business environment.

Assignment Instructions:

Step 1: Choose an Organization - Select a company you work for, have worked for, or are familiar with. It may also be a startup or nonprofit. Ensure you can access or research enough detail about its marketing and technology practices.

Step 2: Write a 3–4 Pages Analysis (APA Format) - Structure your report as follows:

Section 1: Marketing & Technology Integration

  • Describe how the organization identifies customer needs and designs marketing strategies.
  • Explain how technology is used to support these efforts (e.g., CRM, digital marketing tools, analytics, automation).
  • Describe how these efforts will impact qualitative customer experience or business growth.

Section 2: Organizational Readiness & Cross-Functional Collaboration

  • Analyze how HR and OD can support the integration of marketing and technology.
  • Discuss how HR facilitates communication and collaboration between marketing, IT, and other departments.
  • Identify any gaps in alignment or areas of resistance to innovation. Include at least one proposed initiative (e.g., a learning program, innovation lab, or cross-training model).

Section 3: Workforce Development & Change Management

  • Recommend specific training or professional development initiatives to prepare employees for digital transformation and cross-functional collaboration.
  • Describe how HR can foster a culture of adaptability, continuous learning, and innovation.

Section 4: Strategic Reflection

  • Conclude with your reflections on how organizations can position themselves to remain competitive by aligning marketing, technology, and HR strategies.
  • Highlight the future role of HR & OD professionals in sustaining this alignment.

As part of your assignment, include content analyzing how artificial intelligence (AI) can support the integration of marketing, technology, and HR functions within your chosen organization:

  • Discuss how AI tools, such as chatbots, predictive analytics, or AI-driven learning platforms, can enhance customer experience, streamline operations, and support workforce.
  • Utilize course resources, Chat GPT, and other tools to develop your response.

Requirements:

  • 3-4 double-spaced pages (excluding title page and references)
  • APA 7th edition formatting
  • Minimum 3 sources, including:
    • Course textbook (Chapters 10 & 11)
    • At least one scholarly article
    • One credible industry source (e.g., BLS, Gartner, McKinsey, SHRM)
