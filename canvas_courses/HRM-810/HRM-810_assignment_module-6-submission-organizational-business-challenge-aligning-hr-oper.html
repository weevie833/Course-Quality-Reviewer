You are acting as a strategic advisor for an organization (select your employer or any other organization). Your role is to develop an integrated improvement proposal that addresses key financial and operational challenges while leveraging HR and OD strategies for sustainable impact.

Instructions:

Step 1: Analyze the organization - Identify the most critical operational and financial issues.

Step 2: Create an Individual Improvement Proposal (2–3 pages, APA format) - Include the following sections:

Problem Identification

      • Describe two key financial and/or operational problems at the organization
      • Explain how these issues impact the company's performance and sustainability

Integrated Strategy Recommendations - Provide three actionable recommendations:

      1. HR or OD Strategy – e.g., training, workforce planning, performance systems
      2. Operational Improvement – e.g., scheduling, inventory control, lean process
      3. Financial Alignment – e.g., cost savings, budgeting, forecasting

For each recommendation, briefly explain:

      • What you would implement
      • How it addresses the problem
      • Expected outcome (e.g., reduced turnover, cost savings, improved efficiency). Use your organizational KPIs to show how these recommends will impact these variables/outcomes.

Implementation & Change Support

      • Describe how you would support adoption of your recommendations (e.g., communication, training, leadership involvement)
      • Address any potential resistance or risks and how you’d mitigate them

Step 3 (Optional): Create a 1-slide infographic or summary chart - Visually summarize your recommendations and expected results.

Step 4: Submit a 1-paragraph reflection - Answer: What did this exercise teach you about the role of HR & OD in operational and financial success?

Note: This assignment is designated Level 1 - No AI. The assignment must be completed entirely without AI assistance. The assignment must be produced relying solely on conventional research and study methods. AI must not be used at any point during the production of the assignment.
