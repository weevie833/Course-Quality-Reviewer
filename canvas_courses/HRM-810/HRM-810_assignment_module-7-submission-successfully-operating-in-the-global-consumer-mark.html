In this assignment, you will analyze how an organization can successfully operate in the global consumer marketplace while also prioritizing internal financial wellness for its workforce. Drawing from Chapters 15 and 19 and relevant course materials, you will create a dual-focus strategy that addresses both external market demands and internal employee development—demonstrating the essential role of HR and OD in bridging these domains.

Assignment Instructions:

Step 1: Select an Organization - Choose a real-world company or create a hypothetical global organization. This may be a company you currently work for, have worked for, or one you're interested in researching.

Step 2: Write a 3-4 Page Strategy Paper (APA Format) - Organize your paper using the following sections:

Section A: Global Consumer Analysis

  • Identify two international markets your company currently serves (or seeks to enter).
  • Analyze how cultural, economic, or technological factors influence consumer behavior in each market.
  • Provide examples of how the company has (or should) adapt its offerings, marketing, or services to align with global consumer expectations.

Section B: Financial Strategy and HR’s Role

  • Discuss the importance of financial literacy and savings/investment strategies for employee well-being.
  • Propose one or two financial wellness initiatives HR can implement (e.g., retirement education, budgeting workshops, investment planning tools).
  • Explain how these programs can enhance retention, engagement, and organizational performance.

Section C: Talent Strategy for Global Readiness

  • Recommend HR/OD practices that would better prepare the workforce to serve global consumers (e.g., cultural training, international assignments, virtual collaboration skills).
  • Discuss how these strategies align talent development with global market competitiveness.

Section D: Integration and Strategic Alignment

  • Explain how HR, OD, and finance departments can collaborate to align employee financial wellness programs with broader organizational goals.
  • Reflect on how this dual focus contributes to long-term sustainability, global responsiveness, and employee satisfaction.
Note: This assignment is designated Level 1 - No AI. The assignment must be completed entirely without AI assistance. The assignment must be produced relying solely on conventional research and study methods. AI must not be used at any point during the production of the assignment.
