This culminating assignment provides an opportunity for you to demonstrate how Human Resources and Organizational Development (HR & OD) strategically supports core business functions.

Drawing on concepts from all eight modules, including economics, global strategy, entrepreneurship, marketing, finance, and operations, you will analyze a real or hypothetical organization to illustrate HR’s integrated role in driving performance, innovation, and sustainability.

Note: There are TWO parts to this assignment. This is PART 1. Part 2 is a separate presentation submission assignment.

Part 1: Written Report (6–8 pages, APA format)

Title: HR’s Strategic Role Across Functional Business Areas

Organize your paper into the following sections:

  1. Executive Summary (0.5–1 page)
    Provide a brief overview of the organization and summarize your key findings and recommendations.
  2. Business Overview & Strategic Goals (1 page)
    Describe the organization’s mission, industry context, global presence, and primary strategic objectives.
  3. HR & OD Integration Across Business Functions (3–4 pages)
    Using concepts from Modules 1–7, analyze HR’s role in the following areas:
    • Finance & Operations: How does HR support financial health, cost control, and operational efficiency?
    • Marketing & Technology: How does HR enable customer-centric innovation and cross-functional collaboration?
    • Entrepreneurship & Growth: How does HR foster agile leadership and organizational culture to support startups or scaling?
    • Global Strategy & CSR: How does HR align global talent and ethical practices with consumer and stakeholder expectations?
  4. Organizational Development & Change Management (1–1.5 pages)
    Recommend one OD intervention to support a current or anticipated change initiative (e.g., restructure, expansion, system implementation).
    Include implementation steps, expected outcomes, and HR’s leadership role.
  5. Conclusion & Reflection (0.5 page)
    Summarize what this analysis reveals about the strategic role of HR. Reflect on how your perspective on HR’s business acumen has evolved.
Note: This assignment is designated Level 1 - No AI. The assignment must be completed entirely without AI assistance. The assignment must be produced relying solely on conventional research and study methods. AI must not be used at any point during the production of the assignment.
