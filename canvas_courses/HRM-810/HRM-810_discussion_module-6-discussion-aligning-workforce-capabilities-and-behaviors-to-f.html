In successful organizations, financial management, operations, and human resources are deeply interconnected. Effective budgeting, cost control, and operational excellence depend not only on smart systems and strategy, but also on the people who carry them out. HR and OD professionals play a vital role in aligning workforce capabilities and behaviors with financial and operational priorities.
