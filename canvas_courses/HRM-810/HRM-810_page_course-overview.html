Welcome to HRM 810 Business Acumen: Role of HR in Business

This course provides an overview of business operations and the role of the Human Resources function. The course addresses major business concepts including finance, marketing, operations and management. In this context, students will examine how HR professionals effectively communicate with leadership to make a substantial impact on organizational outcomes and strengthen its competitive edge. Students will learn how to align HR practices with an organization's goals, values, and mission, to take proactive steps, drive meaningful change, and build trust with senior leadership as a valuable strategic partner within the organization.

This course will enable you to connect the strategic role of Human Resource and Organization Development (HR & OD) to achieving organizational goals and effectiveness. Course activities are structured to reinforce theoretical knowledge with practical application through a variety of learning activities. 

  1. HR & OD Application Papers – Students will submit short papers throughout the term that address key HRD concepts discussed in class. These assignments allow learners to apply theoretical principles to real-world scenarios in a concise, reflective format.
  2. Case Analyses – Brief case studies from the textbook will be examined to encourage critical thinking and integration of course concepts. These cases will serve as a platform for exploring diverse perspectives and applying analytical skills.
  3. Weekly Discussions – Each week, students will participate in online discussions via Canvas, connecting business topics to HR implications. These discussions are designed to foster engagement, peer learning, and the practical application of course material.
  4. Individual and Group Integrative Paper – Learners will work individually and in teams to explore the comprehensive role of HRD in organizations. This assignment promotes collaboration and synthesis of knowledge gained throughout the course.
  5. Chapter Assessments – Students will complete 2–3 page assessments summarizing and reflecting on the “Review Business Concepts” from each chapter. These assessments reinforce comprehension and application of key ideas.
  6. Research Paper: HR’s Impact on Business Performance – A culminating assignment requires students to research a real-world example demonstrating how HR practices have enhanced organizational performance. This 6–8 page paper must include integration of multiple business functions, highlighting the strategic role of HR within the organization.
