Description of Course Activities and Requirements: 

  • HR & OD Application Papers – These are short papers addressing the respective topics covered during the term. 
  • Cases – Short cases from the textbook will be used to analyze and integrate learner perspectives. 
  • Discussions – Weekly discussions addressing the specific topic. 
  • Individual and Group Paper – This is an integrative paper addressing the role of HRD in organizations. 

Chapter Assessments - Each chapter in the textbook provides Review Business Concepts. 

  • Approximately 2-3 pages double spaced.

Discussions

  • Canvas Discussions: Students will be expected to connect the respective business topic to HR in the weekly discussion topic. 

Research Paper on HR Impacting Business Performance

  • Students will be asked to research and describe an actual organizational example as to how an organization utilized HR practices to enhance organizational effectiveness. Integration of the business functions is a necessity. 6 – 8 pages double spaced.
