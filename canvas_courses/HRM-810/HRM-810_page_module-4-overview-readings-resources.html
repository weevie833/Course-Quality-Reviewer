Overview

This module focuses on the essential principles of management and leadership, the strategic importance of organizational culture and diversity, and the role of career planning and development in sustaining a productive workforce. Students will explore the differences between management and leadership styles, the impact of inclusive and values-driven cultures, and how organizations support employee growth through structured development programs.

The module emphasizes the integral role of Human Resources (HR) and Organizational Development (OD) in shaping effective leadership, fostering a diverse and inclusive culture, and guiding employees through career paths that align with both individual aspirations and organizational goals. HR and OD professionals play a critical part in coaching leaders, facilitating cultural change, and implementing career development systems that enhance retention and engagement.

Assignments this week include:

  • Chapter 7 Assessments: Evaluate key concepts in management and leadership.
  • Discussion #4: Reflect on how HR and OD influence leadership effectiveness, organizational culture, and employee development.
