Overview

This module explores the dynamic relationship between marketing, business technology, and organizational success in today’s competitive environment. Students will examine how businesses identify customer needs, develop marketing strategies, and leverage technology to improve operations, communication, and decision-making. Key topics include digital marketing, data analytics, and the integration of new technologies into business functions.

The role of Human Resources (HR) and Organizational Development (OD) is critical in supporting these functions by building teams that can adapt to technological change, fostering a culture of innovation, and providing training that aligns workforce capabilities with evolving business demands. HR and OD professionals also play a strategic role in change management and in facilitating cross-functional collaboration between marketing, IT, and other departments.

Assignments this week include:

  • Chapter 10 Assessments: Demonstrate how HR/OD contributes to supporting core marketing concepts and their application in business.
  • Discussion #5: Analyze how HR and OD contribute to the successful implementation of marketing strategies and technological advancements.
