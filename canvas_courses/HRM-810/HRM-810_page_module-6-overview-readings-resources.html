Overview

This module examines the core principles of financial management and business operations, focusing on how organizations manage resources, control costs, and optimize production processes. Students will learn how budgeting, forecasting, and financial analysis guide decision-making, while operational strategies ensure efficiency, quality, and customer satisfaction across the supply chain.

The strategic role of Human Resources (HR) and Organizational Development (OD) is emphasized in aligning people, processes, and performance with financial and operational goals. HR supports operational success through workforce planning, training, and performance management, while OD initiatives help streamline processes, foster continuous improvement, and support change management efforts. Together, HR and OD ensure that human capital strategies are integrated with broader business objectives.

Assignments this week include:

  • Chapter 13 Assessments: Apply key operational concepts to real-world business scenarios.
  • Discussion #6: Explore how HR and OD contribute to financial and operational effectiveness in various industries.
