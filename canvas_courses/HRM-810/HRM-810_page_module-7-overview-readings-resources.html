Overview

This module explores the behavior of consumers in the global economy and the importance of savings and investment strategies for individuals and organizations. Students will examine how cultural, economic, and technological factors influence global consumer behavior and purchasing decisions. Additionally, the module covers foundational concepts in personal and corporate finance, including savings, investment planning, and financial goal setting.

The role of Human Resources (HR) and Organizational Development (OD) intersects with these themes through financial wellness programs, global talent strategy, and benefits planning that support both employee financial health and organizational competitiveness. HR and OD professionals help organizations respond to global consumer trends by developing culturally aware teams and aligning talent with international market demands, while also fostering a financially literate and future-focused workforce.

Assignments this week include:

  • Chapter 15 Assessments: Demonstrate how consumers behave in a globalized economy.
  • Discussion #7: Reflect on how HR and OD can support financial well-being and prepare organizations to meet global consumer expectations.
