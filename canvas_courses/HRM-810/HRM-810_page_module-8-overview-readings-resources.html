Overview

This culminating module focuses on the strategic role of Human Resources & Organization Development (HR & OD) across all core functional business areas, including marketing, finance, operations, and information technology. Students will synthesize what they’ve learned throughout the course to examine how HR supports and enhances each area by aligning talent strategies with organizational goals, fostering collaboration, and driving performance. Emphasis is placed on Organizational Development (OD) as a key mechanism for managing change, improving processes, and cultivating a resilient and agile workforce.

The final presentations provide an opportunity for students to demonstrate their understanding of HR’s impact on business success through research, analysis, and practical application. These capstone presentations highlight the integration of HR strategies within various business functions and reinforce the value of HR and OD as strategic partners in achieving sustainable organizational growth.

Assignments this week include:

  • Discussion #8: Reflect on how HR and OD influence decision-making and performance across business functions.
  • Final Presentations: Showcase key insights and practical applications of HR’s role in supporting and advancing core business areas.
