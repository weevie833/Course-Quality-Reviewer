As the culminating assignment for this course, your group presentation will demonstrate your ability to analyze and apply key employment law concepts to real-world organizational scenarios.

  • Working in teams, you will research a current legal issue or court case impacting HR practices, such as wrongful termination, workplace privacy, discrimination, or labor law compliance and present your findings to the class.
  • Your presentation should clearly outline the background of the issue, relevant laws or regulations, implications for employers and employees, and recommended HR strategies or policy changes.
  • In addition to your summary and analysis, assess whether your selected issue or case has any implications. For example, could this legal principle or trend differ in another country due to cultural norms, labor laws, or international trade considerations? If the issue is primarily domestic, explain why and identify a comparable or contrasting approach in another jurisdiction.
  • Support your analysis with either assigned readings or at least one supplemental scholarly or reputable global HR/legal source.

Presentations should be approximately 15–20 minutes, include visual aids (e.g., slides), and reflect a collaborative effort among all team members. Refer to instructions for recording your group presentation: Instructions for recording a group presentation to video in MS Teams

This assignment is an opportunity to showcase your understanding of course content, legal analysis skills, and practical application in an HR context.
