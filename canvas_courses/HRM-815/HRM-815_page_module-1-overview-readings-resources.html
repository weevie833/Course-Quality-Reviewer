Overview

Welcome to Module 1. In this first week, we will lay the groundwork for understanding employment law by exploring the origins, sources, and foundational concepts that govern the employer-employee relationship. You will begin by examining the legal frameworks that influence workplace practices in the U.S. and how these may differ across global contexts. A key component of this module is understanding what constitutes an employment relationship and how different types of employment (e.g., independent contractor vs. employee) impact legal responsibilities.

This module sets the stage for deeper exploration in future weeks, where we will delve into specific areas such as discrimination, hiring practices, workplace testing, and employee rights. As you progress through the course, you’ll continue to build on these foundational principles to analyze more complex legal and ethical issues in domestic and international employment contexts.
