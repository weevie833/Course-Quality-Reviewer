Overview

In Module 5, we turn our attention to employee accommodations and protections related to disability, religion, gender identity, and work-life balance. You’ll explore the legal foundations of the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA), the Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA), and other relevant regulations. These laws play a crucial role in promoting inclusive and equitable workplaces.

We’ll also examine the complex relationship between organizational leave policies, gender identity expression in the workplace, and the employer’s role in fostering belonging. This week’s focus prepares you to identify reasonable accommodations, interpret leave entitlements, and recommend HR practices that comply with the law while meeting organizational needs.

This module builds on the concepts of discrimination and harassment explored in Modules 2–4 and provides a framework for understanding how inclusive policies support retention, productivity, and legal compliance.
