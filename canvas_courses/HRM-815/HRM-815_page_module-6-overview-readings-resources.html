Overview

In Module 6, we examine one of the most regulated and closely watched aspects of HR: compensation and benefits. You'll explore how federal laws such as the Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA) and the Equal Pay Act (EPA) shape employer responsibilities and influence compensation structures.

This module will help you understand the legal standards for minimum wage, overtime pay, employee classification, and pay equity. We will also address which benefits employers are legally required to provide, the difference between statutory and voluntary benefits, and how these offerings can impact recruitment, retention, and organizational culture.

As you analyze real-world compensation and benefit challenges, you’ll begin to recognize the balance HR must strike between legal compliance, fairness, and financial sustainability.

Groups are expected to have an outline of final paper with brief descriptions.
