Overview

In Module 7, you will explore two critical areas of employment law and HR practice: employee privacy and termination procedures. You will examine workplace privacy protections, including the legal boundaries of monitoring and surveying employees, as outlined in Chapter 15. You will also analyze how organizations can balance operational needs with employee rights. Chapter 16 will guide you through the process of terminating individual employees, focusing on both proper procedures and impermissible grounds for termination.
