Overview

In Module 8, you will explore current and emerging issues in employment law that are shaping today’s workplaces. You will research recent trends, such as changes in labor regulations, evolving workplace protections, and the legal implications of remote and hybrid work environments. Additionally, you will examine recent court cases that have had a significant impact on organizational policies and HR practices.

Through Discussion #8, you will engage in critical reflection on how these legal developments affect both employers and employees. This final week also includes your group presentation, where you will showcase your understanding of key course concepts through real-world application.

The course concludes on Friday, so be sure to complete all final assignments and submissions by the deadline.
