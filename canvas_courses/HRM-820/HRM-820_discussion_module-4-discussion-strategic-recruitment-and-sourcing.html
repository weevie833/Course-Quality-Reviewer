Attracting the right talent requires more than posting job openings. It demands a strategic approach to recruitment that is aligned with organizational goals, cost considerations, and workforce needs. External recruitment strategies expand the talent pool, while internal recruitment supports development and retention. Both approaches require careful planning, clear communication, and attention to candidate experience. Measuring outcomes through recruitment metrics (e.g., cost-per-hire, time-to-fill, quality of hire) ensures strategies are both effective and efficient.

  • A need to retain and promote current employees to strengthen morale and reduce turnover.
  • Pressure from leadership to reduce recruitment costs and shorten time-to-fill.
