Welcome to HRM 820 Recruitment and Selection:

In HRM 820, students will design, build, implement, and evaluate recruitment and selection tools and practices that align with organizational outcomes and strategic goals. Emphasis is placed on identifying, attracting, and competing for talent that supports competitive advantage. Students will examine and apply key concepts such as strategic staffing, job analysis, workforce forecasting and planning, selection methods, and performance evaluation, all within the framework of legal and ethical compliance.

The course also emphasizes the integration of labor market trends, skills gap analysis, and cost-effective decision-making in recruitment and retention strategies. Through discussions, case analyses, staffing papers, and a group consulting project, students will develop a comprehensive understanding of how to implement talent acquisition practices that drive organizational success. Importantly, students will incorporate course content from HRM 805 analyzing the role of recruitment and selection in meeting team and organizational outcomes.
