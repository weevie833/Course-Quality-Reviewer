Use your UNH Outlook Calendar to schedule meeting times. 

  1. Access your UNH Outlook Calendar through your UNH email. Look for the calendar button below the email button in Outlook.



  2. Select the day/time to invite your team members by clicking on the cell that corresponds to the day/time you are proposing.



  3. Add a title that makes sense to your invitees. Type in each team member as an invitee. UNH's Outlook will automatically locate the email address for each team member - select them as they appear. Make sure the Teams Meeting slider is activated, as shown below.



  4. Click the Send button. A notification will be sent to all invitees' UNH Outlook accounts where they will either accept the meeting invitation, decline it, or suggest a new time.
  5. At the appointed time, click the Join button in the calendar invite. This will launch Microsoft Teams video chat.



  6. You will be prompted to launch the desktop version of Teams video. DECLINE this prompt by clicking CANCEL. Instead, click the "Continue on this browser" button. The video session will launch in your browser without delay.
