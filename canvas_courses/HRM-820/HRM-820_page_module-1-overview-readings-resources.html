Overview

Welcome to Module 1 of the Recruitment and Selection course. In this foundational module, we introduce the core concepts that will shape your understanding of staffing throughout the term. You will explore what staffing entails, why it matters, and how it functions as a strategic component of organizational success. We'll examine staffing models and how they guide recruitment and selection processes, and you'll learn how staffing decisions align with broader business and HR strategies.

This week’s activities are designed to orient you to the course and your classmates while also initiating your critical thinking about staffing. The “Meet Your Classmates” discussion helps build our learning community, while Discussion #1 challenges you to distinguish between traditional and strategic staffing models, an essential distinction for effective HR practice.
