Overview

In Module 2, we build on the strategic concepts introduced in Module 1 by exploring the legal and organizational contexts that shape staffing decisions. We begin with an examination of the employment relationship and the legal framework, emphasizing how compliance with labor laws and regulations is fundamental to ethical and effective staffing. Understanding the social and legal environment is essential for minimizing risk and promoting fairness in recruitment and selection processes.

We will also examine how internal and external influences, such as labor markets, organizational goals, and workforce demographics, impact human resource planning. We also focus to the mechanics of staffing planning, including how to develop internal staffing strategies that align with business needs. These concepts will help you understand how organizations forecast talent needs and design effective staffing systems.
