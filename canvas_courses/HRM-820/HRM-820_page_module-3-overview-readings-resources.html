Overview

Module 3 focuses on the essential role of job analysis in the recruitment and selection process. Accurate and thorough job analysis is the foundation for nearly every staffing activity, from writing job descriptions to assessing candidates. You will explore the need for job analysis, learn how to conduct a job requirements analysis, and examine how competency modeling provides a broader view of what it takes to succeed in a given role. In addition, you'll be introduced to job rewards analysis, which highlights how understanding both the demands and the benefits of a job can improve recruitment and retention outcomes.

This week’s activities are designed to help you connect these analytical tools to real-world staffing practices. In Discussion #3, you will reflect on how organizations use, or fail to use job analysis effectively. In Case Study #1, you will apply course concepts to a practical scenario, evaluating how job requirements and competencies influence hiring outcomes.
