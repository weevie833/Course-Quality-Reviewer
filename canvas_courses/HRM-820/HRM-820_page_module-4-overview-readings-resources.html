Overview

In Module 4, we explore the transition from job analysis to the implementation of effective recruitment strategies. Building on the job and competency data developed in the previous module, this week focuses on strategic recruitment planning, a process that ensures alignment between organizational goals and the methods used to attract talent.

You will examine both external and internal recruitment strategies, including how to define recruitment goals, develop targeted communication messages, and select appropriate sourcing channels. A key focus will be on the effectiveness of these strategies, evaluated through recruitment metrics that measure quality, cost, and efficiency. Additionally, we will explore how branding, outreach, and candidate experience influence an organization’s ability to attract the right applicants.
