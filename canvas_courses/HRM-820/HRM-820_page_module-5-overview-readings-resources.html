Overview

Module 5 creates a shift from recruitment to selection, focusing on how organizations evaluate candidates using reliable, valid, and strategic assessment tools. Drawing from Chapters 7 and 8 of Staffing Organizations, this module emphasizes the importance of measurement in staffing decisions. You will explore what makes a measure high-quality, including reliability, validity, and utility, and how those measures contribute to more accurate and equitable hiring outcomes.

Key concepts this week include the logic of prediction focusing on how organizations use assessment data to forecast future job performance and the nature of predictors, such as resumes, application blanks, and biographical data. You'll also examine initial assessment methods that help organizations efficiently screen applicants while balancing fairness, legal compliance, and strategic fit.
