Overview

In Module 6, we complete the staffing cycle by focusing on how organizations make final selection decisions, extend job offers, and transition candidates into employees. Building on the assessment and prediction tools introduced in Module 5, this module examines how to choose the most appropriate assessment methods, assign scores, and use that data to make informed, equitable hiring decisions.

You will also explore the employment relationship from the organization’s perspective, including legal considerations, psychological contracts, and candidate expectations. Key topics include the job offer process, its content (e.g., compensation, benefits, contingencies), and the strategic handling of negotiations and acceptances. Finally, we address the critical role of new employee orientation and socialization in ensuring a successful match and long-term retention.
