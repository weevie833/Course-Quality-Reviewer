Overview

In Module 7, we explore the final and often most critical stage of the staffing cycle: retention management. Even the most effective recruitment and selection strategies can fall short if organizations are unable to retain the talent they work so hard to acquire. Drawing from Chapter 14 of Staffing Organizations, this module focuses on the causes and consequences of employee turnover, and how organizations can design targeted strategies to reduce voluntary departures.

You will begin by analyzing the different types and drivers of turnover, including avoidable and unavoidable, functional and dysfunctional forms. We then examine how to conduct turnover analysis using data to uncover trends and root causes. The module concludes with a focus on retention initiatives, emphasizing proactive and strategic approaches to keeping top performers.

Readings
