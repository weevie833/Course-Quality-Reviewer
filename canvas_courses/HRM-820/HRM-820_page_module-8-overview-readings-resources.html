Overview

As we conclude the course, Module 8 invites you to explore emerging trends and challenges that are reshaping recruitment and selection practices. This includes a critical look at the use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in hiring, as well as broader ethical, legal, and strategic implications of technology-driven decision-making. You will engage with current journal articles to deepen your understanding of how innovations like algorithmic screening, data analytics, and virtual assessments are influencing the staffing landscape.

Discussion #8 provides an opportunity to reflect on a topical issue of your choice such as AI, hiring tools, data privacy, or another trend and consider its impact on both organizations and applicants.

This week also marks the submission of your Group Project and Presentation, where your team will deliver an integrated staffing plan for your chosen organization. This final assignment brings together all the key elements of the course, strategy, planning, legal compliance, recruitment, selection, and retention, and showcases your ability to apply course concepts in a practical, consulting-oriented format.

Please remember: The course concludes on Friday, so ensure all work is submitted on time. Congratulations on reaching the final module!
