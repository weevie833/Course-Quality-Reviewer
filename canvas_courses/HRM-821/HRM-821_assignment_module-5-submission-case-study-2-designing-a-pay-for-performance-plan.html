Scenario: An organization is facing stagnant performance and declining employee engagement. Senior leadership has asked HR to develop a pay-for-performance plan to better align employee efforts with organizational goals. The challenge is to design a system that motivates employees, drives results, and maintains fairness, while also fitting the organization’s culture and strategic priorities.

Task:

  • Analyze the organization’s strategic goals and identify how compensation can reinforce them.
  • Evaluate different types of pay-for-performance plans (e.g., individual incentives, group incentives, gainsharing, profit sharing, merit pay).
  • Recommend a specific pay-for-performance approach, explaining how it supports strategy and addresses risks.
  • Discuss the implementation challenges HR leaders should anticipate (e.g., employee reactions, measurement of performance, cost control).

Deliverable: A written analysis (2-3 pages, APA formatted) that includes:

  • Strategic analysis of organizational goals and challenges
  • Comparison of pay-for-performance plan options
  • Recommended approach with rationale
  • Anticipated challenges and strategies to address them

This case study encourages you to connect theory with practice by designing a pay-for-performance system that strengthens the link between organizational performance and compensation outcomes.
