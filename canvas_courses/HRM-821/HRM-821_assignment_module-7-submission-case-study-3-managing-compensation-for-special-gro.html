Scenario: An organization is expanding globally while also facing scrutiny over executive pay and pressure from a newly formed union. Leadership is concerned about balancing competitive pay for executives, fair treatment for employees, and consistency in international operations. HR has been asked to evaluate and recommend strategies that address these concerns while aligning with organizational strategy and stakeholder expectations.

Task:

  • Analyze the organization’s current challenges in executive compensation, union influence, and international pay practices.
  • Evaluate the risks and opportunities associated with each area.
  • Recommend compensation strategies that address fairness, competitiveness, and alignment with business goals.
  • Consider how HR can communicate and implement these strategies to minimize resistance and build trust across different groups.

Deliverable: A written analysis (2-3 pages, APA formatted) that includes:

  • Identification of key issues across executive, union, and international compensation
  • Application of course concepts and readings to analyze challenges
  • Recommendations for strategies to improve alignment and effectiveness
  • Consideration of implementation and communication strategies

This case study encourages you to think holistically about compensation strategy by addressing the needs of multiple stakeholders and navigating complex organizational environments.
