Overview

In this first module, we explore the foundations of strategic pay and the pay model, focusing on how compensation systems are designed, their objectives, and the ways pay influences employee behavior. You will begin building our learning community through the Meet Your Classmates Discussion and apply key concepts in Discussion #1, where you will connect the pay model to organizational strategies.

This module lays the groundwork for the course and prepares you to dive deeper into how compensation systems support performance and broader business outcomes in the weeks ahead.
