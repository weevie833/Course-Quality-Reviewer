Overview

In this module, we shift from the foundations of the pay model to the strategic choices organizations make when designing compensation systems. Drawing from Chapter 2 of Compensation (Gerhart, 14th ed., 2023), we will explore how total compensation strategies support HR objectives, align with organizational goals, and serve as sources of competitive advantage.

You will apply these ideas in Discussion #2, where you will consider how compensation decisions reflect broader business strategy, and in Case Study #1, which allows you to analyze a real scenario and connect theory to practice. Together, these activities highlight the importance of linking pay systems to strategy as the foundation for organizational success.

This module builds directly on Module 1 by moving from understanding the pay model to applying it in strategic contexts, preparing you for deeper engagement with compensation design and performance alignment in the weeks ahead.
