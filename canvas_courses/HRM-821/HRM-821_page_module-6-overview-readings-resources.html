Overview

In this module, we turn to the critical role of performance appraisals in shaping compensation decisions. Performance evaluations are often the foundation for determining merit pay, bonuses, promotions, and developmental opportunities. Yet, the effectiveness of these systems depends on how accurately job performance is defined, measured, and aligned with organizational goals.

Drawing from Chapter 11 of Compensation (Gerhart, 14th ed., 2023), we will explore common methods of performance appraisal, their strengths and limitations, and the strategies organizations use to reduce bias and improve fairness. We will also consider how performance appraisal systems connect directly to pay-for-performance and broader HR strategies.

This module emphasizes both the technical and strategic aspects of performance measurement, helping you evaluate how appraisal systems can motivate employees, reinforce desired behaviors, and support organizational effectiveness.
