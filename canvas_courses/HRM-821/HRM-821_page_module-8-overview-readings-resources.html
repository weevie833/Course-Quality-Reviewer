Overview

In this final module, we focus on the future of compensation and performance management. Organizations are increasingly challenged by shifting workforce expectations, rapid technological change, and global competition. Key trends—such as pay transparency, skills-based pay, AI-driven HR analytics, flexible benefits, and the integration of well-being into total rewards—are reshaping how companies attract, motivate, and retain talent.

We will also explore what defines effective performance practices in today’s environment, emphasizing continuous feedback, holistic evaluation of employee contributions, and the alignment of performance management with organizational strategy. Technology plays a central role, enabling more data-driven, personalized, and agile approaches to rewards and performance.

This module culminates with your Final Paper, where you will integrate what you have learned throughout the course to evaluate trends, analyze practices, and propose strategic recommendations for aligning rewards and performance management with organizational strategies.
