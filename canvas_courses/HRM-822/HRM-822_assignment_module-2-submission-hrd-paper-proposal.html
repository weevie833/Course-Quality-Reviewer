Please submit a 1–2 page proposal for your Final Integrative (Strategic HRD) Paper. 

Your proposal should include: 

  • Organization/Context: Briefly describe the organization (or workplace setting) you will use.
  • Problem or Opportunity: Clearly state the performance problem or development opportunity you plan to address.
  • Needs Assessment (concise): Summarize your organizational, task, and person analysis and what each reveals about the gap.
  • HRD Rationale: Explain why this gap is best addressed through HRD interventions.
  • Initial Direction: Identify the type of intervention you are considering (e.g., onboarding, coaching, skills training, leadership development) and the outcome you want to improve.
