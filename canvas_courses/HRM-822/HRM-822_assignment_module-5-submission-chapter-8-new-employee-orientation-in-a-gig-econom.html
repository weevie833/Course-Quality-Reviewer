Assignment Description: Orientation Design for Gig/Contract Workforce 

Please use course resources, analysis of company practices, and your own personal observations to respond to the scenario below. This activity is designed to help you apply HRD and onboarding concepts to a growing workforce trend: the gig economy and contract-based employment. 

A sizable number of individuals today work as independent contractors, freelance workers, consultants, or temporary workers. The term “gig economy” is often used to describe these short-term work arrangements and the HR challenges that come with them. 

Scenario:

Assume you have been asked to design an orientation program for up to 20 independent contractors who have been hired to work full-time in an office setting, each on a one-year contract. Although they are not traditional full-time employees, these contractors still need structured onboarding to support productivity, engagement, and alignment with organizational expectations. 

In your submission, address the following: 

  • Orientation Content: What specific topics and information should be included in the orientation program for independent contractors? (e.g., workplace expectations, policies, security, performance standards, culture, communication norms, collaboration processes, etc.)
  • Training Media: What training media and delivery methods would you use to support orientation and learning? Refer to Chapters 5 and 6 for guidance (e.g., in-person sessions, self-paced modules, job aids, microlearning, LMS resources, mentorship/shadowing, etc.).
  • Technology and Access Issues: What specific technology-related issues must be addressed to ensure contractors can succeed in orientation and perform effectively? Consider access control, cybersecurity, system training, communication platforms, confidentiality requirements, and IT support needs. 

Your response should demonstrate thoughtful application of course concepts and provide a realistic orientation plan that an HRD professional could implement in a workplace setting.
