Welcome to HRM 822: Talent Management and Development

HRM 822: Talent Management and Development examines how organizations strategically develop and manage talent to enhance employee capability, performance, and long-term competitive advantage. The course positions talent management and development as a core human resource function that aligns workforce capabilities with organizational strategy and business objectives. Students explore key processes including organizational and individual needs assessment, instructional design and delivery, coaching and mentoring, onboarding and acculturation, and experiential learning.

Emphasis is placed on ensuring the effective transfer of learning to the workplace and evaluating the impact of development initiatives on individual and organizational performance. Through critical thinking exercises, case analyses, discussions, and applied writing assignments, students integrate theory, research, and organizational experience to address real-world talent development challenges.

The course culminates in an integrative paper that synthesizes learning and examines the strategic role of talent management and development in driving organizational effectiveness, productivity, and sustainable performance.
