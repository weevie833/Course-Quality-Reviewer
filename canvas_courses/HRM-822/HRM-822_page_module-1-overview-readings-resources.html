Overview

Module 1 introduces the foundational concepts of Human Resource Development (HRD) and establishes the strategic context for talent management and development within organizations. Students examine the purpose and scope of HRD and distinguish it from human resource management (HRM), highlighting how the two functions complement one another while serving distinct roles.

This module emphasizes the alignment of HRD with organizational goals and business strategy, demonstrating how effective talent development initiatives contribute to employee capability, performance, and competitive advantage. Students also explore contemporary challenges facing HRD professionals, including technological change, evolving workforce expectations, globalization, and the increasing demand for continuous learning.

Through readings, discussions, and introductory activities, students begin connecting HRD theory to organizational practice while engaging with classmates to build a collaborative learning environment for the course.
