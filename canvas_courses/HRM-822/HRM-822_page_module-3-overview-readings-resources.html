Overview

Module 3 focuses on the systematic process of identifying and analyzing learning and performance needs within organizations. Students examine needs assessment as a critical foundation of effective talent development, emphasizing how strategic and organizational analysis ensures that learning initiatives are aligned with business goals and workforce requirements.

The module explores the three core components of needs assessment: organizational analysis, task analysis, and person analysis. Students learn how each level of analysis contributes to diagnosing performance gaps and determining whether training or other interventions are appropriate. In addition, the module introduces principles for preparing learning materials and designing workplace learning interventions that directly address identified needs.

Through readings, discussion, and an integrative case, students apply assessment and design concepts to real organizational scenarios, strengthening their ability to link talent development decisions to measurable performance outcomes.
