Overview

Module 4 focuses on the effective implementation, delivery, and evaluation of workplace learning interventions. Students examine a range of instructional delivery methods, including traditional, blended, and technology-enabled approaches, and evaluate how delivery choices influence learner engagement, knowledge transfer, and performance outcomes.

The module emphasizes the use of evaluation models to assess the effectiveness of talent development initiatives at multiple levels, including individual learning, behavior change, and organizational results. Special attention is given to the role of technology in supporting learning delivery, data collection, and performance measurement, as well as the use of return on investment (ROI) analysis to demonstrate the value of human resource development initiatives.

Through readings, discussion, and applied exercises, students develop the skills needed to implement learning interventions and evaluate their impact, enabling HR professionals to make data-driven decisions and communicate the strategic value of talent development to organizational stakeholders.
