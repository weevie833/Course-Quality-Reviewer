Overview

Module 5 examines onboarding and employee socialization as critical components of talent management and long-term employee retention. Students explore how structured onboarding processes, realistic job previews, and intentional socialization practices support new hires’ adjustment, engagement, and performance within an organization.

The module emphasizes the strategic role of onboarding in shaping employee expectations, reducing early turnover, and fostering organizational commitment. Students analyze how realistic job previews contribute to informed employment decisions and how socialization processes influence retention, job satisfaction, and cultural alignment.

Through readings, discussion, and applied exercises, students evaluate onboarding and socialization practices and consider how organizations can design effective orientation and integration strategies that support both employee success and organizational outcomes.
