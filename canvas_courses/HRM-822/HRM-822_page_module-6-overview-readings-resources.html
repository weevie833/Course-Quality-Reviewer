Overview

Module 6 focuses on coaching and career development as essential components of talent management and employee growth. Students examine the effectiveness of coaching as a developmental tool, including how coaching conversations support performance improvement, learning, and employee engagement. The module explores the structure and purpose of effective coaching discussions and the role of managers and HR professionals in facilitating development-focused dialogue.

In addition, students analyze stages of life and career development and how individual career needs evolve over time. The module emphasizes the process of career management, highlighting shared responsibilities between employees and organizations in planning, development, and long-term career success.

Through readings, discussion, and applied exercises, students reflect on coaching and career development practices and consider how organizations can design systems that support continuous learning, career alignment, and workforce sustainability.
