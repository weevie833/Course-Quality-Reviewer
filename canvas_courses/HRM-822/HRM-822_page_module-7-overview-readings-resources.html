Overview

Module 7 explores special topics in talent management and development with an emphasis on the financial and organizational impact of HRD initiatives. Students examine the linkages between talent management practices, employee capability, and organizational performance, drawing on evidence from industry research and real-world examples.

The module highlights how investments in talent development influence key outcomes such as productivity, retention, engagement, and financial performance. Students analyze how HR professionals can use data, metrics, and industry evidence to demonstrate the value of talent management and development to organizational stakeholders.

Through readings and discussion, students synthesize course concepts and reflect on how talent development functions as a strategic driver of organizational effectiveness and competitive advantage.
