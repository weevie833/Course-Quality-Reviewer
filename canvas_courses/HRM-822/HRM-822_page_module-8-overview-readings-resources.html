Overview

Module 8 serves as the culminating module of HRM 822 and focuses on the role of Human Resource Development in organizational development (OD) and planned change. Students examine change process theory and models of planned change, emphasizing how HRD supports organizations in navigating transformation, improving performance, and sustaining competitive advantage.

The module highlights HRD’s role in diagnosing organizational challenges, designing development interventions, and supporting individuals and groups through change. Students consider how learning, coaching, and talent development initiatives contribute to successful change efforts and long-term organizational effectiveness.

This module also supports completion of the Final Strategic HRD Paper, requiring students to integrate course concepts and apply them to strategic HRD challenges in organizational contexts.
