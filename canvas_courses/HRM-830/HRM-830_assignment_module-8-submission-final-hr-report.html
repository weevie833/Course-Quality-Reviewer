Assignment: Team Simulation Reflection (Managing the HR Function)

Purpose

This reflection assignment evaluates your team’s HR decisions in the simulation, focusing on what strategies worked, where HR investments paid off, what could have been improved, and how outcomes connect to course readings. Your goal is to demonstrate strategic HR thinking using data from simulation reports.

Team Simulation Reflection Report (Written)

  • Length: 5–6 pages (APA 7)
  • Must use: simulation reports/metrics + course readings

Required Sections

  1. Executive Summary: Overall results and key takeaways.
  2. HR Strategy Overview: What approach your team followed (staffing, pay, training, performance, retention) and why.
  3. Effective HR Strategies (Evidence-Based): Identify at least 2–3 strategies that worked well. For each:
    1. What you did
    2. Evidence it worked (metrics/trends)
    3. Link to readings
  4. HR Investments That Paid Off: Where you allocated resources well (e.g., compensation, training, hiring) and what outcomes improved.
  5. What You Would Do Differently: Provide at least 2–3 improvements, supported by data and course concepts.
  6. Connection to Course Readings (Required): Connect results to at least 4 readings and key course frameworks (strategic alignment, HR analytics, HPWS, etc.).
  7. Team Process Reflection: How your team coordinated, made decisions, and what collaboration lessons were learned.
