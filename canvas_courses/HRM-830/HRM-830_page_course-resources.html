Assignments

Discussions: The specialty of Human Resources is rich with relevant topics! The discussions are in the course to here to interpret, evaluate, and compare the various aspects of the HR field today. You will be expected to post an initial response (approximately 400 words) to the discussion prompt in each module with support for the arguments you make. Integration of learning resources is required.

Case Studies: Using case studies from the text (n=4), you will integrate course concepts to analyze the respective cases. HR Paper will be to research the use of HR metrics and relate it to your organization and explain the relevance and how a process can be developed to capture data and then to use to analyze and make decisions.

Business Applications: These are activities using statistical tools and HR concepts to make business decisions (n=3).

Simulation/Predictive HR Analytics: You will use data and HR practices to analyze HR strategies to understand, interpret, and predict behaviors such as employee turnover, performance, reliability and validity of selection methods. We will use actual data, Excel or other Statistical tools to do data analysis to practice using the data and developing expertise with basic and advanced HR analytic techniques.
