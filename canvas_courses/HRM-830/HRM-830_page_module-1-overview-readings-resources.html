Overview

Module 1 introduces students to the core concepts of HR analytics and their growing importance in shaping effective people strategy and organizational decision making. The module explores what HR analytics is, how it differs from traditional HR reporting, and why organizations increasingly rely on workforce data to inform strategic talent decisions. Students examine the connection between HR analytics and people strategy, gaining insight into how data-driven approaches support alignment between human capital initiatives, business objectives, and organizational performance.

A central focus of the module is evidence-based decision making, emphasizing the integration of organizational data, research evidence, professional expertise, and stakeholder perspectives. Through Chapter 1: Understanding HR Analytics and structured discussions, students consider how evidence-based HR improves decision quality, reduces bias, and enhances HR’s credibility as a strategic partner. The Meet Your Classmates Discussion and Discussion #1encourage students to reflect on their professional experiences and critically assess how analytics can be applied ethically and strategically in real-world HR contexts.
