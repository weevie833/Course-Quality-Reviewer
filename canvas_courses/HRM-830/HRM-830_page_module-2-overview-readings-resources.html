Overview

Module 2 focuses on the foundational data infrastructure that supports HR analytics. Students explore key HR information sources, including HRIS data, payroll systems, performance management platforms, engagement surveys, and external labor market data. Emphasis is placed on understanding data quality, integration, and limitations, as well as how different data sources support various HR and strategic decisions.

Students are introduced to commonly used analytical tools, including SPSS, Excel, and other HR analytics platforms, and examine how these tools are applied to manage and analyze HR data. The module also introduces the concept of Big Data in HR, highlighting opportunities and challenges associated with large, complex datasets. Through Discussion #2and a data analysis exercise, students gain hands-on exposure to working with HR data and begin developing analytical confidence.
