Overview

Module 3 builds analytical capability by focusing on interpreting HR data and linking insights to organizational strategy. Students examine key HR analytic metrics such as turnover rates, absenteeism, productivity, engagement, and workforce demographics, with an emphasis on understanding their relevance to business outcomes and strategic goals.

Through Chapter 3 and applied discussions, students learn how to move beyond descriptive metrics to draw meaningful conclusions that inform leadership decisions. The HR Metrics Paper challenges students to critically evaluate selected metrics and explain how they support or hinder strategic objectives. Discussion #3 reinforces analytical thinking and encourages application to real organizational contexts.
