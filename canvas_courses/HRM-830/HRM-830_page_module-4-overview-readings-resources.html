Overview

Module 4 introduces predictive analytics through the examination of employee turnover and its organizational impact. Students explore the causes and patterns of turnover, learning how predictive models can identify at-risk employees and inform proactive retention strategies.

A key focus of the module is the cost of employee turnover, including direct, indirect, and opportunity costs. Using Chapter 6 and real-world case studies, students assess how turnover affects organizational performance and financial outcomes. Discussion #4 and Case Study #1 provide opportunities to apply predictive thinking to retention challenges faced by organizations.

Readings
