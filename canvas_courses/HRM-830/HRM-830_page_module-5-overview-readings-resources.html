Overview

Module 5 focuses on the use of analytics to predict employee performance and support performance management decisions. Students examine the factors that influence performance outcomes and learn how data can be used to identify high performers, development needs, and performance risks.

The module introduces linear regression as a foundational statistical method for predicting performance outcomes. Through Chapter 7 and applied case analysis, students gain experience interpreting predictive models and understanding their limitations. Discussion #5 and Case Study #2 emphasize ethical use, accuracy, and strategic application of performance analytics.
