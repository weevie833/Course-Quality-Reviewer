Overview

Module 6 explores how analytics can improve recruitment and selection decisions while reducing bias and enhancing fairness. Students examine how data supports workforce planning, candidate screening, and selection decisions aligned with organizational needs.

Key topics include bias in selection, reliability in employee selection, and the use of analytics to evaluate hiring tools and processes. Through Chapter 8, Discussion #6, and Case Study #3, students analyze how evidence-based hiring improves quality of hire, diversity outcomes, and legal defensibility.
