Overview

Module 7 emphasizes the translation of HR analytics into business value. Students explore real-world business applications and scenarios where HR analytics informs strategic planning, investment decisions, and organizational change initiatives.

Using Chapter 10, students learn how to develop scenario modeling and business cases that communicate HR insights to senior leadership. Discussion #7 and applied activities focus on storytelling with data, aligning analytics with executive priorities, and demonstrating HR’s strategic impact.

Readings

  • Bottesch, S., Schwenke, C., Förster, M., & Klier, M. (2025). Driving business value through people analytics: Literature review and research agenda from an information systems perspective. Electronic Markets, 35(1), Article 106.
