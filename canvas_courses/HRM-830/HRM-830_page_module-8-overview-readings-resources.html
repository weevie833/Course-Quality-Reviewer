Overview

The final module synthesizes course concepts while examining the ethical and strategic implications of HR analytics. Students explore the use of the Balanced Scorecard, ethical considerations in employee data use, and the positioning of HR analytics as a scientific and evidence-based discipline.

Through Chapter 12 and Discussion #8, students reflect on the evolving role of HR analytics in organizations and consider future trends, including AI, automation, and advanced predictive modeling. The course culminates in the Final Predictive HR Analytics Project, where students apply analytical tools and evidence-based decision making to a comprehensive HR problem.
