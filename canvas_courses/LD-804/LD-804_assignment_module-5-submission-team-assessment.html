You have inherited a large team to complete a project. The team is comprised of current employees as well as other stakeholders.

  • What steps will you need to take as a new leader to assess the current strengths of the team (their knowledge and skills)?
  • Determine what they will need to be successful (do they need training, equipment, resources etc. and how will they communicate)?
  • How you will recognize each person’s influence on the team?
  • How you will facilitate and monitor the team charter?
  • Determine critical obstacles the team may encounter and must overcome?

Write a 3-4-page assessment, in APA format, of this process by clearly identifying the actions, steps and assessments you will take to create a high-performing team.
