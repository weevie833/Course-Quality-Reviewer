Your best friend has assumed a promotion to a leadership role.  They have asked you for advice as to how to lead an existing team. In this assignment, you will share the advice you would give to them.

🖐️To prepare for this assignment, interview two people to gather and analyze data for your presentation based on the knowledge you've gained in this class.

In this video discussion, you are going to create and share a five minute video that provides advice you would give your best friend based on your research. Include:

  • A summary of the questions you asked of your interview subjects.
  • A statistical analysis of your collected data  (the two subjects, the text, and your personal experience).
  • An explanation explaining why you would give them this advice based on an analysis of your research.

In all cases, use the reading(s) as your basis for personal observation.

  • Include the citation for the article by adding a PPT slide (or other visual item) to your video, or you can add your citation in text form directly in your post.
  • In your responses, please ask questions and challenge assumptions. Responses can be text or video based.
