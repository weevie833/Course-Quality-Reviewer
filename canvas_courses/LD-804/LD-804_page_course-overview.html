Successfully participating in a team is important in almost every aspect of our lives. We may lead a team, work on a team, volunteer, and we may include our definition of family to include team. In this course, we will explore many elements of teams. We will begin with learning our individual characteristics to understand our communication style. We will explore different types of teams and team roles.

This course covers a lot of facets of teams, formation, motivation,  etc... There is a team simulation in this course which will allow you to practice what you've learned in a safe environment.

In each of the courses in the program it will be important that you are well acquainted with the Graduate Resources and Orientation. This page provides numerous resources and important information to help you on your journey here at CPS Online.

The Course Resources page in each of your courses will also be essential to your success in this program. It provides resources, tutorials, and tips needed to complete many assignments. Should you ever be looking for resources, this is where you will locate them.
