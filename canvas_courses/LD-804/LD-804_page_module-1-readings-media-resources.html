Overview

Welcome to Module 1!

In this module, we will review fundamental leadership and team-building and developing concepts.  Hopefully, some of you have had an opportunity to lead or be a member of a successful team.  If not, I am sure all of you can think of a team you identify as successful. 

It may not be in their every day results and in more of a certain "thing' that they exude, you cannot articulate what the 'thing' is but just realize that they have it.  They are overall successful and often exceed expectations of their peers and leadership.

Often times, they are not the experts in their field or even the best at what they do, however, together they become the best team.  Is it by chance?  Is it the leader or is it the team members or both?  We begin to explore these questions within the reading this week and understand ourselves in order to begin mapping our path to be successful team leaders or members!

There is a communication/personality test and reflection paper due this week. In the past, many students have shared this activity with others they work with and have found it very informative. If you have the ability to do so, I challenge you to share and use the experience as part of your reflection at the end of this course. Other students have used this tool as part of their capstone.  It is a powerful assessment.

Remember, it is just as important to measure the team's success as it is to measure an individual's success. Consider how you measure a team's success?
