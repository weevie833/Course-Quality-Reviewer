Overview

Welcome to Module 3!

What is leadership? We often are unable to define leadership but we know a good leader when we see one. Anyone can become a leader at any level of the organization. There are appointed leaders and there are informal leaders. One could argue that followership is an important aspect of leadership. As quoted in the article below "Want to be a good leader? Learn to follow," (Duran-Staton & Masson, 2021).

This week we will discuss many leadership concepts, depending upon which program you're in, this is not new. Consider this as a refresher and a chance to revisit your thoughts on leadership and followership as you have progressed through the program.
