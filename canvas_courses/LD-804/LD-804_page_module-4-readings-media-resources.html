Welcome to Module 4!

In this module, we will discuss succession planning which is vital to team leadership but often overlooked. Succession planning doesn't only include filling empty spots on your team but anticipating future team needs.

Work Life Balance is a phrase we often hear or say. What does it mean and is it possible? Since 2020, many have had to reconsider the definition of balance. This module will explore work life balance, what it means to us, our workplace, and our team.
