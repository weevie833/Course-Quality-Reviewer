Overview

Welcome to Module 5!

We will highlight the benefits of working with a diverse group of people. Diversity helps an organization to become more effective and productive. Feel free to share your experiences working with diverse groups. Even if you work in a team where everyone looks the same, there are other types of diversity to consider and honor.

Strongly emotionally intelligent (EI) people have traits and skills that allow them to work better and more efficiently. Emotional intelligence is not set; like IQ, people can develop their emotional intelligence over time.  The EI chapter is written by a former student as the literature review portion of her capstone. Jamiee provides tips to improve your emotional intelligence; the SHRM article also offers tips to improve your EI.
