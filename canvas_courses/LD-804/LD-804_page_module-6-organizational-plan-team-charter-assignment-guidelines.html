You have volunteered to chair a committee responsible for planning your high school (or college) class reunion. It has been several years since you have seen your classmates (whose culture traditionally is rife with cliques, ingroups, and outgroups) and it is important that you create a reunion that everyone will enjoy. After the initial meeting with your committee, you realize there is more diversity than you remembered, and the culture does not appear to have changed too much since your school days.  You will need to employ the leadership skills you have learned to have a successful event, which begins with your role as committee chair. You know that to best utilize the talents of all your committee members is to first create an effective team. 

For this assignment, you will create a Team Charter for your class reunion committee that includes: 

  • a thorough development of a team charter that considers potential challenges of your committee. Be sure to use the items from the team charter template to outline/begin your work. (Chapter 3, Defining Team Success and Team Beginnings) 
  • an explanation of how the committee plans to assess its goals, provide feedback, and address any performance gaps. (Chapter 4, Setting Team Goals and Providing Team Feedback) 
  • an explanation of how the committee composition affects all roles and responsibilities. (Chapter 4, Setting Team Goals and Providing Team Feedback) 
  • the development of at least one team building exercise that addresses an issue that your committee may have. If you are using one of the examples provided, please make it your own. (Chapter 4, Setting Team Goals and Providing Team Feedback) 
  • the development of ground rules and explanation of the reasoning behind these choices. (Chapter 5, The Psychology of Groups) 
  • an explanation of potential barriers as well as coping strategies should complications arise. Make sure to include the reasoning behind each of these explanations. (Chapter 5, The Psychology of Groups)
  • language that promotes the use of diversity in leadership. (Chapter 6, Diversity)  

Assignment specifics: 

  • This written assignment should be 5-7 pages in APA style. 
  • This written assignment is the development of a thorough Team Charter for your event. You DO NOT need to develop an organizational plan for the class reunion, it is just meant to provide you with a purpose.
