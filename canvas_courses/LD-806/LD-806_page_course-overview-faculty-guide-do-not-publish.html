Course Overview

Fundraising is the act of soliciting contributions or pledges. Development refers to nurturing and building relationships between donors/philanthropists and organizations over time. This course differentiates fundraising, grant writing, and development strategies in organizations. It reviews ethics and transparency within fundraising, technology to facilitate resource development, social and business trends, and organizational adaptation. The course culminates in completing a final project, an Open Educational Resource (OER) submission focused on a specific fundraising aspect. This OER is designed for nonprofit executive leadership, boards, or entry-level staff who may have limited experience with—or may not fully understand—the distinctions between fundraising and development.
