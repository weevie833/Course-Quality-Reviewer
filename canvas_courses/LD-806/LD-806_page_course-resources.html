Student Self-Assessment 

For this assessment, you will assess and grade your demonstration of understanding of the subject matter through your discussion posts. The rubric for this assessment provides you with clear guidance on how to assess yourself. For each criterion, provide a couple sentences worth of rationale to justify your grade, which is as much for you as it is for me, the instructor.

OER Submission Self-Assessment

For this assessment, you will assess and grade the overall quality of your class and/or group’s contribution to the OER. The rubric for this assessment provides you with clear guidance on how to assess the quality. Provide a couple sentences worth of rationale to justify your grade, which is as much for you as it is for me, the instructor.

Self and Classmate OER Contribution Assessment

For this assessment, you will assess and grade your individual contribution to the OER submission. Were you invested? Did you work well in a team environment? For this assignment, you will not only assess your own effort and contribution, but that of your classmates. The rubric for this assessment provides you with clear guidance on how to assess the quality. Provide a couple sentences worth of rationale to justify your grade, which is as much for you as it is for me, the instructor.

Creating an Infographic

  • SmartArt is a simple way to create an Infographic using Word or PPT. 
  • Visme is a free online tool for making creative Infographics. (Downloading your creation is not free in Visme, however, you can share your infographic by getting a share link. When you have completed your infographic, select 'Share' in the top right corner, select 'Share Privately', make sure it reads 'Anyone with the link', and copy the link at the bottom of the window. Copy link into Infographic discussion or submission.)
  • Canva. is a free online tool for making creative Infographics.

You do not need to use the suggestions above. These are just some options & tools that we have used in the past. With Visme & Canva you will need to 'sign up' by using an email. If you have a junk email address, that may be best to use.
