Module 1: Faculty Guide--do not publish

In this module, students explore the core principles of fundraising and development, focusing on building long-term donor relationships to support philanthropic causes. Through discussions and activities, students learn how to collaborate effectively within an organization to gather insights and create compelling cases for donors. This module provides the foundation for understanding the strategic role of fundraising in organizational sustainability, which sets the stage for their final assignment.

 

Module Objectives

By the end of this module, students should be able to:

  • Understand the role of fundraising in supporting philanthropic causes.
  • Recognize the importance of development in building long-term donor relationships.
  • Reflect on and share their personal experiences with fundraising.

Conclusion

This first module introduces students to the foundational concepts of fundraising and development. By reflecting on their experiences and motivations, students understand how effective fundraising supports philanthropic causes. The activities in this module are designed to lay a strong foundation for future assignments, including the final project, which will be completed later in the course.
