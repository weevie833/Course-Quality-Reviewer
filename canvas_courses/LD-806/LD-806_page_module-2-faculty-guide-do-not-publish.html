In Module 2, students learn how to collect and analyze data on current and prospective donors, focusing on demographics, giving history, relationships, and philanthropic interests. They will explore how to rank prospects by connection to the organization and financial capacity, creating an action plan to guide fundraising and development strategies for campaigns.

Module Objectives

By the end of this module, students should be able to:

  • Research donor demographics, giving behaviors, and philanthropic interests.
  • Analyze data to identify potential donor prospects.
  • Rank prospects based on connection to the organization and financial capacity.
  • Develop an action plan that outlines fundraising and development strategies for specific projects or campaigns.
  • Begin to work on OER Submission

Conclusion

This module is designed to equip students with essential skills for understanding and engaging with current and prospective donors. By focusing on data collection and analysis, students learn to identify key donor attributes and rank prospects effectively. Students are also placed in groups in preparation for their final project.
