Prospective Donor and Donor Research and Database Management 

To develop an understanding of current and prospective donors, it is necessary to collect and analyze data to determine best fit for particular projects and fundraising campaigns. Some attributes of potential donors into which research should be conducted include demographics, past giving behaviors, existent relationships, and philanthropic interests.

Based on this research, which includes a variety of methods, project and campaign prospects can (and should) be ranked according to linkage to organization, financial means (if known) and philanthropic interests (if known). These rankings should be codified into a list that serves as the basis for an action plan that lays out the activities that will make up the fundraising and development component of the project and/or campaign.
