Overview

Before launching any campaign, including conducting any prospect donor research, it is crucial to understand what donors value. Corporations, for instance, generally view philanthropy differently than a foundation. There are also differences among different individual donor groups. For example, women view philanthropy differently than men. Religion, ethnicity, age, and socioeconomic status also influence a donor’s view on philanthropy. 

In this module, we will look at corporate giving, foundation fundraising, gender and philanthropy, LGBTQIA+ giving, faith and giving, ethnic diversity and giving, generational differences in giving, and high net worth donors.
