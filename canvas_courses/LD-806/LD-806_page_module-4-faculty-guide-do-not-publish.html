In this module, students learn to create actionable plans to engage prospective donors after identifying and ranking them. The focus is on building trust and long-term commitment through fundraising and communication strategies, including major gifts and capital campaigns.  Students also continue to work on their OER final submission.

Module Objectives

By the end of this module, students should be able to:

  • Develop actionable plans for engaging and cultivating relationships with prospective donors.
  • Analyze various fundraising structures, including major gifts, capital campaigns, and planned giving.
  • Create effective communication plans to support fundraising efforts.
  • Research and identify reasons for the failure of fundraising events or campaigns, outlining their causes and implications.
  • Articulate lessons learned from their research to advise organizations on avoiding common pitfalls in fundraising.

Conclusion

In this module, students explore key strategies for engaging donors, structuring fundraising efforts, and creating communication plans. By understanding common fundraising pitfalls and learning from past failures, students are better equipped to develop successful, long-term donor relationships. The knowledge gained in this module will directly support students’ ability to contribute meaningfully to future fundraising initiatives.
