Overview

Upon identification, validation and ranking of prospective donors, a plan of action should be affected in close consultation with key organizational stakeholders. The objective behind such a plan is to engage prospects, initiate, cultivate and/or deepen relationships with them, and solicit their support. Anticipated outcomes from systematic cultivation and stewardship plans include a high degree of trust in the organization as well as long-term commitment to its mission, values, and funding priorities.

In this week’s module, we look at several ways you can structure your fundraising, which include fundraising programs, major gifts, capital campaigns, and planned giving. It all starts, however, with a plan, and some examples of communication plans are provided for you as a reference.

Additionally, you should also begin to work on assembling your content for the class’s deliverable of an OER. Based on the class proposal for the OER, it is conceivable that future posts in each discussion module may change to better reflect the structure of the OER. In other words, your work each week should directly connect with the OER and not necessarily represent work for just work’s sake.
