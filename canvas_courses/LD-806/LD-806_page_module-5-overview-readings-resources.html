Overview

In this week’s module, you will do a deep dive into the science (but more art!) of solicitation. All the strategizing and planning in the world will amount to very little, if you do not approach the actual ‘ask’ for money in the proper way. These ways to ask include events, direct mail, grant writing, corporate solicitation, digital (eblasts and social media), traditional PR. The proposed mix of fundraising      and development activities will rest to a large degree on assumptions made from the research and the nature of the project or campaign for which funds must be raised. 

In this week’s module, you will also have the opportunity to deepen your understanding of fundraising and development by watching a 30-minute recorded interview of a fundraising and development professional on the following topics:

  1. General approaches to fundraising and development, including philosophy and processes.
  2. How to structure fundraising.
  3. Importance of stewardship and what makes a good ‘ask.’
