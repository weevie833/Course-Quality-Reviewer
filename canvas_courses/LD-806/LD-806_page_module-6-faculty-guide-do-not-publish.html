In this module, students explore the ethical and legal considerations essential to effective fundraising and development. Topics include donor confidentiality, appropriate use of funds, and transparency in reporting outcomes to stakeholders. Students also learn the importance of ethical practices in handling donations, which include financial management, donor record maintenance, and audit trails. Students also turn in a working draft of their OER.

Module Objectives

By the end of this module, students should be able to:

  • Understand the legal and ethical considerations governing fundraising and development.
  • Analyze the importance of donor confidentiality and the appropriate use of funds.
  • Evaluate the significance of transparency in reporting outcomes to donors and stakeholders.
  • Explain the role of ethical practices in gift accounting, donor record maintenance, and financial management.
  • Demonstrate how stewardship practices ensure donor trust and organizational accountability.

Conclusion

This module emphasizes the importance of ethical practices and transparency in fundraising and development. By understanding the legal and moral responsibilities, students gain insights into maintaining donor trust, properly managing gifts, and exercising accountability. This module helps students build foundational knowledge regarding responsible and effective stewardship.
