In this module, students explore marketing fundamentals, key marketing plan components, and how marketing facilitates value exchange. While building a complete marketing plan is beyond the course’s scope, understanding essential concepts prepares students to apply them to future fundraising endeavors.

Module Objectives

By the end of this module, students should be able to:

  • Understand the basic principles of marketing and their relevance to fundraising.
  • Identify critical components of a marketing plan and how they apply to nonprofit organizations.
  • Analyze the role of marketing in creating value exchanges between organizations and donors.
  • Reflect on how marketing concepts can be integrated into their future fundraising efforts.
  • Apply marketing knowledge to enhance their professional development in fundraising contexts.

Conclusion

This module provides students with a comprehensive understanding of marketing principles and their role in fundraising. By exploring the components of a marketing plan and the value exchanges it facilitates, students can apply these concepts in their future endeavors.
