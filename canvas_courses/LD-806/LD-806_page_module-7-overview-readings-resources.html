Overview

This week’s module concludes your reading of the text, and this subject has been saved for last, because a lot of what you have learned so far directly relates to ‘marketing.’ Regardless of the context, marketing can be viewed as a way to make possible an exchange of items of value. 

In this week’s module, you will learn some of the nuts and bolts of marketing, what constitutes a plan, and how to build one. While the ability to build an actual plan falls outside the purview of this course, it is crucial you develop an understanding of general marketing principles, which you can further develop in your own career in relation to any fundraising endeavor in which you may participate.
