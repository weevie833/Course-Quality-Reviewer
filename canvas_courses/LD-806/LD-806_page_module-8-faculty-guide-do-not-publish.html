In this final module, students reflect on their experiences with open pedagogy. They will engage in self-assessment, focusing on the significance of storytelling and its role in engaging stakeholders and conveying the mission of their initiatives.

Module Objectives

By the end of this module, students should be able to:

  • Reflect on their experiences with open educational resources and open pedagogy.
  • Assess their personal growth and learning throughout the course.
  • Analyze the importance of storytelling in fundraising and development strategies.

Conclusion

In this module, students reflect on their journey through the OER and open pedagogy experience, assessing their growth and understanding of key concepts. Students will also reflect on the role of storytelling in fundraising and strategic planning.
