Overview

In this last module, you will reflect on the open educational resource/open pedagogy experience and provide a few different assessments about your experience. 

While working on your self-assessment, however, consider the importance of what it means to tell a good story. At the heart of what you have learned this semester is the need to tell a good story. How might a consideration of storytelling influence your fundraising or development strategy?
