Using Exercise 2-1 “Identifying the Need for Change” in Planning and Managing Changing, as a guide, consider issues in your workplace.

  • Using the lens of marketplace factors, environmental factors, skills, technological or processes, and cultural factors. Analyze your organization.
  • Identify two factors where a change response is required.
  • After assessing the organization, describe the changes you've identified and describe solutions for the organization.
  • Develop a PowerPoint presentation with at least 6 slides (Not including the title and reference slide) to illustrate the factors and solutions.
  • Record yourself presenting your PowerPoint presentation directly within your presentation.  You should average about 1-2 minutes per slide when presenting. Reading directly from the slide without additional details is not acceptable. Your presentation video submission should be no more than 12 minutes long.
