Almost all projects introduce some level of change. It is critical to manage the change carefully in order to minimize the disruption to the organization and/or associated stakeholders. In this assignment you will develop a detailed change management plan for your capstone project or case study incorporating the following elements:

  • Vision for the change/alignment to the strategic goals of the organization.
  • How you will share that vision/objectives/outcomes with the organization/stakeholders (socialize the change initiative)
  • Expected impact of change on the organization/stakeholders (Note: if your capstone project or case study is a smaller, less complex initiative, consider the impact on change for a larger initiative.)
  • Expected impact of change on the individuals within or external to the organization, or the scope of the project (who will be champions and who will be your resisters)
  • How stakeholders will be engaged in the change initiative (detail how you will engage senior leaders separate from individual contributors and champions separate from resisters)
  • Roles and responsibilities around change management as it applies to its scope
  • Timeline for implementing the change
