Communication is a key success factor of any project. It is imperative that when planning for a project there exists a comprehensive communication plan which will address the needs of the various stakeholders to be involved.

For this assignment you will develop a detailed communication plan for your capstone project or case study incorporating the following elements:

  • Communication objective(s)
    • Stakeholder information requirements (differentiate internal and external)
    • Overall communication roles and responsibilities
    • Socialization plan (how will the stakeholders be engaged early on for your capstone project)
    • Communication modes and methods to be used and specifically for what purposes
    • Requirements for distributing information to stakeholder
  • Communication component
    • Estimated due dates, i.e., the distribution and collection of survey information
    • Audience to whom communicating
    • Responsible parties
    • Distribution method
  • Requirements for gathering information and reporting
    • Information input required
    • Responsible party/parties for collecting and reporting
    • Responsible party/parties for submitting information
    • Estimated due dates
  • Overall guidelines for gathering and distributing information from and to stakeholders
  • How often communications will be reviewed and revised/updated
