Introduce yourself to our course community!

Begin by telling us the basics:

  • What is your preferred name?
  • What program of study are you currently in?
  • How how far along are you in your program of study? 

Then tell us something that helps us get to know you a little better:

  • What is your favorite meal to cook?
  • If you were given one hour of free time, how would you spend it? 
  • What is your favorite place to study OR what is your favorite study tip? 

Make sure to engage with your new classmates. Building a community right from the beginning of this short 8 week course sure be helpful as we move through this material!
