Think about a recent change in the workplace that directly impacted you.

  • Detail how you first reacted to this change
  • Consider if your thoughts were positive or negative
  • If your thoughts were positive, how did this change help you at work?
  • If your thoughts were negative, how did you express your thoughts and feelings at work? After some time, were you able to accept the change and see its purpose and benefit?
  • In your responses to your classmates, think about how their responses may or may not have resonated with your actual work experiences.
