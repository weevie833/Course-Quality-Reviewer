Welcome to Change Management and Communication!

The ability for leaders, managers, and project managers - and frankly anyone - to champion and embrace change, and help others do so, is essential to moving forward change initiatives. Organizational change is necessary for any organization that wants to continue to grow and be relevant, compete effectively, and attract and maintain top talent. What worked for an organization in its early years will not work later on. 

The most successful leaders in the organization know how to lead the organization through change - regardless of the complexity of that change. They know how to engage others in supporting and championing that change and see the positive side of change. Change is all about opportunity! And in the best organizations - change is looked at as something to be pursued, not something that happens to you. 

In this course we'll discuss effective communication around change initiatives, enabling you to engage others around you in change so that the change initiative - regardless of what it is - can be successful. We'll also focus on how to assess and evaluate change, set a vision for change and manage change.
