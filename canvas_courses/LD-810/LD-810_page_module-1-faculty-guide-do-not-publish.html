This module is designed to facilitate introductions among students and engage them in an initial exploration of the role of a change leader through a video assignment. The activities will help students understand the key attributes and responsibilities of a change leader, setting the stage for deeper discussions in subsequent modules.

Learning Objectives

  • Facilitate student introductions to build a collaborative learning environment.
  • Understand the fundamental role of a change leader.
  • Analyze the attributes and responsibilities of effective change leaders.

Module Structure

  1. Student Introductions
  2. Video Assignment: The Role of a Change Leader

This introductory module is crucial for building a foundation in change management. By getting to know each other and exploring the role of a change leader, students will be better prepared for the more complex topics that will follow. Ensure that the activities are engaging and interactive to maximize student participation and learning.
