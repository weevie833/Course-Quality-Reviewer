In this module, students will explore the critical role of a change manager within an organization. They will identify and analyze change issues in their workplace, create a PowerPoint deck, and prepare a presentation to discuss the change. In a discussion forum, they will reflect on their reactions to workplace changes.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students should be able to:

  1. Understand the responsibilities and skills required of a change manager.
  2. Identify and analyze a specific change issue within their workplace.
  3. Develop a comprehensive PowerPoint presentation addressing the change issue.
  4. Effectively communicate their analysis and proposed solutions through a presentation.
  5. Reflect on and articulate their personal reactions to changes in their workplace.
