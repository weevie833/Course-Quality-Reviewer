In this module, students will explore the reasons why people resist change and the strategies to manage this resistance effectively. They will create an outline for a change management plan they will develop in detail later in the course. Additionally, students will identify and focus on a change management skill they wish to improve.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will be able to:

  1. Identify common reasons why people resist change.
  2. Analyze different strategies to manage resistance to change.
  3. Create an outline for a comprehensive change management plan.
  4. Reflect on a change management skill they need to improve and develop a plan to enhance it.

Module Components

  1. Introduction to Resistance to Change
  2. Strategies to Manage Resistance
  3. Creating an Outline for a Change Management Plan
  4. Focusing on a Change Management Skill

This module aims to equip students with a deep understanding of why people resist change and how to manage it effectively. By the end of the module, students will have a solid foundation to build a comprehensive change management plan and improve critical change management skills, preparing them for future challenges in organizational change management.
