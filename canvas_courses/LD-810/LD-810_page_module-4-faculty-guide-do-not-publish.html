This module aims to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of change management principles and practices. Students will create a change management plan for an organizational project, integrating theoretical knowledge with practical application.

Module Objectives

  1. Understand the key concepts and principles of change management.
  2. Analyze the impact of change on organizations and individuals.
  3. Develop a comprehensive change management plan.
  4. Apply change management strategies to real-world scenarios.
  5. Reflect on the effectiveness of change management practices.

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this module, students will be able to:

  • Articulate the importance of change management in organizational success.
  • Identify and assess the drivers and resistors of change.
  • Design a change management plan that includes communication strategies, stakeholder engagement, and risk mitigation.
  • Evaluate the potential outcomes of change initiatives and adjust plans accordingly.
