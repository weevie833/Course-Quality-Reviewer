In this module, students will engage in an interactive change management simulation. The simulation will provide a practical experience where students can apply change management theories and strategies in a controlled environment. Following the simulation, students will reflect on their experiences in a discussion forum, analyze their decision-making processes, and evaluate the outcomes.

This module provides a hands-on experience that allows students to apply theoretical knowledge in a practical setting. Encourage students to take this opportunity seriously and to reflect deeply on their learning, as it will significantly enhance their understanding of change management.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will be able to:

  1. Apply change management theories and principles in a simulated environment.
  2. Analyze the outcomes of change management strategies.
  3. Reflect on their decision-making processes and outcomes to improve future change management practices.
  4. Collaborate with peers to discuss strategies and outcomes.

Note: Students must purchase the course packet for the simulation. Faculty are required to create an account to access and manage the simulation.
