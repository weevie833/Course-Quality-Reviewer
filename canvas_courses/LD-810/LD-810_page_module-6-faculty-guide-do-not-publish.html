In this module, students will create a communication management plan for an organizational project. This plan will outline the strategies, tools, and methods for effectively communicating change within an organization. The goal is to ensure that students understand the critical role of communication in change management and can develop a comprehensive plan to manage communication effectively.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will:

  1. Understand the importance of communication in managing organizational change.
  2. Identify key stakeholders and their communication needs.
  3. Develop a communication management plan that includes strategies, tools, and methods for effective communication.
  4. Demonstrate the ability to tailor communication strategies to different stakeholder groups.
  5. Evaluate the effectiveness of a communication management plan.

This module aims to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary to develop an effective communication management plan, a critical component of successful change management. By understanding and addressing the communication needs of different stakeholders, students will be better prepared to manage organizational change effectively.
