In this module, students will complete the final instance of a change management simulation and reflect on their experiences and learnings. This simulation will provide a hands-on opportunity to apply the theories and strategies of change management in a controlled environment. The discussion board will help students synthesize their experiences of the simulation and integrate their learnings.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will:

  1. Demonstrate the ability to apply change management theories and strategies in a simulated environment.
  2. Analyze the outcomes of their decisions within the simulation.
  3. Reflect on their performance and identify areas of strength and improvement.
  4. Integrate the course learning outcomes with their experiences in the simulation.
