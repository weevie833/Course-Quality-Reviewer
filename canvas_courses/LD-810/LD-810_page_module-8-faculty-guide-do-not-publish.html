In this module, students will reflect on the concepts, strategies, and theories of change management they have learned throughout the course. The primary focus is on writing a reflection paper that ties together the course learning outcomes with their personal and professional experiences.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will be able to:

  1. Critically evaluate their learning experiences in the course.
  2. Synthesize key concepts and theories of change management.
  3. Reflect on personal growth and development in understanding change management.
  4. Connect course materials to practical scenarios and real-world applications.
  5. Demonstrate effective written communication skills.
