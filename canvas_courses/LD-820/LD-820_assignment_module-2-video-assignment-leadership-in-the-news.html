In this exercise, we will use the concept of Inquiry-Based Learning (IBL), which places an emphasis on your finding answers through a discovery process.  This skill will assist you with evaluating evidence and discussing it—this skill you will use throughout the program and specifically in the last two courses.

Using the KWL template, select an interesting story from a news source, about emotional intelligence in relationship to leadership to use as the basis for your inquiry and examination. 

  • In the first column, write what you already know about the topic. Write what you want to know about the topic in the second column.
  • After you have completed your research, write what you learned in the third column.
  • Then create and submit a 5-6 minute presentation outlining your findings.
  • You will submit the completed template in the following assignment.
