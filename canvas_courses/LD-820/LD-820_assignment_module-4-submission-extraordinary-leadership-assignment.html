In this assignment, you will have the opportunity to apply leadership concepts to a leader you believe was/is an extraordinary leader. Select a leader about whom you would like to learn more. This person may be a leader whom you have worked with or someone who is a public figure from business, the arts, politics, etc. The person may not be a fictional character. In this paper, you will review key events in the person’s life and analyze his/her leadership approach based on the research you have collected, course readings and class discussions

Submit a two- three-page analysis in a Word document. Cite at least two references from your text or other sources to support your responses. Use established APA guidelines.

 

Submission is due Sunday 11:59pm.

Be sure to review the Writing Rubric (available in Course Resources) to see how you will be evaluated.

This assignment provides an opportunity to practice the skills needed to complete the MS Leadership Capstone. The focus of this capstone preparatory assignment is Research. Please participate fully and make sure to review instructor feedback to continue to improve your skills throughout the program.

Other skills practiced here can also include Writing & Information Literacy skills such as applying the difference between annotated bibliographies and literature reviews, outlining, information literacy, APA formatting, and paper writing. Make sure to take note of those writing/literacy skills as well as they will be equally important to your success throughout this program.
