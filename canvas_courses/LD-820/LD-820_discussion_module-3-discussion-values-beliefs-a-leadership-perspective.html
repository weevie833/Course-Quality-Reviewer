Discuss your values and beliefs relative to your leadership perspective.

Leaders don’t just “talk the talk” when it comes to values; they “walk the talk.” So how have you put your values into practice?

  • Select a value that is important to you and write a brief scenario describing an action you took that demonstrated that this value was important to you. For instance, if “service to others” is important to you, you might write about a time when you volunteered to serve dinner at the local shelter on Thanksgiving or about a time when you joined a group of friends and headed down to Texas to help rebuild the community after Hurricane Harvey.
  • In other words, where’s the evidence that you walk your talk?

In your replies, if you share the value, can you reflect on ways that you have demonstrated this value. If you don't share the value, can you share an observation of how someone you know has demonstrated that value.
