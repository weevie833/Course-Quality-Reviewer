It is important to begin preparations for this assignment in the first week of the course. This will not be due until the end of Module 3, but you should begin contacting people to ask for their assistance. You will ask at least 3-5 people you consider an employer, colleague or Mentor [please no family members] to complete and return the 360 Multi-Perspective Feedback Assessment. Be sure to complete one on yourself.

You will ask reviewers to indicate the rating that corresponds to the degree to which they observe you demonstrating the behavior, according to the rating key at the top of each section. N/O should be used if they have not been in situations that would afford them the opportunity to observe the behavior. At the end of each section, ask them to provide comments and examples to support their ratings. If they need more space for comments, they can attach an additional page.

Once you have received all of the assessments back, combine the scores to establish an average, and then input that data into one master assessment sheet. Please scan in all of the materials into one document before submitting.
