For this assignment you will participate in a mandatory UNH IRB Training to prepare you for your research in your MS Leadership Capstone Course. When entering the IRB Training Course, navigate using the green arrows in the bottom middle. Once you complete the course, submit a screenshot of your certificate and upload it here. Save the certificate to submit during your capstone course at the end of your program. 

As you're completing the training, please take note of the different types of research methodologies, you will use this information in the Module 5 Discussion: IRB and Methods. 

This assignment provides an opportunity to practice the skills needed to complete the MS Leadership Capstone. The focus of this capstone preparatory assignment is Methodology. Please participate fully and make sure to review instructor feedback to continue to improve your skills throughout the program.

Other skills practiced here can also include Writing & Information Literacy skills such as applying the difference between annotated bibliographies and literature reviews, outlining, information literacy, APA formatting, and paper writing. Make sure to take note of those writing/literacy skills as well as they will be equally important to your success throughout this program.

All resources for completing the task above can be located in the MS Leadership Capstone and Writing & Information Literacy sections on the Course Resources page.
