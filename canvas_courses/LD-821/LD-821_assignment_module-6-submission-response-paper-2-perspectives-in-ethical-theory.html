Response Paper Prompt:

Create your own ethical stance using one or a combination of the theories discussed this week in the lecture material “Perspectives on Ethical Theory”. Develop three strong arguments in correlation with the text reading that supports your stance that this thesis is the most accurate in describing the core of how we should make moral decisions. 

 

Excellent written work should begin with a detailed outline before moving to the writing stage. Your paper should include:

  • incorporate at least three direct text references
  • use examples from the text to support your arguments
  • offer a number of strong arguments and analysis
  • support such work with a number of comprehensive and thorough insights
  • be at 3 pages in length (12 Font, double spaced) 
  • include no mechanical mistakes and be properly cited. 

 

 Make sure to review the Writing Rubric to see how you will be evaluated.  

This assignment provides an opportunity to practice the skills needed to complete the MS Leadership Capstone. Please participate fully and make sure to review instructor feedback to continue to improve your skills throughout the program.

Other skills practiced here can also include Writing & Information Literacy skills such as applying the difference between annotated bibliographies and literature reviews, outlining, information literacy, APA formatting, and paper writing. Make sure to take note of those writing/literacy skills as well as they will be equally important to your success throughout this program.
