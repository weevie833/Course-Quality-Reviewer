Welcome to LD 821 Ethical Decision-Making!

This is an ethics course designed for professionals who are pursuing leadership studies. 

This course focuses on the conflicts which arise when an individual’s ethics are counter to the organization’s practices. A historical review of various leader’s actions taken and consequences faced when confronted with an ethical dilemma is conducted. Each student assesses their ethical framework in context to an effective leadership model.
