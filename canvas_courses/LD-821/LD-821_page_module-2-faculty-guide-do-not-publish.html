This module introduces students to the foundational concepts of ethics and morality, encouraging them to engage in critical discussions about what constitutes right and wrong. The focus will be on framing these conversations in a way that supports ethical decision-making, particularly in leadership contexts. Students will participate in a discussion forum exploring the importance of ethical leadership and will write a response paper analyzing thinking problems that hinder good decision-making. Insights from these activities will contribute to their signature assignment.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students should be able to:

  • Understand and articulate the basic principles of ethics and morality.
  • Critically assess what constitutes right and wrong in various contexts.
  • Discuss the importance of ethical behavior in leadership roles.
  • Identify and analyze thinking problems that impede good decision-making.
  • Integrate insights from discussions and assignments into their signature assignment on leadership ethics.

Conclusion

This module is designed to lay the groundwork for students to engage deeply with the fundamental concepts of ethics and morality, particularly in the context of leadership. Through discussions and reflective writing, students will explore what it means to be an ethical leader and identify the cognitive challenges that can undermine good decision-making. These insights will be critical as they develop their philosophy of leadership ethics, culminating in their signature assignment.
