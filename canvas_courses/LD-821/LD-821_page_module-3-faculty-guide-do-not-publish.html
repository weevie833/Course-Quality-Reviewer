Module Overview

This module is designed to help students explore the concepts of morality and good decision-making, particularly in the context of organizational values. Through discussions on the factors that define ethical situations and the forces that motivate ethical behavior, students will develop a deeper understanding of how ethical decisions are made in organizations. The insights gained in this module will be instrumental in shaping their philosophy of leadership ethics, which they will articulate in their signature assignment.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students should be able to:

  • Understand the role of morality in ethical decision-making within organizations.
  • Identify and analyze key values that drive ethical behavior in organizations.
  • Discuss the two factors that define ethical situations and what motivates people to act ethically.
  • Integrate these discussions into their developing philosophy of leadership ethics for their signature assignment.

Conclusion

This module is designed to deepen students' understanding of how morality and values influence ethical decision-making in organizations. Through discussions and reflections on ethical situations, students will gain valuable insights that will contribute to their philosophy of leadership ethics, which will be articulated in their signature assignment.
