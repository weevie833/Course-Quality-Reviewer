This module guides students through an exploration of how societies and organizations make ethical decisions, using the seven notions of ethical theory as a framework. Students will engage with Chapter 4 of their e-book, participate in discussions on leadership ethics and the ethics of AI, and write a response paper based on lecture materials. These activities are designed to help students develop a deeper understanding of ethical decision-making and refine their philosophy of leadership ethics, which will contribute to their signature assignment.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students should be able to:

  • Understand and apply the seven notions of ethical theory to analyze ethical decision-making in societies and organizations.
  • Critically reflect on the strengths and weaknesses of ethical perspectives, particularly about leadership.
  • Engage in informed discussions about the ethics of AI in leadership.
  • Develop insights that will contribute to their philosophy of leadership ethics.

Conclusion

This module encourages students to delve into the complexities of ethical decision-making in societies and organizations, offering them opportunities to critically engage with key ethical theories and apply these insights to modern challenges like AI in leadership. Through a combination of discussions, reflective writing, and analysis, students will refine their leadership ethics philosophy, which will be articulated in their signature assignment.
