This module aims to integrate the concepts of critical thinking, community building, and leadership ethics. Students will explore how the values that support critical thinking contribute to building a strong, ethical community. Additionally, students will demonstrate their understanding of the role of institutional research through an infographic and finalize their learning with a signature assignment that articulates their philosophy of proper leadership ethics.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students should be able to:

  • Identify and articulate the values that support critical thinking and how they relate to building ethical communities.
  • Create an infographic that demonstrates a comprehensive understanding of the role of institutional research in supporting ethical decision-making.
  • Develop and express a personal philosophy of leadership ethics in their signature assignment.

Conclusion

This module brings together the critical elements of ethical thinking, community engagement, and leadership philosophy. Through a combination of reflective writing, creative expression, and philosophical development, students will gain a holistic understanding of how ethical leadership is informed by critical thinking and supported by institutional research.
