This module encourages students to reflect deeply on the ethical concepts and principles they have explored throughout the course. The reflection paper assignment is designed to help students connect these ethical theories and concepts to their personal and professional lives and thesis work. Through this process, students will enhance their understanding of how ethics can be applied in real-world contexts and in their scholarly work.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students should be able to:

  • Reflect critically on the ethical theories and principles covered in the course.
  • Apply ethical concepts to their professional and personal experiences.
  • Integrate ethical considerations into their capstone work.
  • Develop a coherent and well-structured reflection paper.

The reflection paper assignment is a key component of the ethics course, enabling students to connect theoretical knowledge with practical application. By thoughtfully reflecting on their learning, students will be better equipped to navigate ethical challenges in their professional and personal lives and contribute to ethical scholarship through their capstone work.
