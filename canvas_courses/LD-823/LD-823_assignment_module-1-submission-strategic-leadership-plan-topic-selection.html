In the final module of this course, you will submit a 9-12 page paper (excluding title and reference pages) on a strategic leadership function that interests you, along with a personal leadership plan. The following leadership functions are available to choose from:

  1. Making Strategic Decisions
  2. Engaging with Stakeholders
  3. Performing Human Resource Management Activities
  4. Motivating and Influencing
  5. Managing Information
  6. Overseeing Operations and Administration
  7. Managing Social and Ethical Issues
  8. Managing Conflicting Demands

 

Assignment Directions:

  • Choose a topic of personal interest from the list above, which will serve as the basis for your final paper.
  • Draft at least 250 words with your rationale for choosing it and its connection to strategic leadership from your perspective.
  • Engage with AI as a tool to brainstorm your topic (such as Chat GPT, Google Gemini, Microsoft CoPilot, etc.) with the following prompt or one similar:
    • I am preparing to write a leadership-based graduate-level paper about (insert your selected function). Please review this rationale for the topic and its connection to strategic leadership, and suggest three additional elements I should consider when writing a literature review for this paper: (paste 250-word draft).
  • Compare your rationale with the AI output and write a reflective paragraph. You may use the following prompts? 
    • What were the differences between your rational and the AI-generated text?
    • Did it provide areas you hadn't considered? What were they? Are they viable?
    • Did you find the use of AI helpful?
  • Submit this assignment as a Word document in APA format. Make sure to cite AI-generated text.

This assignment is due Sunday night.
