For this assignment, you will create a stakeholder map based on a project or initiative you have been closely involved with (past or present), or a hypothetical/fictional setting. Considering your experience and understanding of stakeholder mapping, describe in 1-2 pages (excluding title and reference pages) what the project or initiative was, how you identified and categorized the stakeholders, and how communication to each group was organized.

In addition to the 1–2-page paper, please submit a second document or image of a visual stakeholder map representing your paper's project or initiative.
