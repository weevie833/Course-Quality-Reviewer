This week, you will complete and submit an annotated bibliography with at least six sources that provide background on your selected strategic leadership function. For each source, please include at least one paragraph summarizing the source and your findings. This submission must include at least six scholarly sources.

Please note: You will draft a literature review next week, which will build on this annotated bibliography and serve as a key foundational piece for your final paper.
