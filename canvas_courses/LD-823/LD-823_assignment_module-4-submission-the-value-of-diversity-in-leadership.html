In a 2-3-page paper, using APA and three outside sources, describe the benefits of diversity in your organization. Does the mission statement and vision statement say about diversity? You may want to interview your supervisor, someone from HR, and or someone from senior leadership. Are there any barriers to increasing the diversity in the organization where you work? How could you share the benefits with your coworkers and help to foster a greater sense of pride in the diversity of your organization?
