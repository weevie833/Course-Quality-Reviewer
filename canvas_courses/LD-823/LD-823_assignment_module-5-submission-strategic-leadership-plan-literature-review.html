In module 7 of this course, you will submit a 9-12- page paper (excluding title and reference pages) on a strategic leadership function that interests you, along with a personal leadership plan.

For this week, please submit a 5-6 page literature review on your leadership function. Continuing from your introduction section, this paper must be written in APA 7th Edition Professional Style Guidelines and include a title page and references section.

At a minimum, this section of your paper should provide background on the function you selected and include the following:

Sources: Include at least six quality sources that provide background on your function (e.g., peer-reviewed articles, reports from trusted associations or agencies)

Introduction:

  • An introductory paragraph that explains your topic.
  • A summary of the key topics that will appear in the review and their significance.

Body:

  • Summarize and synthesize: Give an overview of the main themes of the research, summarize the themes, and synthesize the materials. Combine the works into a cohesive review.
  • Analyze and interpret: Don’t just paraphrase other researchers – add your own interpretations where possible, discussing the significance of findings in relation to the literature as a whole.
  • Critically Evaluate: Mention the strengths and weaknesses of your sources.
  • Write in well-structured paragraphs: Use transition words and topic sentences to draw connections, comparisons, and contrasts.

Conclusion:

  • Summarize the key findings you have taken from the literature and emphasize their significance.
  • Connect it back to the purpose of your paper.
