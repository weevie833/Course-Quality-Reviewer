Module Overview

In Module 1, students will be introduced to each other and the foundational concepts of strategic leadership. This module sets the stage for the rest of the course by encouraging students to introduce themselves, reflect on their leadership experience, and choose a topic for their signature assignment, the strategic leadership plan. Students will participate in a video discussion forum for introductions and select one of the eight provided topics to focus on for their strategic leadership plan.

Module Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will:

  1. Engage with classmates and begin building a collaborative learning environment.
  2. Understand the critical elements of strategic leadership and how they apply to personal leadership development.
  3. Please choose a topic for their signature assignment (strategic leadership plan) that aligns with their career goals and interests.
  4. Begin reflecting on their leadership experiences and areas for growth throughout the course.

Instructor Role:

  • Encourage students to create a welcoming, supportive atmosphere for sharing personal and professional experiences.
  • Provide examples of effective leadership reflection and guide students to think critically about their strengths, areas for growth, and course goals.
  • Monitor the discussion to ensure students engage constructively with each other’s reflections.
  • Guide how to choose a topic that aligns with each student’s leadership goals and professional aspirations.
  • Offer feedback on topic selections, ensuring that students clearly understand their chosen area and how to develop it throughout the course.
  • Please encourage students to select topics that challenge them to grow and explore new dimensions of strategic leadership.

Module Conclusion

In Module 1, students were introduced to the foundational concepts of strategic leadership and began building a community with their classmates through the video discussion forum. Students reflected on their leadership experiences shared goals for the course and selected a topic for their strategic leadership plan, which will serve as the signature assignment for this course. This topic selection allows students to focus on an area of leadership that aligns with their personal and professional development.

As students progress, they will continue to build on these foundational concepts, deepening their understanding of strategic leadership and applying their learning to the signature assignment, which will grow throughout the course.
