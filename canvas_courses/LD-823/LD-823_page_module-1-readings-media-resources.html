Overview

In this module, we will examine the foundational elements of strategic leadership and set the stage for the course. 

The world craves effective leaders, especially those with a strategic vision. In today's complex landscape, this means going beyond day-to-day operations. Our course, Emergence of a Strategic Leader, will delve into the multifaceted role of a strategic leader. We'll explore how these leaders make critical decisions, navigate complex stakeholder relationships, and manage human resources effectively. You'll gain insights into the art of motivation and influence, the importance of information management, and the ability to oversee operational and administrative functions. We'll also address the critical skills needed to navigate social and ethical dilemmas, all while managing the inevitable clash of competing demands.
