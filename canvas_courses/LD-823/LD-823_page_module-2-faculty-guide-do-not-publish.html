Module Overview

In Module 2, students will focus on the processes and elements involved in strategic decisions. Strategic decision-making is a core component of leadership, especially when navigating complex organizational changes like mergers and acquisitions. This module allows students to analyze decision-making frameworks and apply them to real-world scenarios. Students will participate in a discussion forum to identify decision-making elements and complete an assignment to explore future states related to a merger or acquisition.

Module Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will:

  1. Understand the key elements involved in making strategic decisions within a leadership context.
  2. Analyze and reflect on decision-making frameworks and their application in organizational strategy.
  3. Apply decision-making principles to anticipate and explore future states related to mergers or acquisitions.
  4. Demonstrate critical thinking and strategic analysis skills through engagement with peer discussions and real-world case analysis.

Instructor Role:

  • Please encourage students to critically engage with the decision-making process, guiding them to consider how the different elements interconnect to support effective leadership.
  • Provide examples of strategic decision-making frameworks and best practices to help students draw connections between theory and real-world leadership scenarios.
  • Monitor the discussion to ensure that students actively engage with each other and offer meaningful feedback.
  • Guide students in analyzing how mergers or acquisitions create strategic opportunities and challenges, emphasizing the role of decision-making in shaping organizational outcomes.
  • Provide feedback on how students can use strategic decision-making frameworks to consider multiple future states and weigh the risks and rewards associated with each.
  • Offer support to students who need help structuring their analysis or identifying appropriate decision-making elements for the assignment.

Module Conclusion

In this module, students explored the decision-making process as a crucial part of strategic leadership, focusing on how decisions shape organizational outcomes, particularly in the context of mergers and acquisitions. The discussion forum allowed students to reflect on critical elements of strategic decision-making and learn from each other’s experiences. The assignment challenged students to anticipate future states following a merger or acquisition, analyzing opportunities, challenges, and strategic decisions that can influence long-term success.

As students progress, they will continue to build on their understanding of strategic decision-making and apply these insights to more complex scenarios in future modules, further refining their leadership skills.
