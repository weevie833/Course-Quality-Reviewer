Module Overview

In Module 3, students will focus on the critical role of stakeholders in strategic leadership. Stakeholder management and engagement are crucial to ensuring organizational success, and this module helps students explore how to identify, map, and communicate with stakeholders effectively. Students will participate in a discussion forum to analyze a stakeholder strategy for a familiar organization, create a stakeholder map through an infographic assignment, and complete an annotated bibliography to support their leadership plan. The bibliography will ensure that students use solid and relevant sources to support their ongoing work in the course.

Module Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will:

  1. Understand the importance of stakeholders in strategic leadership and how to develop effective stakeholder strategies.
  2. Analyze the stakeholder strategy of an organization they are familiar with.
  3. Create a stakeholder map and explain the roles of various stakeholders in an organization.
  4. Develop an annotated bibliography with scholarly sources that support their strategic leadership plan.

Instructor Role:

  • Please encourage students to critically assess the stakeholder strategies of various organizations, guiding them to consider the balance between internal and external stakeholders.
  • Provide examples of best stakeholder management practices and strategies aligning with broader organizational goals.
  • Monitor the discussion to ensure students engage with each other’s posts thoughtfully and constructively.
  • Guide how to design a clear and informative stakeholder map, emphasizing the importance of categorizing stakeholders and illustrating their relationships to the organization.
  • Encourage students to consider different stakeholders' varying levels of influence and importance and how these factors affect organizational decisions.
  • Offer feedback on draft maps (if applicable) to help students refine their visual representation and analysis before submission.
  • Provide guidance on selecting reputable scholarly sources relevant to the strategic leadership plan. Offer tips on conducting thorough research and evaluating the credibility of sources.
  • Review the annotated bibliography to ensure students use appropriate sources to support their future work in the course.

Conclusion

Summary: In Module 3, students explored the critical role of stakeholders in strategic leadership, focusing on how to identify, map, and engage with key stakeholders effectively. The discussion forum allowed students to analyze stakeholder strategies in familiar organizations, while the infographic assignment challenged them to visually represent stakeholder relationships and roles. The annotated bibliography helped students build a research foundation for their strategic leadership plan by selecting relevant scholarly sources and evaluating their contributions.

As students move forward, they will continue to build on the foundation of stakeholder management and integrate the insights from this module into their broader strategic leadership work, including their final leadership plan.
