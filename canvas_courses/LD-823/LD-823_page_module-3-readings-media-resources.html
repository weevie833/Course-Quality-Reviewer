Overview

This module will cover strategic tactics for internal and external stakeholder engagement.

Effective strategic leadership hinges on actively engaging both internal and external stakeholders. Internally, employees offer crucial insights into daily operations and opportunities for growth. Engaging them through open communication and collaborative planning fosters a sense of ownership and fuels innovation. This can be done by soliciting ideas through surveys or focused discussions or even by empowering employees to spearhead strategic initiatives.

Externally, customers, suppliers, and even community members offer valuable perspectives on market trends, potential risks, and opportunities for growth. By engaging with them through surveys, focus groups, or even social media interaction, strategic leaders gain a well-rounded view of the competitive landscape and can craft strategies that resonate with the broader ecosystem. This two-pronged approach to stakeholder engagement empowers strategic leaders to develop plans with a higher chance of success.
