In Module 4, students will explore the intersection of human resources management (HRM) and strategic leadership. This module emphasizes how leaders can use HR strategies to align with organizational goals and promote diversity, inclusion, and effective resource management. Students will participate in a video discussion forum to reflect on HR's role in leadership and share their strategic leadership plans. The module also includes the creation of an infographic outlining a strategic HR management system and an assignment focusing on using the mission and vision statement as a lens for improving workplace diversity.

Module Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will:

  1. Reflect on the role of human resources management in strategic leadership and how it aligns with organizational goals.
  2. Understand how to design a strategic human resources management system that addresses critical HR functions.
  3. Apply their mission and vision statement to create actionable strategies for improving workplace diversity.
  4. Integrate HR management principles into their strategic leadership plan and communicate them effectively through visual representation.

Instructor Role:

  • Encourage students to reflect critically on the role of HR in leadership, guiding them to explore how strategic HRM aligns with broader organizational objectives.
  • Provide feedback on how students can integrate HR strategies into their leadership plans, emphasizing alignment with mission, vision, and organizational goals.
  • Monitor the discussion to ensure students offer meaningful feedback and engage with one another’s perspectives on HR and leadership.
  • Provide examples or templates for effective infographics, guiding students on balancing visual design with meaningful content.
  • Encourage students to think strategically about how each HR function supports broader organizational objectives, particularly in talent development and diversity.
  • Offer feedback on draft infographics (if applicable) to help students refine their work before submission.
  • Guide students in aligning their mission and vision statements with diversity goals, helping them understand the connection between organizational values and HR strategies.
  • Encourage students to think critically about diversity challenges and propose practical solutions.
  • Provide feedback on draft assignments (if applicable), offering suggestions for improving alignment between diversity strategies and the organization’s mission and vision.

Module Conclusion

In Module 4, students explored the role of human resources management in strategic leadership, focusing on how HRM aligns with organizational goals and promotes diversity, engagement, and development. The video discussion forum allowed students to reflect on their evolving views of HRM and share elements of their strategic leadership plans. The infographic assignment challenged students to communicate the critical components of a strategic HRM system visually. In contrast, the mission and vision statement assignment required them to propose actionable strategies for improving workplace diversity.

As students move forward, they should be able to see HR as an essential part of their strategic leadership toolkit, capable of shaping organizational culture, driving diversity, and aligning people with mission-driven goals.
