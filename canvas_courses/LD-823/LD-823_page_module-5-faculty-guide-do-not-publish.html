Module Overview

In Module 5, students will explore strategies for motivating and influencing individuals and teams within the context of strategic leadership. This module emphasizes the importance of understanding motivation tactics and how to maintain or improve organizational morale. Students will participate in a discussion forum on motivation tactics and morale and complete a literature review focused on a leadership function of their choice. This assignment requires students to identify at least six scholarly sources, synthesize the main themes, and discuss the shortcomings of each source.

Module Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will:

  1. Understand various tactics for motivating and influencing teams in leadership contexts.
  2. Reflect on the role of morale in team performance and organizational success.
  3. Conduct a comprehensive literature review on a leadership function, synthesizing key points from academic sources and identifying their shortcomings.
  4. Apply scholarly research to enhance their understanding of leadership functions related to motivation and influence.

Instructor Role:

  • Encourage students to think critically about different motivational approaches, focusing on both theory and practical application in leadership settings.
  • Provide examples of common motivation strategies, such as intrinsic/extrinsic rewards, empowerment, recognition, and team-building.
  • Guide students to reflect on how leaders can proactively influence morale and why maintaining a positive work environment is essential for team success.
  • Provide guidance on how to conduct a thorough literature review, including tips on identifying reputable sources, synthesizing findings, and analyzing the strengths and weaknesses of the literature.
  • Encourage students to select a leadership function that aligns with their interests or current/future leadership roles, helping them make connections between research and real-world application.
  • Offer feedback on drafts (if applicable) to help students refine their analysis and improve their synthesis of the literature.

Module Conclusion

In this module, students explored the critical leadership functions of motivating and influencing individuals and teams. The discussion forum allowed students to reflect on effective motivation tactics and the role of morale in leadership, offering practical insights into how leaders can inspire and engage their teams. The literature review assignment challenged students to deepen their understanding of a specific leadership function, analyzing scholarly research to identify common themes and critical shortcomings in the literature.

As students progress in the course, they will continue to build on their foundational knowledge of motivation, influence, and leadership functions, applying these insights to more complex leadership challenges.
