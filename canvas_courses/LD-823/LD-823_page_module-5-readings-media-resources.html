Overview

This week, we will explore strategic leadership tactics for creating and sustaining motivation and influence.

Motivation and influence are the foundations of successful strategic leadership. Regardless of a formal title, a strategic leader must ignite a fire within their team. This is achieved by creating a compelling vision for the future that excites and motivates individuals to contribute their best efforts.  However, a clear vision alone isn't enough. Strategic leaders wield influence to shape the course of action. This influence can be formal, utilizing their position to allocate resources or make decisions. More importantly, it can be informal, inspiring trust and buy-in through effective communication, empathy, and a genuine commitment to the team's success. A strategic leader can rally a team around a shared vision by effectively motivating and influencing others, even without a traditional leadership position.
