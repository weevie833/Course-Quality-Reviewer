Module Overview

In Module 7, students will focus on the role of overseeing operations and administration in strategic leadership. This module highlights the importance of operational efficiency, decision-making, and administrative management as part of a leader’s strategic responsibilities. Students will participate in a video discussion about the course materials and complete their signature assignment, which is a strategic leadership plan focusing on their personal development. This assignment builds on the literature review completed in Module 5 and integrates the course concepts to help students create a comprehensive personal development strategy.

Module Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will:

  1. Understand the role of operations and administration in strategic leadership and how leaders oversee these functions to drive organizational success.
  2. Reflect on their leadership development concerning overseeing operations and managing administrative responsibilities.
  3. Create a strategic leadership plan that focuses on their personal development, utilizing insights from the literature review completed in Module 5.
  4. Synthesize course concepts and personal insights to design a plan for continuous leadership growth in overseeing operations and administration.

Instructor Role:

  • Encourage students to engage deeply with the concepts of operations and administration in strategic leadership, guiding them to reflect on theoretical and practical aspects.
  • Provide feedback on how students can connect course concepts to their leadership experiences or aspirations.
  • Monitor the discussion to ensure students are engaging with each other meaningfully, providing constructive feedback, and sharing relevant experiences.
  • Guide students in developing a strategic leadership plan that is both reflective and actionable. Please encourage them to set realistic goals and outline clear strategies for achieving them.
  • Provide feedback on how students can effectively integrate theoretical insights from their literature review into their personal development plan.
  • Offer support and resources for students as they draft their strategic leadership plans, ensuring that their goals align with their career aspirations and leadership strengths.

Module Conclusion

As this is the final content module, students are encouraged to reflect on the significant growth they have achieved throughout the course. The video discussion allows students to share and engage with their peers regarding the vital concepts of overseeing operations and administration, providing practical examples and insights.

The signature assignment—the strategic leadership plan—serves as a key demonstration of each student’s ability to apply the leadership theories and frameworks they have studied throughout the course to their personal and professional growth. This assignment not only helps students synthesize their learning but also provides a clear roadmap for their continued development as strategic leaders.

By the end of this module, students will have a comprehensive understanding of overseeing operations and administration in strategic leadership. They will be prepared to continue their leadership development beyond the classroom.
