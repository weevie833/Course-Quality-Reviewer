Welcome to Volunteer Leadership!

This course focuses on practical application of current trends in volunteer leadership and management.  Volunteer recruitment, screening, training, retention, evaluation, and appreciation are considered in the context of managing with limited resources, the emergence of workplace volunteer programs, generational differences, and competition for skilled volunteers. Explore the role of ethics and risk management as they apply to service, and how to mitigate concerns. Other topics include advocacy and professional development within the field.
