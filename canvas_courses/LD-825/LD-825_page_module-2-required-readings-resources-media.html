Overview

In Module 2, you will learn that volunteers are not free. Organizations and volunteer leaders must invest in volunteers to achieve the desired results. Investment may be in the form of money, training, insurance, or time getting to know their motivations. You will learn about the importance and benefits of using service descriptions to facilitate clear expectations with volunteers.
