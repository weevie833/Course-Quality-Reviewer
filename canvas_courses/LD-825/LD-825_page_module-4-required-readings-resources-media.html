Overview

In Module 4, you will consider using persuasion to connect people to your cause. We will explore if there are circumstances that would prevent a person from providing service. You will consider screening questions to ensure you are placing the right person in the right role.

Orientation and training can be important tools when preparing individuals for your service roles, but they are not applicable to all opportunities, including micro-volunteering or single day service. Grad students will create a volunteer training manual.  Undergrad students will identify the important components of the handbook and state why they were included. You will explore how skilled volunteers and company partnerships may benefit your program.

Get Started: In this module you will get started on your Volunteer Handbook Assignment (due Module 7). It is also important to get started on scheduling your interview in preparation for your Interview Assignment (due Module 6).
