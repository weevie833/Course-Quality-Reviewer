Module Overview

In Module 5, students will focus on program evaluation, feedback, and organizational culture in volunteer leadership. This module encourages students to understand the importance of evaluating volunteer programs, handling difficult interpersonal situations, and navigating ethical dilemmas. Students will engage in two discussion forums: one on the value of program evaluation and the other on managing difficult situations with various stakeholders. They will also complete an assignment involving analyzing an ethical dilemma using a worksheet in the course assignment.

Module Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will:

  1. Understand the importance of program evaluation in volunteer leadership and how to include stakeholders in the evaluation process.
  2. Develop strategies for handling difficult interpersonal situations with volunteers, employees, or other stakeholders.
  3. Analyze an ethical dilemma in volunteer leadership and apply a structured approach to resolving the issue using a provided worksheet.
  4. Apply course concepts to real-world volunteer leadership challenges, particularly in program evaluation, stakeholder management, and ethical decision-making.

Instructor Role:

  • Encourage students to think critically about the purpose and methods of program evaluation, including how it contributes to program improvement and stakeholder satisfaction.
  • Guide different evaluation techniques (e.g., surveys, interviews, focus groups) and emphasize the importance of involving various stakeholders.
  • Monitor the discussion for thoughtful engagement, ensuring students reflect on real-world applications of program evaluation in volunteer leadership.
  • Encourage students to reflect on actual or hypothetical situations where interpersonal challenges arise in volunteer or organizational settings, focusing on how communication and conflict resolution can impact outcomes.
  • Provide examples of effective communication strategies and conflict resolution techniques, helping students connect theory to practice.
  • Monitor the discussion for thoughtful engagement and challenge students to consider the long-term impact of effectively managing difficult situations.
  • Ensure students understand the importance of ethical decision-making in volunteer leadership and how to apply ethical principles to real-world dilemmas.
  • Guide how to complete the worksheet, emphasizing the importance of considering all stakeholders and the ethical implications of different solutions.
  • Offer support to students who may experience technical difficulties accessing the assignment instructions, advising them to download the file if necessary.

Conclusion

In this module, students will explore critical topics related to program evaluation, feedback, and organizational culture. The discussion forums encourage students to think about the importance of program evaluation and how to handle difficult interpersonal situations with stakeholders. The ethical dilemma assignment challenges students to apply ethical decision-making skills to a real-world case study. By the end of the module, students will have a deeper understanding of how program evaluation contributes to organizational success and how ethical leadership and conflict resolution play key roles in managing volunteers and stakeholders effectively.
