Overview

Many volunteer leaders struggle to find the resources to evaluate their programs, which taken as a whole can be daunting. One consideration is to evaluate smaller more manageable areas such as volunteer roles, the orientation session, or the recruitment messaging. In this module you will explore why evaluating and measuring impact is important to making progress, recruiting volunteers and even funding.

Volunteer leaders are often faced with challenging situations, if left unchecked, these situations can lead to turnover, toxic environments, and damage to the organization’s reputation.  In this module you will examine some of the difficult personalities you have encountered and consider some feasible solutions. You will also consider a case study that has ethical implications, explain how you would handle the situation and why.
