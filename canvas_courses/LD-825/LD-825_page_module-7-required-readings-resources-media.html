Overview

Volunteer leaders are often faced with challenging situations, if left unchecked, these situations can lead to turnover, toxic environments, and damage to the organization’s reputation.  In this module you will examine some of the difficult personalities you have encountered and consider some feasible solutions. You will also consider a case study that has ethical implications, explain how you would handle the situation and why. You will explore professional credentialing and the case for professional development within the volunteer leadership field. You will also explore core values and community importance.
