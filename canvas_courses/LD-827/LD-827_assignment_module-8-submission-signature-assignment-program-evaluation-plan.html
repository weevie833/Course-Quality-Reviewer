Elements of an Evaluation Plan

Your final assignment is the creation of a Program Evaluation PLAN for an agency that interests you or one with which you are very familiar, such as where you work, this plan will be based on the program you selected in module 3. The final paper will be 6-7 pages, not counting the title page or reference pages.

Components

  • What is the purpose of the evaluation? Is the Nonprofit obliged by its funding sources to have an annual evaluation? Example: Head Start programs get Federal money and must have independent evaluations completed each year.
  • Create 3 -4 key Evaluation Questions

Examples: Some sample questions could be:

  • Who is the target population?
  • What services do they need?
  • How best should the project/ program deliver those services (or activities)?
  • How will the program work with the target population to sustain the project/ program?
  • What is the structure or organization of the project/ program?
  • Adopt a timetable and work plan.

This will be an estimate on your part. You are not completing an evaluation; you are creating a plan to do so.

  • How would you collect data for an evaluation?

Below is a summary of the most commonly used methods in evaluation:

  • Document analysis.
  • Surveys.
  • Interviews.
  • Observations.
  • Focus groups.
