How would you recruit board members for a nonprofit providing food for low-income people? What type of person would you want to include? 

  • This is a broad question that seeks your opinion.
  • Please justify your thoughts on the skills, networks, and experience that are most beneficial in a board member.
  • Graduate Students should cite examples to highlight their opinions.
  • Undergraduates may cite examples but do not need to do so.

Please post your response by Thursday midnight and respond to a minimum of 2 classmates with additional feedback. For instance, is there more to consider? How do others thoughts improve your own? Responses are due by Sunday midnight.  More peer responses are highly encouraged to increase discussion activity throughout the week. 

Make sure to review the Discussion Rubric (located in Course Resources).
