Creating an Infographic

  • SmartArt is a simple way to create an Infographic using Word or PPT. 
  • Visme is a free online tool for making creative Infographics. (Downloading your creation is not free in Visme, however, you can share your infographic by getting a share link. When you have completed your infographic, select 'Share' in the top right corner, select 'Share Privately', make sure it reads 'Anyone with the link', and copy the link at the bottom of the window. Copy link into Infographic discussion or submission.)
  • Canva. is a free online tool for making creative Infographics.

You do not need to use the suggestions above. These are just some options & tools that we have used in the past. With Visme & Canva you will need to 'sign up' by using an email. If you have a junk email address, that may be best to use.
