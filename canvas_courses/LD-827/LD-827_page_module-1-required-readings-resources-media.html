Overview

In Week 1 you will start a course that will review virtually every aspect of Nonprofit Management. We will start with introductions.

We will learn how nonprofit organizations depend on the support and goodwill of their stakeholders for success.  Review common types of stakeholders in nonprofit organizations and at what level they have influence.

Readings

  • Chapters 1-3 in Nonprofit Management: Principles and Practice
  • Sarbanes-Oxley Act
  • Identifying and Analyzing Stakeholders and their Interests (Community Tool Box) 
  • Five-Step Approach to Stakeholder Engagement (BSR.org)
