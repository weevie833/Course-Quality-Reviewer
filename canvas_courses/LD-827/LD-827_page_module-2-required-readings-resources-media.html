Overview

In Module 2 you will learn how nonprofit organizations depend on leadership to provide guidance internally and externally. Governing Boards provide many crucial functions including legal responsibility for expenditures and partnerships, vision and mission alignment, and the experience and resources each board member brings to the organization. The role of the Governing Board has changed as expectations about ethics and accountability for nonprofit organizations have increased.

I will offer optional Zoom Sessions in this module. Although this is not mandatory, attendance will aid in your understanding of the expectations of the Signature Assignment/Program Evaluation. I can answer any questions as you begin your Signature Assignment/Program Evaluation.
