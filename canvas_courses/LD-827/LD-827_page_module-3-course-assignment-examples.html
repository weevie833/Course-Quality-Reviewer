Module 3

I have selected the Certified Public Manager Education Foundation as the nonprofit organization I will feature in all assignment and discussions that will culminate with my final submission of a Program Evaluation Plan design in module eight.

I have selected this organization because I serve on its Board of Directors. Our Board of Directors is hard at work on many of the same issues that are the basis or many of our assignments in this course. Since the purpose and mission of the CPMEF is financial support for CPM students and alumni we always need to raise money. I look forward to our reading the work of my classmates as they discuss fundraising. I believe the exchange of ideas will be most helpful.

The purpose of the Foundation is to aid current Certified Public Manager students and graduates of the 40 programs across the country with their pursuit of continuing education through scholarships and grants. These are outlined in the organization’s understanding of its mission and vision and stated purposes.

MISSION STATEMENT

“To provide support for Certified Public Managers® and their organizations in achieving educational and professional goals through the contribution of finances, goods, and services.

VISION

The vision of the Certified Public Manager® Education Foundation (CPMEF) is to cultivate public managers to be their best by providing support for Certified Public Managers® to participate in professional development and education opportunities.

The purpose of this organization shall be to help support and enhance efforts of Certified Public Managers® to attend continuing education from conferences and programs where a number of Certified Public Managers® will be present.  The CPMEF shall maintain a relationship with American Academy of Certified Public Managers but will also assist other CPM organizations when at least 60% of its members are CPMs.

OBJECTIVES

The Foundation’s objectives are to support enhancements to Professional Development Conferences, encourage financial support and contributions from interested parties and others; provide financial resources for scholarship programs; and related programs that support continuing education for CPMs.”

I have selected this organization because, to be effective, it must raise money to provide its intended services. In future assignments, I will present a SWOT and/or a PEST analysis of this organization. A SWOT and PEST analysis, in many ways, are internal program evaluations that describe the health and sustainability of an organization.

In addition to the SWOT and PEST task in future modules, I intend to create a Balanced Scorecard infographic that describes the health of the financial and structural aspects of the Foundation.

In Module 4, there are several asks for which I will continue to focus on the CPMEF as my selected organization for my final submission. One of these tasks is to review types of evaluation teams and discuss why we might select a specific type (Assuming it is best suited for our selected organization.)

In Module 5, I will use the CPMEF as the focal point for partnerships and collaborations. In this regard, the CPMEF has several partnerships and collaborations and is working on several others at this moment in time.

In Module 6, we are asked to discuss plans to recruit appropriate board members. This is a fortuitous task since the CPMEF has begun discussions dedicated to adding two additional board members to the group. In Module Six I will also describe the type of board the CPMEF has as an outline.
