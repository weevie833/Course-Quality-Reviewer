This module explores the role of governing boards in organizations, focusing on ethical challenges and the recruitment of board members. Students will engage in discussions about the ethical issues boards may face, strategies to mitigate these challenges, and best practices for board recruitment. Additionally, students will submit a short paper analyzing different types of governing boards, comparing their structures, roles, and effectiveness.

Why It’s Important

  • Ethical Governance: Understanding the ethical responsibilities of governing boards is essential for maintaining accountability and trust within an organization. This module helps students critically examine these responsibilities and develop strategies for ethical governance.
  • Board Recruitment: Effective board recruitment is critical for building a diverse and competent board that can drive organizational success. This module equips students with the tools to identify key qualities in board members and understand the importance of board diversity.
  • Board Analysis: By comparing different types of boards, students will gain insights into how governance structures vary across sectors, deepening their understanding of organizational leadership and decision-making.
