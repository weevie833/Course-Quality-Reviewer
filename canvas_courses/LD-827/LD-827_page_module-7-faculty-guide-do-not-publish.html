This module focuses on fundraising strategies within organizational and program contexts. Students will discuss fundraising approaches and reflect on specific fundraising events that align with their professional interests. The module culminates with a written paper in which students apply course materials to describe a fundraising event they would like to attend and the insights they expect to gain from it.

Why It’s Important

  • Understanding Fundraising: This module helps students deepen their understanding of fundraising as a critical component of program sustainability and organizational success. Students can connect theoretical knowledge with practical applications by examining real-world events and strategies.
  • Professional Growth: Reflecting on a fundraising event allows students to consider how they can leverage similar career opportunities, strengthening their ability to design, execute, or participate in successful fundraising efforts.
