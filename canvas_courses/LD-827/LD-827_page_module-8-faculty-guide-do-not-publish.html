This module marks the culmination of the course, where students will present their signature assignment—a comprehensive Program Evaluation Plan—and submit a written course reflection. The primary goals of this module are for students to demonstrate their ability to apply course concepts practically and professionally and to engage in critical reflection on their learning experiences.

  • Program Evaluation Application: This module provides an opportunity for students to synthesize course content and apply it to a practical, professional context, reinforcing their skills in designing and evaluating programs.
  • Personal Reflection: The reflection paper allows students to critically assess their own learning and professional growth, preparing them to continue developing as leaders and evaluators in their future careers.
