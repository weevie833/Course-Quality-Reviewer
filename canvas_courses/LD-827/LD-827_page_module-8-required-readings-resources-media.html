Overview

In Module 8, you will learn how nonprofit organizations rely on executive leadership to guide the organization. Directors (sometimes called Executive Directors, Chief Executive Officers, or similar titles) are primary leadership figures in nonprofit organizations. Their responsibilities can expand and contract depending on the size of the organization and the organization’s by-laws. Leadership theories provide topics to consider. You will also examine types of funding, including grants and fund-raising events. In addition, you will review Federal regulations about funding and accountability.
