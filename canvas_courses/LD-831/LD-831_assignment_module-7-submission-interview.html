Conflict Assignment Interview Questions:

Your task is to interview someone you know and ask the attached questions in written format.

In addition to presenting their responses, you will also write a one-page summary of your thoughts on the interview. 

  • How would you describe the conflict(s) you have with someone at work? (No need to use a real name)
  • If you could describe the conflict by using a metaphor what would it be? (An example- The situation is a minefield. Or- The situation is icy.)
  • Can you describe the nature of the type of work each of you do?
  • Is this problem new or is there some history involved?
  • Does the behavior of the person include any signs of personality disorders? (An example could be: The person appears to be paranoid…or the person appears to have a passive-aggressive personality). Feel free to use your own terminology.
  • Do you believe that gender differences are playing a part in the conflict? (If yes, can you cite an example?)
  • Do you believe any cultural issues exist that add to this conflict? If so, can you describe them?
  • How do you generally deal with conflict?
  • How might an outsider with no role in the conflict describe what she sees?

 

Make sure to review the Writing Rubric to see how you will be evaluated. Rubric is located on the Course Resources page, or can be seen in the rubrics tab in this assignment.
