In this course, you have to interact with your colleagues. Reflect on what they have brought to this class and to you personally. Acknowledge each person, and take a moment to write what you appreciate about them and how they have shown up in this course. What have you learned from them? What did they do that made you feel good, understand something, or think differently? What is their unique talent that they may not know about but should be aware of?  

In your replies, expand on the posts made by your classmates. Reply to two classmates.
