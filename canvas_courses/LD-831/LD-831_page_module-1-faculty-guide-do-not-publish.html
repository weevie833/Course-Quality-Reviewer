In the first module, students will get to know each other and discuss their insights into real-life conflict situations. The reading will provide a basic introduction to generally recognized best practices for resolving contact. (This will continue throughout the term.) Assignments include selecting a topic for a final paper/ assignment.

Module Objectives

By the end of this module, students should be able to:

  • Understand Basic Language used to describe conflicts
  • Begin to reflect on their strengths and weaknesses when dealing with conflict.
  • Describe a conflict they have observed and possible solutions.

Conclusion

This first module is designed to introduce students to each other and begin thinking about their personal and professional experiences with conflict.

This module aims to lay a strong foundation for the course's continuation, ensuring that students are clear about the course's direction and their learning journey.
