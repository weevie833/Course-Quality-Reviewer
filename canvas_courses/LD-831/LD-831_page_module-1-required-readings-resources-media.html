Overview

In this first week we will explore the definitions of social conflict and interpersonal conflict. Every interaction we have with others opens the doors to differing needs and differing values. Understanding the root causes of conflict is the key to understanding how to manage it effectively. 

  • Communications issues will be examined
  • Introduction to Social Conflict
  • Emergence of Conflicts

In Week one we will get to know each other and discuss the nature of conflicts and how they arise.
