This module begins with a look at some techniques for resolving conflicts by various methods such as employing “Fresh Eyes”. The module asks students to do some self-assessments and to create some personal definitions of conflict.

The module reviews methods for negotiating with difficult people and the importance of forgiveness with family, friends, and coworkers.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students should be able to:

  • Understand and articulate the different perspectives when looking at the same information.
  • Critically assess the weaknesses and strengths when confronted with a conflict.
  • Looking at a problem from the perspective of the “other”.
  • Know more about their communication styles.

Conclusion

This module is a platform for students to explore their strengths and weaknesses, as well as the emotional impact of conflict. It is heavily influenced by William Ury's 'Getting to Yes with Yourself ', which provides a robust framework for understanding and resolving conflicts.
