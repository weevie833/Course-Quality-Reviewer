This module asks that students investigate ways to:

  • Deal with difficult people.
  • Conduct a personal experiment with a friend, classmate, or relative to assess their ability to Listen Actively.
  • Learn basic information about mediation and arbitration.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students should be able to:

  • Find ways to find allies across the table.
  • Identify and analyze the various types of difficult people.
  • Discuss ways to deal with difficult people
  • Integrate these discussions into their developing great leadership practices.

Conclusion

This module is designed to deepen students' understanding of becoming better listeners, a journey that requires patience and empathy. It also highlights the need for others to develop their listening skills. The module will also explore the various types of difficult people, including self-reflection. By doing so, students can devise strategies for more positive outcomes.
