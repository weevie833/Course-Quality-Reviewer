Overview

Many people shy away from conflicts but often outright addressing them moves a group, project, team or individuals to a better place. Core to resolving conflicts is building trust and working to understand what might be going on for another person. In the corporate world, even people who come from a bad place are often just trying to survive. They aren't focused on the other person only how to keep themselves or their teams afloat. The ability to have empathy for others to truly put yourself into their world will help you understand how to approach and develop possible solutions that can help both parties come to a "win/win".

There are different approaches to resolving conflict. Some of these are closely related to personality type. We will examine our personalities using an open-source DISC profile. The teacher will be happy to set up meetings to discuss your profile and what it means. Please make an appointment with the teacher for this discussion.
