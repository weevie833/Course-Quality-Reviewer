Negotiations & Ways to Influence Others

This module asks that students investigate ways to:

  • See yourself through the eyes of others.
  • Investigate Conflict from a global setting
  • Become acquainted with how and why conflicts can escalate.
  • Learn basic information de-escalation techniques.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students should be able to demonstrate:

  • Learn the communication techniques that positively influence others in a conflict situation;
  • Learn to apply the five phases of effective conflict resolution;
  • Understand how to use active questioning and listening to identify differences in need, value, and motivation.

Conclusion and learning outcomes

    • You can show an ability to adapt your communication energy to suit the situation.
    • Use communication styles to influence others.
    • Effectively use non-verbal communication skills (body language, physical position, expression, and posture).
    • Demonstrate the ability to identify and separate the differences that prevent agreement.
    • Use a collaborative approach to understand and agree on facts.
    • Demonstrate an ability to explore options and ideas jointly.
