Overview

When working with teams there is often a point of escalation - a place where everyone gets out on the table what is going on for them. It is also known as the elephant in the room. In order to make change whether that is in a social conflict, corporate or individual context there needs to be a point that brings out what needs to come out. This can be constructive and often helps lead to the resolution or de-escalation of that conflict.

We will examine finding common ground by establishing mutual interests to roll back escalation.
