Overview

How do we bring the conflict to a close? What does it look like once we do that? How can we ensure that we maintain the relationship - do we even need to? Many times, having had the conflict brings a better outcome. It's not easy to get there but when you do my hope is that you are moving toward a more positive outcome for all parties involved.

Investigating options is a critical skill. This skill is not easy but worth the effort. We can find multiple options when we look at things differently
