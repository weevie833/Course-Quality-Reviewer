Overview

In this module, students will explore how to best enhance their skills for conflict resolution and formal negotiations.  They will share tools they have uncovered and the following topics

Tools to Share

  • Seven Elements
  • Five Steps to get to yes.
  • Alternative Dispute Resolution, or ADR

Learning Objectives

The prime object of this module is to begin the study of formal negotiations and alternative methods of dispute resolution, such as fact-finding, Arbitration, and Mediation

Conclusion and Outcomes

The course's primary focus is conflict resolution techniques and communication skills required. In module 6 the desired outcome is for students to grasp negotiation skills and laws that may govern formal negotiations.
