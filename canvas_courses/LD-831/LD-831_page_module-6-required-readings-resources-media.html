Overview

In this module you will explore how to best navigate negotiations - it isn't easy as they are constantly changing. Moving towards a constructive solution is key. In order to learn more about how to do this well you are invited to interview someone who does this all the time. They may or may not be trained in formal negotiation tactics but listen for their approaches to negotiations. As you do compare to the information in the book and the additional resources.

In this module you will help share what you have learned about negotiations from the book Getting Past No: Negotiating with Difficult People by William Ury. You will have the opportunity to apply this learning to upcoming negotiations you will be having at work or in your personal life.

We will investigate some of the issues and laws surrounding union contract negotiations. We will review Federal and State laws that apply to union organizing and contracts, as well as rights and obligations.
