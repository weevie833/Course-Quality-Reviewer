Overview

In this module students will share what they have learned about negotiations from the book Getting Past No: Negotiating with Difficult People by William Ury. You will can apply this learning to upcoming negotiations you will be having at work or in your personal life.  

They also will get a chance to direct some of the dialogue by “Challenging” the teacher

Learning Objectives

  • Complete and submit their final papers.
  • Engage in inquiry with the teacher. A serious attempt to have students dig into the subject matter.

Outcomes

  • Final Paper completed and submitted
  • A fruitful dialogue with the instructor to call upon her or his experience with conflict and formal negotiations.
