Overview

In this module you will help share what you have learned about negotiations from the book Getting Past No: Negotiating with Difficult People by William Ury. You will also have the opportunity to apply this learning to upcoming negotiations you will be having at work or in your personal life.  This Week: Share your next negotiation and the BATNA answers you will use to help it become a success. Complete your reading and analysis of the Getting Past No: Negotiating with Difficult People

In this module you will pull together all the tools you have learned and determine what you want to do with them. My hope is that you understand these are the secrets to navigating through relationships in a way that is respectful, authentic and powerful. These will serve you in your work and your personal life.
