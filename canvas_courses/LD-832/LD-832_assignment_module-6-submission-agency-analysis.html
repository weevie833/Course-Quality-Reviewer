Identify 2-3 organizations that provide diversity and leadership training services, tools, or methods to people within organizations.  Research these organizations in terms of services they provide and populations served, and interview 2 persons providing these types of services, tools or methods.

Interviews:  Conduct 2 interviews (phone or in-person) with either a) 1 staff person currently providing consulting, training or teaching diversity and leadership training, be sure to include components of equity, culture competency and equality, or b) 2 staff at the same agency who have different roles or work in different programs or services within the agency.  The interview could include the following questions listed below.  It’s best to email the questions in advance to each person you interview.  Remember that the people you interview may have busy schedules, and may need to get approval from a supervisor. 

There is a reminder on the Course Resources page to be sure to contact the people you wish to interview very early in the semester!

 

Be sure to send a follow-up email or written note to thank them for taking the time to speak with you.

 

Part I – Potential Questions to Ask:

  • Name(s) and telephone number(s) of each individual interviewed
  • What is the person’s title?
  • What are their job responsibilities?
  • What do they do during a typical day?
  • What do they like most about their job?
  • What are their biggest challenges and how do they handle them?
  • How does the organization ensure it provides services that are respectful of the diversity of their clients?
  • Are there any policies (rules and regulations) of the agency or established by the funder that help or hinder providing services to their clients?
  • How were you introduced into the realm of educating industries on diversity and leadership?
  • Why did they decide to become an educator on diversity and leadership services and why they chose their specific industry?
  • What advice do they have for you in your career as an advocate and champion of diversity and leadership?
  • What drives you and keeps your attention to teach such a sensitive topic?
  • What are some external factors that impact your ability to effectively teach diversity and leadership?
  • Discuss the agencies in terms policy analysis, assessing the current policy issues demonstrated impacting the agency. Are the policies being implemented in a way that is sensitive to industry sites?

 

Responses to the interview questions may be submitted in question and answer. 

Part II - Agency Analysis Paper:  Write a 3 - 4 page paper typed, double spaced, discussing the following:

  • Identify your industry site location and sources of funding (federal, state, local or private funds).
  • Describe the agencies leaders or employees by race, ethnicity, age, gender and religion (when possible).
  • Describe the client population(s) served by each agency by providing demographic data such as age, gender, social class, ethnicity/race, etc. Use yearly statistics from the agency.
  • Describe the tone of the interview, the environment, the office culture.
  • What other range of services is offered by the agency and how does it complement its ability to promote / teach diversity and leadership?
  • Compare and contrast the two agencies in terms or services, industry served and other relevant similarities or differences.

 

Submission is due Sunday by 11:59 pm.
