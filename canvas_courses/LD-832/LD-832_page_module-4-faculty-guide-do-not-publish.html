Module Overview

This module focuses on understanding the inequities faced by LGBTQIAP+ individuals in the workplace and the benefits of fostering an inclusive environment. Through discussions, readings, and a video assignment, students will identify key workplace issues affecting the LGBTQIAP+ community, analyze these issues using course texts, and present the benefits of creating inclusive workplace policies.

Module Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will:

  1. Identify the unique challenges and inequities faced by LGBTQIAP+ individuals in the workplace.
  2. Analyze workplace practices and policies related to LGBTQIAP+ inclusion using course readings and case studies.
  3. Present the benefits of fostering LGBTQIAP+ inclusion in the workplace through a video assignment.
  4. Reflect on practical strategies for improving workplace policies and culture to promote LGBTQIAP+ equity.

Instructor Role:

  • Guide students to think critically about systemic and individual-level barriers to LGBTQIAP+ inclusion in the workplace.
  • Encourage the use of research or course readings to support their arguments.
  • Monitor the discussion for respectful and thoughtful engagement, ensuring students approach the subject sensitively.
  • Ensure students draw on the text to provide evidence-based analysis of LGBTQIAP+ workplace issues.
  • Encourage students to propose realistic and actionable solutions that organizations can implement.
  • Guide the discussion toward practical applications of the course material in workplace settings.
  • Encourage creativity and use compelling examples or case studies to strengthen the argument for LGBTQIAP+ inclusion.

Conclusion

This module empowers students to critically explore the systemic challenges faced by LGBTQIAP+ individuals in the workplace and to present the case for inclusion through discussions, text analysis, and a video assignment. By understanding the ethical and business benefits of LGBTQIAP+ inclusion, students will be better equipped to advocate for policies and practices that create equitable workplaces for all employees.
