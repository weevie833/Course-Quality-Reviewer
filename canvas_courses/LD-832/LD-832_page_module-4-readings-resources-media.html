Overview

LGBTQIAP+ inclusion in the workplace is one that is both advancing significantly in some places and still very much threatened in others.  Equality in the Workplace considers the advancements and barriers still facing the LGBTQIAP+ community in organization’s today and the impact that this has on all of us.
