Module Overview

This module focuses on gender in the workplace, exploring how systemic gender inequities have been highlighted and exacerbated by the COVID-19 pandemic, as well as how work-life balance differs across genders. Students will engage in two discussion boards: one discussing the impact of COVID-19 on gender inequities in the workplace and the other examining gender dynamics in work-life balance. These discussions aim to deepen students’ understanding of how gender influences workplace experiences and how organizations can address these challenges.

Module Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will:

  1. Analyze how the COVID-19 pandemic has affected gender inequities in the workplace.
  2. Reflect on work-life balance challenges and how these differ across genders.
  3. Explore strategies to foster gender equity and support work-life balance in professional settings.
  4. Synthesize course concepts to understand better how gender impacts workplace dynamics and equity.

Instructor Role:

  • Encourage students to ground their responses in research or real-world examples, such as data on job loss rates or changes in caregiving responsibilities by gender.
  • Guide the conversation toward practical solutions and organizational strategies for addressing gender inequities exacerbated by the pandemic.
  • Monitor the discussion to ensure respectful engagement and critical thinking.
  • Encourage students to connect personal experiences with broader societal trends and organizational policies related to gender and work-life balance.
  • Foster a discussion on actionable strategies workplaces can implement to support employees in achieving work-life balance, focusing on gender inclusivity.
  • Monitor the discussion to ensure respectful and thoughtful dialogue, as work-life balance issues can be profoundly personal and influenced by cultural or societal norms.

Conclusion

This module allows students to critically explore how gender inequities manifest in the workplace, particularly during times of crisis like the COVID-19 pandemic, and how gender shapes the experience of work-life balance. Through discussions, reflection, and optional assignments, students will deepen their understanding of gender-related challenges in professional settings and be better equipped to advocate for gender equity and inclusivity in their future workplaces.
