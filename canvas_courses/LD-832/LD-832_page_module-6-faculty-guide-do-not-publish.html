Module Overview

This module focuses on understanding communication styles and methods, with a particular emphasis on how these impact diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI) in workplace environments. Students will engage in two discussion boards: one discussing a breakdown in communication and the other analyzing inclusion practices in their workplace. These discussions will lay the foundation for an agency analysis assignment where students will conduct interviews to assess the communication and inclusion practices of two to three organizations.

Module Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will:

  1. Analyze communication breakdowns.
  2. Critically reflect on the inclusion practices and communication methods in their workplace.
  3. Conduct interviews to assess communication and inclusion strategies in different organizations.
  4. Synthesize interview findings in an agency analysis, comparing and contrasting communication methods and inclusion practices across organizations.

Instructor Role:

  • Guide students in reflecting on their personal or observed experiences and connecting them to communication theories and methods discussed in the course.
  • Monitor the discussion for depth of reflection and ensure that students are providing thoughtful responses that are grounded in course material.
  • Encourage students to provide detailed examples and connect their workplace analysis to broader communication and diversity theories.
  • Facilitate the discussion by prompting students to think critically about the effectiveness of workplace communication systems in promoting inclusion.

Module Wrap-Up and Key Takeaways

  • Instructor Reflection: Consider posting a final message summarizing the module's key themes, focusing on the importance of effective communication in fostering inclusive workplaces.

  • Encourage Future Application:

    • Recommend that students take what they have learned from their agency analysis and reflect on how they can apply these insights to their workplace or future professional environments.

Conclusion

This module integrates theoretical concepts of communication styles with real-world practice, encouraging students to critically reflect on how communication methods influence workplace inclusivity. Through discussion boards, interviews, and an agency analysis paper, students will gain a deep understanding of how different organizations approach communication and what strategies can be used to foster inclusive environments. This hands-on learning will help students bridge the gap between theory and practice, preparing them to be inclusive leaders in their professional fields.
