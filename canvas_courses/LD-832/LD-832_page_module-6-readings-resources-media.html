Overview

One of the most fundamental aspects of how we create culture is through communication. As we expand our understanding of communication methods, we can begin to recognize that there are important considerations for how we communicate and those influences within our places of work.  This module will address how we can better understand the various vernaculars used by various communities and how we can embrace them for inclusivity at work.
