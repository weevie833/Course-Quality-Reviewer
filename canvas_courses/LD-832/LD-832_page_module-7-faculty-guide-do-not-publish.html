Module Overview

This module focuses on fostering inclusive workplaces through the lens of inclusive leadership and understanding systemic inequalities. Students will engage in two discussion boards: one on the concept of inclusive leadership and the other on the Sandra Bland case, using this case to reflect on racial injustice and its implications for diversity and inclusion. Additionally, students will submit their final reflective paper, synthesizing course content with their learning and reflections on fostering inclusivity in workplace environments.

Module Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will:

  1. Analyze the role of inclusive leadership in fostering diversity and equity in workplaces.
  2. Critically engage with the Sandra Bland case and its implications for workplace inclusivity and racial justice.
  3. Demonstrate an understanding of systemic issues affecting diversity and inclusion in professional settings.
  4. Synthesize course learnings in a reflective final paper, drawing on the text and personal reflections.

Instructor Role:

  • Facilitate the discussion by encouraging students to explore connections between systemic racial issues and workplace inclusivity.
  • Ensure that students approach the topic with sensitivity and thoughtfulness, particularly given its emotional and serious nature.
  • Provide additional context or resources on the Sandra Bland case to deepen the discussion.
  • Instructor Reflection: Consider posting a final summary message or video that highlights the module's key themes and encourages students to apply what they have learned about inclusive leadership and workplace diversity in the future.

  • Encourage Ongoing Engagement:

    • Recommend professional organizations, books, or ongoing learning opportunities related to DEI for students who want to continue exploring these topics.

Conclusion

This module integrates theoretical concepts of inclusive leadership with practical, real-world cases like the Sandra Bland case to deepen students’ understanding of diversity, equity, and inclusion in workplace settings. Through discussions and reflective writing, students are encouraged to critically examine systemic issues and consider how they can foster more inclusive work environments. The final reflective paper based on the text provides an opportunity for students to synthesize their learning, preparing them to be inclusive leaders in their future professional contexts.
