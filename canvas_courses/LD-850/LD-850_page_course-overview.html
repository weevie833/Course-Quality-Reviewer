The journey began a while ago as you entered the graduate program, it is here and now that you establish the path you have been preparing to take. Part of the challenge is that you are making your own path, so not only is it daunting to look at the road ahead but you are building the road as you go.  

The good news is that I am here to help you along the way!  I want you to enjoy this leg of your journey as it may be the most challenging but it will also be the most rewarding.  Be prepared to push yourselves...now let's get started!
