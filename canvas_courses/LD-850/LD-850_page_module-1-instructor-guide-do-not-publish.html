This module aims to provide students with an understanding of how to leverage library resources, guide them through the creation of an e-portfolio, and prepare them to submit their final leadership paper. The collaboration with the library staff will help students become more resourceful researchers, while the e-portfolio component will support their professional and academic growth.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students should be able to:

  • Navigate library resources efficiently for academic and professional research.
  • Develop an e-portfolio that showcases their skills, experiences, and leadership competencies.
  • Submit their final leadership paper with a clear, well-structured argument supported by credible sources.

Instructor Notes:

  • Coordinate with the librarian in advance to ensure the session is tailored to the needs of your students.
  • Please encourage students to ask questions and take notes, as this will directly support their leadership paper research.
