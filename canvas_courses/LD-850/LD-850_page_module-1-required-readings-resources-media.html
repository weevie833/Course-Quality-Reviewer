Overview

Welcome to module one!  

At this point, you have completed the Capstone Research Proposal and any required IRB documents. If you plan on using human participants in your research, you should have already submitted your IRB Application. Once you’ve received approval you can begin your research.

This week we will discuss how to analyze data regardless of the methodology you have chosen for your Capstone Research Paper (survey, interview, case study, or mixed methods).
