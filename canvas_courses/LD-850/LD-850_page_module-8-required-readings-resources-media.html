Resources & Media - How to Submit Your Capstone ePortfolio

  1. Go to your Capstone ePortfolio: Dashboard>Portfolios> Choose the ePortfolio you created. The correct one will have a "Home" page inside with further instructions.
  2. To submit, go to the "Home" page. Adding content here such as an introduction or summary is required in order to submit.
  3. Once there is content, you will see that the submit button on the right side of the home page turn green and no longer has a line through it. This will allow you to submit your final version as well as drafts during the term to obtain feedback from your instructor.

For a step by step tutorial on submitting Chalk & Wire assignments, review the instructions in the "Create a new Capstone e-Portfolio"  guide (at the bottom).
