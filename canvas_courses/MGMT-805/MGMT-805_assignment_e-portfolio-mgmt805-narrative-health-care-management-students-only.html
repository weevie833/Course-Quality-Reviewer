This Narrative and subsequent Narratives are designed to assist you in developing your Capstone ePortfolio which is the major project of the capstone course in this program. In the capstone course you will integrate and build on your prior course work and experiences and demonstrate your professional skills through a Capstone ePortfolio project that you create and present. By reflecting on the past, present, and future throughout the entire program you are documenting your professional growth over time and building aspects of your capstone project.

Take a moment to think about everything you have experienced in this course and up to this point in your program. Next, connect your course work, real world experiences, professional standards and what you have learned from your colleagues in this narrative. For example, you may want to cover:

  • What are the key lessons learned from this course? Your program up to this point?
  • Reflect on the learning outcomes of this course and their importance to the overall program.
  • Describe your successes and how you achieved them.
  • Describe your challenges and what you learned from them?
  • Describe how you have grown professionally as related to the programmatic standards.
  • Reflect and describe how the skills learned from this course were or can be applied in your professional life.
  • Describe your next goals in your professional development. Use the programmatic professional standards as a guideline.
