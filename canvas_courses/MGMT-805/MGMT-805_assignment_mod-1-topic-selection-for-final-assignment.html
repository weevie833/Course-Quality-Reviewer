Toward the end of this class (feel free to look ahead for specifics), you will complete a 8-10 page paper on a topic related to organizational behavior. To find examples for potential topics, please review our course textbook. There is a large variety to choose from.

For this week:

This week you will select your topic and begin preparing for your final paper. In one paragraph, describe your selection and include a research question. What is a research question? It is what you are seeking to find within your topic. 

 

Example: My topic is Organizational Culture and my research question is: How does a CEO's leadership style impact the culture of their organization?
