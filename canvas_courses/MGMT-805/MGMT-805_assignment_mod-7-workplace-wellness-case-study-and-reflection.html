Directions

Building on this week’s ‘Read, Review, Watch’ content, this assignment requires an evaluation of a contemporary (within the past five years) organizational wellness program or initiative that contributed to increased performance. You will assess the organization and its wellness initiatives through individual research—this should not be based on a personal experience.

  • The aim for this assignment is to evaluate the wellness initiative or program and the applicability of the core concepts we have reviewed this module.
  • Please use the template as a guide to complete your assessment.
  • Your work should include a minimum of two sources aside from our textbook, and all references should be properly listed and cited following APA 7th Edition style guidelines.

Important Note: Your submission may be considered for inclusion in the ‘complementary examples’ section of our course textbook. Consideration will be based on content and relevance in relation to contemporary events, as well as new and existing submissions. Should your work get selected, you will receive a message from the course instructor with more information, including a link to provide consent.

If you are opposed to having your work included in the course textbook, please add a comment in your assignment submission such as “exclude from textbook” or send a message to the course instructor to inform them.
