Welcome to MGMT 805: Organizational Behavior!

Over the next eight weeks, we will explore a variety of areas related to organizations, workplace dynamics, and effective management practices. Some of the areas include social diversity, perceptions and behaviors, culture, team and group dynamics, conflict and negotiation, decision-making, motivational factors, communication methods, change management, and organizational design and structure.

Each module will cover new areas and include various modes of engagement and assessment, including discussion forums, short papers, ePortfolio activities, and a final signature paper. Please review the course syllabus for a more detailed breakdown of the policies and requirements, and reach out at any time with questions or to schedule a meeting.
