Overview

In this module, we explore the intersection of ethical decision-making and inclusive leadership. Specifically, we will operationalize "inclusion" using Bourke and Dillon’s Six Signature Traits of Inclusive Leadership: Commitment, Courage, Cognizance of Bias, Curiosity, Cultural Intelligence, and Collaboration. You will learn to evaluate how these specific traits—or the lack thereof—impact organizational sustainability, team performance, and ethical climates.
