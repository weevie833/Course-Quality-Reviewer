Objective: Reflective writing is a process of identifying, questioning, and critically evaluating course-based learning opportunities integrated with your own observations, experiences, impressions, beliefs, assumptions, or biases. It describes how this process stimulated a new or creative understanding of the course content. 

Assignment Description: This assignment requires you to write a reflective paper that describes and explains, in an introspective, first-person narrative, your reactions and feelings about how you experienced learning throughout the course and up to this point in your program. This reflection should connect your coursework, real-world experiences, professional standards, and insights gained from interactions with colleagues. 

Guidelines: 

Length and Format: 

  • The reflection narrative should be 3-5 pages in length. 
  • Use APA v7 style for formatting and citations (if used). 

Content: 

  • Key Lessons Learned: Reflect on the essential lessons from this course and your overall program to date. How have these lessons shaped your understanding of virtualization and cloud systems? 
  • Learning Outcomes: Discuss the learning outcomes of this course and their significance to the broader goals of the Information Technology Management program. 
  • Successes and Achievements: Describe the successes you have experienced in this course. How did you achieve these successes, and what strategies were effective? 
  • Challenges and Learning: Identify the challenges you encountered. What did you learn from these challenges, and how have they contributed to your growth? 
  • Professional Growth: Reflect on how you have grown professionally in relation to the programmatic standards. How has this course contributed to your professional development? 
  • Application of Skills: Explain how the skills learned in this course were or can be applied in your professional life. Provide specific examples. 
  • Future Goals: Describe your next goals in your professional development. Use the program standards as a guideline for setting these goals. 

Submission: 

  • Submit your reflection narrative by Friday at 11:59 pm.
