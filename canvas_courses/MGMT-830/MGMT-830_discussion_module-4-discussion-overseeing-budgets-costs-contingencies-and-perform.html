Managing an IT project requires careful planning and strategic oversight to ensure that budgets, costs, contingencies, and performance are effectively managed.

In this discussion, reflect on the key strategies that are essential for overseeing these aspects of IT project management.

  • What specific strategies would you use to manage project budgets and control costs effectively?
  • How would you handle unexpected contingencies or risks that could impact the project?
  • What methods or tools would you use to measure and evaluate project performance throughout its lifecycle?
