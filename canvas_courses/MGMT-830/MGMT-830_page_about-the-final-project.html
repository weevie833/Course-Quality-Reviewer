This week, we will start developing the deliverable for your final project, due in Module 8. The final project entails creating a comprehensive multi-page document detailing the processes involved in integrating an Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system into your company. This project will emphasize the strategic and financial considerations of selecting and implementing an ERP system.

The context for this project is a clothing manufacturing company that currently manages its ERP-related tasks using Excel and Outlook, and is seeking to transition away from paper-based processes. The company has allocated a budget of $1.5 million for this initiative and aims to integrate the ERP system with its existing Customer Relationship Management (CRM) environment. This integration will enable the sales team to view inventory, create and track orders, and send quoting information through the ERP system.

Over the next four weeks, we will develop a detailed implementation plan for this project. You are required to find an IT planning template online and customize it to meet the deliverable requirements for Modules 4, 5, 6, 7, and 8. Each week, you will submit the updated template along with the weekly assignment, progressively building out the project plan.
