Overview

In Module 2, we will delve into the critical topic of IT governance and policy. Participants will gain a comprehensive understanding of the frameworks and principles that guide ethical, transparent, and accountable IT project management. Key areas covered will include:

  • Stakeholder Analysis: Identifying and understanding the interests, expectations, and influence of various stakeholders involved in IT projects, including employees, product teams, customers, vendors, and regulators.
  • Governance Frameworks: Exploring established governance frameworks, such as COBIT and ITIL, and their application in IT project management.
  • Policy Development: Discussing the process of creating and implementing effective IT policies to address issues such as data security, risk management, and compliance.
  • Ethical Considerations: Examining the ethical implications of IT projects and exploring best practices for ensuring responsible and socially conscious IT practices.

By the end of this week, students will be equipped with the knowledge and skills necessary to navigate complex IT governance landscapes and contribute to the successful delivery of IT projects that align with organizational objectives and ethical standards.
