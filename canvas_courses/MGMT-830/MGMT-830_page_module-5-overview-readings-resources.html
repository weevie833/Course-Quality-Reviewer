Overview

In Module 5, we will explore the critical aspects of IT project financial management. Participants will gain insights into the key factors that contribute to the financial success of IT projects and identify potential gaps that may hinder their profitability. Topics covered will include:

  • Key Factors for Success: Analyzing the essential elements that drive the financial performance of IT projects, such as effective budgeting, cost control, risk management, and stakeholder engagement.
  • Potential Gaps: Identifying common financial challenges that IT projects may encounter, such as cost overruns, schedule delays, and inadequate resource allocation.
  • Basic Financial Estimation: Learning techniques for estimating the financial costs and benefits of IT projects, including top-down and bottom-up approaches.

By the end of this week, students will be equipped with a practical understanding of the financial management principles that are essential for the successful execution of IT projects.
