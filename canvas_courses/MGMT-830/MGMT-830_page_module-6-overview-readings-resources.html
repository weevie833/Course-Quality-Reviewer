Overview

In Module 6, we will delve into the critical topic of IT value realization. Participants will gain a comprehensive understanding of how to measure and maximize the benefits of IT investments. Key areas covered will include:

  • Key Factors for Success: Exploring the essential elements that contribute to the successful realization of IT value, such as clear objectives, effective communication, and ongoing monitoring.
  • Cost Savings: Analyzing various strategies for identifying and capturing cost savings through IT initiatives, including process optimization, resource consolidation, and technology adoption.
  • Corporate Resources: Examining how IT investments can optimize the utilization of corporate resources, such as human capital, infrastructure, and financial assets.
  • Time to Market: Discussing the importance of accelerating time to market for IT projects and exploring techniques for achieving faster delivery.
  • Vendor Management: Examining the role of effective vendor management in maximizing IT value, including contract negotiation, performance evaluation, and risk mitigation.

By the end of this week, students will be equipped with the knowledge and skills to assess the value of IT investments, identify opportunities for improvement, and ensure that IT projects deliver the expected business outcomes
