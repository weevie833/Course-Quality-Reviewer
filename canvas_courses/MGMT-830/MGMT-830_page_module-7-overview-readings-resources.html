Overview

In Module 7, we will explore the emerging trends and challenges shaping the future of IT strategic planning and financial management. Participants will gain insights into the evolving landscape of IT and discuss the need for organizations to adapt their strategies and financial practices to remain competitive. Topics covered will include:

  • Controlling IT Strategy: Examining the importance of aligning IT strategies with overall business objectives and developing effective governance mechanisms to ensure accountability and control.
  • Future of IT: Discussing emerging technologies and their potential impact on IT strategy and financial management, such as artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and the Internet of Things.
  • The Need for Agility: Exploring the concept of agility in IT and discussing the importance of being adaptable to changing market conditions and technological advancements.
  • State of Constant Change: Emphasizing the need for organizations to embrace a mindset of continuous improvement and innovation to stay ahead of the curve.

By the end of this week, students will be equipped with a forward-looking perspective on the future of IT and the skills necessary to navigate the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead.
