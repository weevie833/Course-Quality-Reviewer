For example, when I was the acting CIO for a small consulting company, I was informed my usual laptop provider was immediately dropping the product line preferred by our consultants. This disrupted my supply chain providing a certain laptop model and configuration to new consultants. I needed to either find a new provider or change the model and configuration for any future new hires.  The lack of advance notice impacted my trust in the original supplier, so I looked for another source. I found one that could provide the model and configuration that was our standard, but at a higher price, thereby negatively impacting my budget forecasts.
