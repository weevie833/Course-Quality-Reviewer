Overview

Welcome to Principles of Operations Management. This interdisciplinary course will provide you an opportunity in Module 2, to better understand the role of an operations manager within your organization, be it within the healthcare industry or some other industry sector. You will be exposed to the differences between the operations manager and project manager roles, differences that often lead to organizational conflict and employee confusion. You will also read about where those roles are actually similar. Then, each of the subsequent modules will focus on a particular operations management function and its impact on an organization and/or a project.  

This first module is focused on introducing ourselves to each other and to highlight our individual experiences with operations management. The outcome of this week’s work should be a self-awareness of what you know about operations management and where there are gaps in that knowledge.
