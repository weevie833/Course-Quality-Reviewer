Overview

A key function within operations management is the determination and fulfillment of the resource and capacity needs across the organization, including any needs required of a project. Forecasting is the function that provides the operations manager that insight When developing the forecast, the operations manager is balancing needs to the organization’s capacity. The operations manager will understand the current and future resource needs of their organization including items such as workspace, the equipment needed to perform their job, etc. These forecasts then become the basis for development of budgets. A realistic forecast of demands enables operations managers and those they support to make sound business decisions.
