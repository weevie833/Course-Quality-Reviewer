Overview

In the first part of this module, we will explore the integrated operations functions. Imagine the inventory and distribution systems of WalMart, Amazon, Wayfair and other mega-online retailers. These organizations could not have grown into the behemoths they are now without the applications of excellent inventory control systems that flagged when constraints were being reached. This In this part of the module, you will explore the concept of aggregate planning and its value to overall inventory planning and management. You will read about Materials Requirement Package (MRP) software and its contribution to efficiencies and performance, and about its counterpart Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software packages. 

The second part of this module focuses on conflict identification and resolution. Operations and project managers share common challenges in determining when a conflict is developing and resolving it before a significant impact to the organization occurs. Sometimes these conflicts arise around the availability of a certain resource to support a given activity, with ambiguous priorities existing. These conflicts can have impacts to the organization’s supply chain, especially if the conflict is between two departments relying on the same vendor for a product, and the vendor can provide only so many. This In this part of the module, you will be exposed you to how these conflicts arise and what you can undertake to prevent them. You will review techniques and tools you can deploy to resolve a conflict while it is still isolated.
