Overview

Maintenance of resources of all types is critical to the smooth operations of an organization be it a manufacturing line or the equipment within an oncology radiation unit. This also includes the “maintenance” of human resources – are they properly trained; will they still be employed here when they are needed? Operations need the resources to be reliable, ready to be used when called upon. This module will expose you to how that reliability can be determined and how a continuous maintenance program contributes to that reliability.  

You will also explore the concepts of lean operations and short-term scheduling, two additional methods used by operations managers to ensure smooth operations in the most efficient manner.
