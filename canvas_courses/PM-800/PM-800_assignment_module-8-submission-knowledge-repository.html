Organizations with a strong project management culture use knowledge repositories to retain important information that may be used by future projects.  E.g. Estimating time or cost may be based on the history of prior projects that is part of a repository.  In the PMBOK® Guide reading this week, one of the outputs of Close Project or Phase is organizational process asset updates, which is usually stored in a repository for future retrieval.  Project documents may be included in repositories.  This is a list of some possible project documents:

  • Assumptions log
  • Basis of estimates
  • Change log
  • Issue log
  • Lessons learned register
  • Milestone list
  • Project Communications
  • Project Management Plan
  • Project Status Reports
  • Quality control measurements
  • Quality reports
  • Requirements documentation
  • Risk register
  • Risk report

For this assignment, choose at least 5 project documents that you think are important to retain for future reference.  Justify your choices; you need not limit your choices to this list.  Feel free to use a project with which you have experience.
