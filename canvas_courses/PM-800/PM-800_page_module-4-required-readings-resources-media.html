Overview

Resource planning and management are vital components of the project management plan. The Resource Management component of your Project Management Plan defines the staffing requirements and potential HR challenges and solutions. It also defines the skills, numbers, and timing of resources needed to complete the project. While planning for resources, consider training, compensation issues, or incentives that you might employ to ensure project success. Other logistical items such as work locations, collaboration tools and vacation scheduling are addressed in the HR Plan. The Resource Management plan clearly defines the roles and responsibilities of team members and defines the project organizational structure.

At the end of this module you should be able to:

  1. Contrast the differences between time and resource-constrained projects.
  2. Explain options for accelerating project completion.
  3. Develop the resource breakdown structure for a project.
  4. Identify the resource requirements of a project and develop the resource management.
  5. Assign resources to the tasks identified in a schedule.
