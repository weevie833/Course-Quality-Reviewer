This courses is one of several graduate courses that qualify for a badge, also referred to as a digital badge.

A digital badge represents a skill, achievement, or experience. The college defines and evaluates skills and/or competencies that are represented in the badge. Sharing badges is learner-controlled and open across multiple technology platforms, including social media, blogs, online portfolios, etc. Two badges within the learning pathway will result in the micro-credential or “stacked badge”. To learn more, see the UNH Micro-credentials website.

Students who are not already enrolled in a degree program may consider continuing on their educational pathway by completing the other required courses required for a certificate or graduate degree. See Credentials to Degree.

Receiving a Badge (digital credential)

When you successfully complete this course with a  B- (80%) or better, you will receive an email approximately 11 days after the end of the course to let you know that your badge is available. You can access your badge from the email, from within the course in which you earned the badge, or you can access it from the external Parchment Badges website. More information will be provided at the end of the course.

Additional information about your badge

Badges can be shared on social media platforms such as LinkedIn, on a website, or an email signature. UNH uses the Parchment Badges platform.
