When you successfully complete this course with a B- (80%) or better, you will receive an email in your UNH email account approximately 11 days after the end of the course.

You can access your badge: 

  • via the "Go to the Badge" button in the email,
  • via the Parchment Badges link in the Canvas course navigation of the course in which you earned the badge, or
  • externally via the Parchment Badges website.

See the important information below for details for each option.

Badges can be shared on social media platforms such as LinkedIn, on a website, or an email signature.  You can download and print the badge as well. See the on the How to Share Micro-credentials section on the UNH Micro-credentials website.

Logging into Parchment Badges

  1. If you are logging into Parchment Badges for the first time:
    • Go to the Parchment Badges website and select Signin at the top of the page. 
    • Select Create an account (Option 1 shown below).
    • Enter the the email addressed used in Canvas which is typically: [username]@unh.edu. For example, if your username is abc123, then your email address would be: abc123@unh.edu. To lookup your UNH email in Canvas, go to your Account found at the top of the blue global navigation and select Settings. Your UNH email will be displayed.
    • Enter a new password for Parchment badges and submit.
  2. After you have set up your account, you can log into Parchment Badges by using single sign-on (SSO) (Option 2) or by using your UNH email and password you setup in step 1.
  3. We highly recommend that you update your Parchment account immediately to add a personal email address. After you graduate from UNH, you will be able to use your personal email to log into the Parchment Badges website.
