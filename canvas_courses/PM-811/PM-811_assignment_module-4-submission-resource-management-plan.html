In this assignment you will define your Resource / HR Management Plan for the party project. The deliverable should include roles, responsibilities, reporting relationships (including virtual team members, if any), required skills, and a staffing management plan. 

At a minimum, your submission should contain the following components:

  • Project Organization
  • Chart Roles and Responsibilities
  • Staffing Management Plan
  • On-boarding
  • Ramp Up / Down
  • Off-boarding
  • Resource Requirements
  • Skill Definitions
  • Training Requirements
  • Rewards and Recognition
  • Compliance and Regulatory Requirements

You may use the template provided or one of your choice. The plan must be in Microsoft Word format, Times New Roman, 12 point font, with double spacing.
