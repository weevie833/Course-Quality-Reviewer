Overview

Now that your sponsor has signed off on the project charter and you have an established scope, you are ready to begin defining the specifics for the project. You will also work on defining, sequencing and estimating activity durations. You will analyze the constraints and dependencies and develop the schedule with planned dates for completing project activities

At the end of this module you should be able to:

  1. Understand the importance of a WBS to the management of projects
  2. Create a WBS for your project
  3. Understand estimating techniques such as analogous, parametric, three-point etc
  4. Understand the components of a schedule management plan and develop it for the party project
  5. Understand constraints and dependencies within your project
  6. Identify the critical path for your project
  7. Develop the schedule for your project
