Overview

Resource planning and management is a vital component of the Project Management Plan. This plan defines the staffing requirements and potential HR challenges and solutions. It also defines the skills, numbers, and timing of resources needed to complete the project. While planning for resources, consider training, compensation issues, or incentives that you might employ to ensure project success. Other logistical items such as work locations, collaboration tools and vacation scheduling are addressed in the HR Plan. The Resource / HR Management plan clearly defines the roles and responsibilities of team members and defines the project organizational structure.

At the end of this module you should be able to:

  1. Develop the resource breakdown structure for your project
  2. Identify the resource requirements for your project and develop the resource management plan for your project
  3. Assign resources to the activities identified earlier
