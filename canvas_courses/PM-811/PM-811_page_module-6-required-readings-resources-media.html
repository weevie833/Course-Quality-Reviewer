Overview

Risk planning should be initiated early in the planning stage of the project. It may be useful to invite subject matter experts and stakeholders such as customers or regulators to these initial sessions.  The risk management plan is a component of the overall project management plan and it defines how the project risk management activities will be structured and executed.

At the end of this module you should be able to:

  1. Understand the application of tools such as Brainstorming, SWOT analysis, root cause analysis etc. when identifying project risks
  2. Identify individual project risks and overall project risk
  3. Perform qualitative and quantitative risk analysis
  4. Understand the risk response strategies for threats, opportunities, overall project risk and plan the appropriate risk responses for your project
  5. Develop the risk register and risk management plan for your project
