Overview

After estimating the costs of the project and determining the potential impact of risks, you can now develop the budget and obtain approval of it..

At the end of this module you should be able to:

  1. Determine the cost baseline
  2. Understand how to use visual tools such as cumulative cost curve
  3. Determine the project budget by adding the management reserve to the cost baseline
