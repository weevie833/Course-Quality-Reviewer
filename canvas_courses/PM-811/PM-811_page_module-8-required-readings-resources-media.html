Overview

By creating the work breakdown structure, you now have the project deliverables in smaller, more manageable components. You will now identify all the stakeholders i.e. the people, groups or organizations that could impact or be impacted by the project.

The success of a project depends on effective and efficient communications. The communications management plan helps determine how relevant project information can be distributed and received in a timely manner. One group of individuals with whom you should establish regular communications are your project stakeholders. Drawing from your overall communications plan, you can identify all the stakeholders i.e. the people, groups or organizations that could impact or be impacted by the project.

At the end of this module you should be able to:

  1. Identify and understand your stakeholders
  2. Understand the culture and organizational structure
  3. Develop the RACI matrix for your project
  4. Determine the appropriate communication strategy for your project
  5. Understand the diverse communication needs of stakeholders
