For this assignment you will evaluate the impact of a change to the party project. The change can be one that is requested by the customer, the sponsor, or the project team (examples: 5 team members from Department Y will now be joining in or Aunt Sue sprained her wrist and now cannot make the party favors). The deliverables for this assignment are:

  1. A completed change request form to submit for approval.  Follow the change management plan.  
  2. A separate MS Word document describing the evaluation of the change.  This should include an analysis of alternative approaches considered and the rationale for the recommendation.  The alternative analyses must each include a budget evaluation.
  3. Does this change impact schedule? If yes, Include an updated schedule based on the change.
  4. What other project artifacts will be impacted by this change? Update the documents and upload them here.

Validate that the project information is kept up to date i.e. version controlled.
