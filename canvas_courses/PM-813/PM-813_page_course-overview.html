Welcome to PM813: Delivering Business Value

After the completion of project planning activities, the assembled project team can begin on the delivery of the expected product(s) and their associated business values, while the project manager is actively managing the project and leading the project team towards the project’s goals and the delivery of business value. This course will provide you the tools and techniques that can be applied to evaluate a project’s true status. You will explore earned value management, cost management, risk assessments and other techniques used to ensure project success. Within this course, you will have the opportunity to apply the various methods and tools used in a successful project to the party project, in preparation for doing the same within your capstone projects.

Please note that Project Quality Management, Project Procurement Management and Human Resource Planning will be covered in greater detail in future courses.
