Overview

In the previous module, you learned how to use EVMS to assess the progress of the project. Implementing EVMS can be complex for certain types of projects. There are ways to scale back the implementation. In addition, the project team must iteratively assess and prioritize risks as they occur. Executing a well-defined risk management plan will limit losses and ensure project success.

At the end of this module you should be able to:

  1. Explain how to implement EVMS.
  2. Describe how to use partial EVMS.
  3. Characterize two emerging practices in schedule monitoring: Earned Schedule & Critical Chain
  4. Monitor and control risks as they develop.
  5. Discuss developing renewable and sustainable risk management practices.
