Overview

Effective communication is vital to disseminating information about schedules, risks/ issues, status, etc, and receiving feedback throughout the project. The communication needs of the project team and stakeholders have been analyzed during planning. During execution, the project manager must ensure project information and updates are communicated and feedback received.

At the end of this module you should be able to:

  1. Attribute how project managers’ communication strategies impact the success of a project.
  2. Describe how organizational culture informs communication strategies.
  3. Establish strategies to keep team members engaged throughout the project.
