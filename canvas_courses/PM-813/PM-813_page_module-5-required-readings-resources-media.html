Overview

Organization resources are limited and conflicting objectives across projects or stakeholders can lead to conflict. Project managers must be able to understand and manage conflict through the project.

At the end of this module you should be able to:

  1. Identify and manage conflict in a project environment.
  2. Decide when and how to escalate issues effectively.
