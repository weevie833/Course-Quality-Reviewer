Project teams are tasked with delivering business value in an ethical manner. Here’s a list of ethical issues possible in project management and/or project procurement.  Describe an ethical issue you have seen or read about within the sphere of procurement management.  How would you address it?   

Here are some examples of possible ethics violations: 

  • Violation of Basic Rights of Workers on the Project 
  • Ignoring Health or Safety Standards 
  • Making Shady Deals (being asked by an inspector for a "tip" to be ensured that work passed inspection) 
  • Wrong People Hired for or on the Job (Family Members?) 
  • Bias 
  • Blaming Others 
  • Taking Ballgame Tickets (or any gift) from a Supplier/Vendor/Contractor 
  • Customs officials expecting bribes before releasing materials or equipment
