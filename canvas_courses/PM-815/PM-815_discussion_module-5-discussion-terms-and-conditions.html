A contractual term is "any provision forming part of the contract.” Terms and Conditions set the rights and obligations of the parties of the contract, and these are set when the contract is entered into or awarded. 

There are several terms and conditions; if not listed below and you can research on your own. Providing a minimum of one reference, discuss its significance.  Which party or parties (buyer, seller or both) is it intended to benefit or protect, what is the consequence if this term is breached, etc.

  • Payment terms 
  • Non-solicitation of employees 
  • Termination clauses 
  • Parol evidence 
  • "Time is of the essence" 
  • Express Warranties 
  • Implied Warranties
