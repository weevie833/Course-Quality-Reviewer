Overview

In today’s complex business world, we often see parts of a project being contracted out to other companies. This may be done to reduce costs, shorten turnaround time, or address a lack of in-house expertise. Making procurement decisions is an important part of the project planning process.

At the end of this module, you should be able to:

  1. Understand why companies outsource.
  2. Understand ethics in procurement management.
  3. Understand the advantages and disadvantages of centralized versus decentralized procurement.
  4. Understand positional bargaining versus principled negotiation.
