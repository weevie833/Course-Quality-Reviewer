Overview

After the high-level scope of a project is defined, project teams often conduct a make-or-buy analysis to determine what should be produced in-house and what should be purchased. Procurement strategy is defined, and planning artifacts can now be developed.

At the end of this module, you should be able to:

  1. Conduct make-or-buy analysis.
  2. Understand the RFP process.
  3. Prepare a procurement statement of work.
  4. Understand and create source selection criteria.
