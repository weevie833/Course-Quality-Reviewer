Overview

In this module, you learn more about the art of negotiation. You will also practice preparing for a negotiation in the party project.

At the end of this module, you should be able to:

  1. Understand what makes an effective negotiator.
  2. Prepare for a negotiation by defining Interview Strategy, Technique, and Questions.
