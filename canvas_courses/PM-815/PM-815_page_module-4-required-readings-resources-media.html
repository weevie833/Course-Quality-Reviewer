Overview

While most companies have legal departments, it is important for a project manager to understand the legal considerations of procurement. You will also read parts of the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR), which is the primary regulation used by government agencies in their acquisition of supplies and services.

At the end of this module, you should be able to:

  1. Understand legal considerations of procurement.
  2. Understand sealed bidding as defined in the FAR.
