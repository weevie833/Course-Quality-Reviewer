Overview

In this module, you will continue your study of the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR). You will also prepare the first draft of the Procurement Management Plan for the party project.

At the end of this module, you should be able to:

  1. Understand contracting by negotiation as defined in the FAR.
  2. Understand the components of a procurement management plan.

Readings
