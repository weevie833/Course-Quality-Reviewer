Overview

In this module, you will wrap up the planning activities and move to conducting procurements. You will also learn how to develop the Best Alternative to a Negotiated Agreement (BATNA).

At the end of this module, you should be able to:

  1. Understand how to conduct procurements.
  2. Understand the importance of BATNA and practice developing it for the project.
