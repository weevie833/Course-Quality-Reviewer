Overview

In this module, you will learn how to control procurements all the way through closeout. At times, despite excellent planning and good supplier relations, conflicts occur.

At the end of this module, you should be able to:

  1. Understand how to control procurements.
  2. Understand dispute resolution techniques.

Readings

  • Guth, Chapter 4: Dispute Resolution and Chapter 5: Monitor and Control Procurements
