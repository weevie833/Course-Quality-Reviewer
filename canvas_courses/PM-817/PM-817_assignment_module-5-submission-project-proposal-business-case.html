At this point in the PM program, you are well into the planning phase of your Capstone project. However, we are going to challenge you to step back and create a proposal/business case for your project. This is normally done before the charter is approved, as part of the process to gain funding. In the portfolio management process, proposals are reviewed by a cross functional committee to determine which projects to pursue.

For this assignment, you will create a Project Proposal / Business Case for your Capstone project. The Proposal will include significant considerations for the committee to determine if the project should proceed to the feasibility study phase. Your submission should address the following components:

  • Business Overview / Background information
  • Statement of the Business Opportunity or Problem to be solved
  • Desired Outcome
  • High Level Requirements
  • Strategic Alignment (to organization’s strategy, mission, goals)
  • Any expected or known challenges, risks and/or dependencies
  • Impacts (processes, technologies or business areas to be impacted)
  • Probable cost
  • Probable duration
  • Sponsor and key stakeholders
  • Key project team members
