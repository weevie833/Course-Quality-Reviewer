How does PPM help in managing strategic change within the organization?

In responding to this discussion question, consider an organization that is going through transformation change. This may be due to a M&A, a reorganization, or where an organization may be re-visioning who they are in order to meet customer demands.

  • How will PPM help to achieve such strategic change?
  • What challenges is the organization likely to have and how can PPM help to address those challenges?
