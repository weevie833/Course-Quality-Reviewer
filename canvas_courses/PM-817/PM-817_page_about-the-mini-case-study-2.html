Food-for-All non-profit focuses on ensuring that no one ever goes to bed hungry. The headquarters, and largest office is headquartered in Newark, New Jersey.  Branches of the non-profit are located in Chicago; Portland, OR; Detroit, MI; Los Angeles; Opa-Locka, Florida; and Claiborne Parish, Louisiana. There is a great need for Food-for-All’s services and support among a number of poor communities. 

Therefore, there is a need to raise significant money to cover this growing need. Food-for-All has a goal of raising $10 million over the next 18 months. Regular generous donors have already contributed $1.5 million.

They have gathered a number of ideas from employees, board members, volunteers and previous donors for fundraising events; as shown below.

  • A golf outing

  • A bowling tournament
  • A 5K walk/run and picnic
  • A raffle Scavenger hunt
  • A “paint and sip” night
  • Casino night
  • Phone-a-thon
  • Auction
  • Bingo tournament
  • Talent show
  • Haunted house
  • Carnival
  • Bake sale
  • Holiday Caroling
  • Night at the museum
  • Mardi GrasConcert

Food-for-All has limited resources. They run on a very limited budget. While there are paid employees at each location, the majority of the workforce is comprised of volunteers. It is unlikely that Food-for-All will be able to undertake more than four (4) fundraising events over the time period necessary, given their limited resources. 

You have been providing volunteer project management services to Food-for-All for a number of years. You know that it is imperative that the right events be chosen to help them achieve their goal of $700,000. You have an upcoming meeting with the Executive Director and the Board Members. Your goal is to help them determine which fundraising events they should select as part of their fundraising project portfolio for the next 18 months.

RESPOND TO THE FOLLOWING QUESTIONS

  • How would you recommend that the Executive Director and Board Members prioritize the list of potential fundraising events? What 5 – 8 criteria might you recommend and why? How would you weight the criteria?
  • What other information would be important/valuable to know beforehand in order to ensure a productive session?
  • What challenges do you expect you will have in helping them come to consensus to make a decision? How will you address those challenges?
