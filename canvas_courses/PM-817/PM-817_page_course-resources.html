PM817: Final Research Paper Assignment Instructions

Your final project is to submit a 12 – 14-page paper (inclusive of the abstract and citations, references, and resources) on the topic of:

The Benefits and Challenges of Implementing and Engaging the Organization and its Leadership in Project Portfolio Management.

Using materials from the class, including articles, white papers, and videos from this course, as well as research done externally to back up/support your ideas, present on what you believe are the most significant benefits and challenges of implementing and engaging an organization and its leadership in project portfolio management. Consider from the perspective of why PPM may be of value to a smaller organization as well as to large, global organizations.

APA Guidelines, 7th edition, are to be followed (requires a title page, abstract and running head). A minimum of four (4) sources for your paper (excluding Wikipedia, which is not considered a scholarly resource) are to be properly cited per APA Guidelines, 7th edition.

Don’t forgot the UNH Online Library as a great option to do research for your team paper.
