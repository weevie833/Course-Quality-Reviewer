Overview

This week we will further explore how a project portfolio integrates into organizational management.  We will discuss how executive support for PPM processes can be gained. These processes, used to establish and maintain a project portfolio, will be analyzed along with the role of the project management office (PMO).  We will discuss how PPM processes are typically integrated into customary organizational management processes. The differences between program management and portfolio management will also be examined.

Learning Objectives:

  1. Portfolio management and the organization
  2. Organizational influences on portfolio management
  3. Portfolio management process groups
