Overview

Building on your recent learning of PPM, this week we will discuss how PPM integrates with organizational strategic planning and how it supports delivery of business objectives. We will look at how the development of a Portfolio Strategic Plan can be used to support alignments of project-based work with overall organizational strategies.

Learning Objectives:

  1. Align projects to business goals
  2. Use PPM to deliver on organizational strategy
  3. Create a Portfolio Strategic Plan and Charter to ensure alignment
