Overview

Organizational strategic plans and the projects identified to support those plans can, and do, change over time. In this module, we will investigate the impact strategic changes have on the project portfolio and on the people within the organization.  We will discuss how organizational change management techniques can be used to support changes to the project portfolio contents.

Learning Objectives:

  1. Use PPM to manage strategic change within the organization
  2. Manage the people side of change to ensure project and program success

Readings

  • Standard for Portfolio Management, 4th Edition Chapter 3, Sections 3.10 - 3.10.2
  • PMI: Managing the people side of change - an applied framework
  • PMI: Change Management in the Strategic Alignment of Project Portfolios
  • Delivering on Strategy - The Power of PPM
