Overview

Project portfolios are useful with the oversight of new product development (NPD) projects. Often these projects are critical to the achievement of the organization's objectives. Yet they compete with other more tactical but just as critical, projects. In this module, we will develop an understanding of the NPD Lifecyle and how the use of stage gates fits within PPM. Additionally, we will look at IT projects and how PPM can be deployed to support that subset of an organization’s projects.

Learning Objectives:

  1. Use KPI’s to assist in resource allocation between NPD and tactial projects
  2. Appropriately design and use stage gates in NPD projects
  3. Increase the success of IT projects/investments through PPM
