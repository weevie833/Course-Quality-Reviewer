Overview

Over the duration of this course, you have been exposed to PPM and its role in supporting the achievement of corporate strategic objectives.  You should now:

  • Have an appreciation for PPM, its challenges in implementation, the value it brings to organizations along with how to achieve success in PPM implementation.
  • Be able to present on the value of PPM to leadership.
  • Understand PPM and its positive impact in new product development, in strategic project development and resource management.
  • The link between PPM and the organizational strategy as well as how PPM supports an organization's ability to achieve its long-term objectives.

Learning Objectives:

  1. Share your lessons learned/Ah-Ha moments from the course
  2. What are your next steps?
