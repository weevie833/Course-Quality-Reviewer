Overview

This is the introductory week to the topic of quality management. By the end of this week, you will be able to discuss the development of quality as a discipline. You will understand the increasing pressure our global economy places on delivering quality.

One of the key topics within a discussion of quality management is the design and implementation of a quality management approach for your project or organization. This approach should be documented within a Quality Management Plan. You will have the opportunity over the next several weeks to develop such a plan.
