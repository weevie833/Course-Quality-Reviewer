Overview

Up to now, the focus has been on quality management in its entirety. This week, attention is shifted to quality assurance, determining the impact the quality system is having on overall performance. You will learn how to ensure quality processes are being used and measured, and then improved as warranted by the quality metrics.

You will learn about the different types of audits performed as part of a quality management plan, and when each is used. How to respond to an audit will also be addressed.
