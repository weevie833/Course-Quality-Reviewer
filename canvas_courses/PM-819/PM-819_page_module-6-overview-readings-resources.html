Overview

Last week you were introduced to quality management audits and the systems needed to support them. You looked at a range of metrics that could be used to measure quality.

This week we will look at how organizations can plan and implement an organizational quality management system. You will investigate the challenges frequently faced and how they can be overcome.   

You will also complete the development of your draft Project Quality Management Plan, introducing the requirements for a successful quality management program implementation and continuous operations of the same.
