Discussion Forum Guidelines

  • Post your own primary question replies by Day 3, but make sure to check out posts and replies from your fellow students by Day 6.
  • Regarding student replies, I'd like you to reply to AT LEAST one student post in EACH of the forum questions. Of course, more replies is always encouraged...3 posts will just be meeting the bare minimum.
  • The discussion forum is where a lot of our shared learning takes place, so I would encourage as much participation as you can to keep the forum lively and productive.
