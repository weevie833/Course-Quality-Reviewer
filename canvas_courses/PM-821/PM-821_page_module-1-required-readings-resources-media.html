Overview

In this initial module, you will be introduced to the Theory of Constraints (ToC), and how its processes can be useful for a project manager to apply.  

Module 1 Learning Outcomes:

  1. Explain the relationship of ToC to the Division of Labor concepts associated with manufacturing
  2. Describe the key concepts associated with the Theory of Constraints
  3. Have a basic understanding of how the ToC can be applied to project management processes

Readings

  • Goldratt - ToC, pp. 1 - 93 (chapters 1 -12)
  • Robert Dirgo, Chapter 5: Looking Forward
