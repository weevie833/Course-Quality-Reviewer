Overview

Applying the ToC processes to your project management practices can create a significant change for your project stakeholders, including your team members. This week you will explore how to approach that change.

Module 2 Outcomes:

  1. Discuss the organizational change dynamics initiated when adopting a ToC approach to project management
  2. Analyze the change resistance one might expect to experience when implementing the changes
  3. Describe one method to bring others on-board

Readings

  • Goldratt - ToC, pp 94 - 179 (chapters 13 - 22)
