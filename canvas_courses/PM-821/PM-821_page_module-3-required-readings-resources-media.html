Overview

In this module, you will explore the techniques by which the ToC can be applied to normal business processes and effect a cost improvement for the organization.

Module 3 Outcomes:

  1. Describe how the Theory of Constraints can be applied to business processes
  2. Understand when the Theory of Constraints is applicable within our project planning and monitoring activities

Readings

  • Goldratt - TOC, pp. 180 - 337 (Chapters 27 - 34)
