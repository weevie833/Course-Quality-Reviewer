Overview

This week, you will shift your focus from ToC to Critical Chain Scheduling and its relationship to the ToC. and how it differs from the Critical Path method of scheduling.

Module 4 Outcomes:

  1. Explain what is meant by Critical Chain Schedule Management
  2. Describe the relationship between Theory of Constraints and Critical Chain Schedule management
