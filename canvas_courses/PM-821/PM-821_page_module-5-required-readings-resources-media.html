Overview

This module shifts your focus to the business benefits associated with using Critical Chain schedules. You will explore resource allocations within Critical Chain scheduling versus within Critical Path scheduling, identifying how local cost savings are realized through the former.

Module 5 Outcomes:

  1. Describe the benefits of Critical Chain scheduling
  2. Identify the three mechanisms deployed within critical chain scheduling to maintain schedule safety

Readings

  • Goldratt - CC, pp. 73 - 103 (chapters 10 - 13)
