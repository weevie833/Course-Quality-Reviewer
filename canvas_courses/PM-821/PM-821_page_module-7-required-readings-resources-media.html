Overview

As you learned in last week’s assignment, the Critical Chain method of schedule development represents a different approach from the Critical Path Method most of us have learned.  This module provides you the opportunity to compare the two methods and to develop an understanding of when one is preferable to the other.

Module 7 Outcomes:

  1. Compare Critical Chain scheduling to Critical Path scheduling
  2. Describe the benefits of Critical Chain scheduling
