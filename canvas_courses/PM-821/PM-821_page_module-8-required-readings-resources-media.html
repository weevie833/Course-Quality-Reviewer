Overview

This week is the course wrap-up week. By now, you have learned about the Theory of Constraints, Critical Chain Scheduling and how these approaches can be used to enhance your project management approaches.

Module 8 Outcomes:

  1. Describe the relationship between Theory of Constraints and Critical Chain Project Management
  2. Explain how constraint management supports project completion
  3. Identify the change resistance to be overcome when transitioning to Critical Chain Project Management
