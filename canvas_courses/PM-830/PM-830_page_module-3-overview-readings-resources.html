Overview

Last week, students examined various life cycle models used in IT project management. This week, the focus shifts to a deeper exploration of the most prominent models in practice. Students will analyze real-world scenarios where project-specific life cycle models were developed and applied. They will evaluate a case study and justify their selection of an appropriate life cycle model. To demonstrate their understanding, students will create and share a video presentation explaining their choice and its relevance to the project.

Module Objectives:

  1. Analyze and compare prominent life cycle models within the context of IT project management.
  2. Critically evaluate real-world scenarios to determine the most suitable life cycle model for a given project.
  3. Effectively communicate project model selections and justifications to project stakeholders.
