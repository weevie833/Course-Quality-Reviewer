Overview

Quality control, once a labor-intensive task performed at the end of a project, is now seamlessly integrated into core project activities. As requirements are defined, teams also determine the success criteria and responsible stakeholders. Quality control planning occurs alongside development, ensuring that testing is an ongoing process rather than a final checkpoint. In addition to building software, teams must create test scenarios and scripts to validate functionality before deployment to operations.

In this module, students will explore and discuss the latest testing tools available. They will also examine how AI is transforming quality control, from tracking requirements to analyzing test results, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in the process.
