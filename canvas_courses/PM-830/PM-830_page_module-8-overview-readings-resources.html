Overview

The integration of artificial intelligence (AI) into IT project processes and implementations presents both opportunities and challenges for organizations. While AI can enhance efficiency, automate tasks, and improve decision-making, it also introduces risks related to security, ethical concerns, and implementation complexity. Similarly, emerging technologies to successfully plan, execute, and govern technology-driven projects such as blockchain, cloud computing, and the Internet of Things (IoT) bring transformative potential but also complicate IT project management due to regulatory uncertainties, interoperability issues, and evolving standards. Understanding these risks and benefits is critical for IT project managers succeed and deliver the project’s anticipated value.

Module Objectives:

  1. Analyze the risks and benefits of integrating AI into IT project management processes and IT implementations.
  2. Evaluate the complexities and challenges associated with IT projects involving emerging technologies such as blockchain, cloud computing, and IoT.
  3. Develop strategies to mitigate risks and maximize the benefits of emerging technologies in IT project management.

Key Topics:

  • AI in IT project management (automation, predictive analytics, decision support)
  • Risks of AI adoption (security, bias, ethical concerns, regulatory compliance)
  • Blockchain applications and challenges in IT projects
  • Cloud computing integration and its implications for project management
  • IoT and cybersecurity risks in IT implementations
  • Managing uncertainty and regulatory challenges in emerging technologies
  • Strategies for risk mitigation and successful adoption of new technologies
