Develop a 3–4 page AI-Supported Project Charter for a project of your choice.

Your charter should demonstrate the effective use of at least one AI tool (e.g., ChatGPT, Wrike AI, Notion AI) to assist in structuring objectives, defining scope, or identifying stakeholders.

In addition, include a 1-page critical reflection that addresses:

  • How the AI tool contributed to your work.
  • The specific value it added to the chartering process.
  • Any risks, limitations, or ethical concerns you observed in relying on AI for this task.

Your submission should balance technical completeness (a well-structured charter) with critical analysis of AI’s role in supporting, enhancing, or potentially constraining project initiation.

Note: This assignment is designated Level 2 - AI-Assisted Idea Generation and Structuring. AI can be used in the assignment for brainstorming, creating structures, and generating ideas for improving work. However, no AI content is allowed in the final submission.
