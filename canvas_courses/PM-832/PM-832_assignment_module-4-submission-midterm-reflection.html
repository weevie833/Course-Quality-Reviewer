In this midterm writing assignment, you will review each of the AI use cases you have employed so far as part of your project development from the perspective your work as a project manager. Follow the guidelines below to describe the holistic implications of this kind of work for professional PM practice and for yourself as a practitioner within this transformation. 

  • Which AI tools that we have tried so far are the most relevant to your growth as a PM? Why? 
  • How has the use of these AI tools influenced your sense of readiness for using AI in your professional PM work? Cite examples. 
  • How do you anticipate AI will affect how your organization conducts its business? What role do you expect to play in that transformation? 
  • Projecting beyond your organization, how does the influx of AI-based tools transform PM holistically as a profession from your perspective? Consider: 
    • The range of new Competencies project managers will need to master. 
    • The basis of substantiating the integrity of one’s analyses, predictions, and directives.  
    • The basis of accountability for the consequences of AI-assisted analyses, predictions, and directives. 
    • The basis of distinguishing the business value of a PM with/without AI proficiency 
    • Other variables that you have observed or anticipate emerging based on scholarly research and field-based experiences. 
  • Offer a brief conclusion that describes how the first half of the course informs how you intend to approach the remaining tasks for the second half of the course. 

Length: 5–7 double-spaced pages (excluding references).

Format: APA 7th Edition, with proper in-text citations and a reference list.
