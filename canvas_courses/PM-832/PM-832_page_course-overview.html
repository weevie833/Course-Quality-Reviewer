Welcome to PM 832 – Artificial Intelligence in Project Management: Tools, Trends, and Techniques

This course introduces students to the ways in which AI is transforming project management practices. Across eight modules, you will engage in discussions, case studies, and applied assignments using AI tools to explore areas such as initiation, chartering, scheduling, cost estimating, risk management, and stakeholder engagement. Primary tasks include a Midterm AI Portfolio and a Final Integrated AI Project Plan. Weekly expectations include completing assigned readings, posting to discussion forums (one initial post and two replies), and submitting assignments on time. Students are encouraged to use available resources, including the UNH Library, tutoring, and technology support.

Required Texts:   

  1. AI and the Project Manager: How the Rise of Artificial Intelligence Will Change Your World -  Peter Taylor - 1st Ed., 2022
  2. Guide to Project Management Body of Knowledge Project Management Institute - 7th Ed, 2021
