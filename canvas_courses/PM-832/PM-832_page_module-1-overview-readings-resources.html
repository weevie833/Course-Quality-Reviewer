Overview

This module introduces the fundamentals of artificial intelligence (AI) in project management. You will explore how AI tools are transforming initiation, planning, and early execution activities in modern projects. Drawing on Taylor’s AI and the Project Manager (2022), we will consider how AI can enhance efficiency, augment decision-making, and reshape project roles—while also surfacing new ethical and organizational challenges.

By engaging in readings, discussions, and assignments, you will gain a deeper understanding of both the opportunities and the risks that AI presents. This first module sets the stage for the rest of the course by demonstrating how AI supports critical early processes such as chartering and stakeholder identification. The knowledge gained here will connect directly to Module 2, where you will apply AI capabilities to develop a project charter and begin thinking critically about early stakeholder engagement.
