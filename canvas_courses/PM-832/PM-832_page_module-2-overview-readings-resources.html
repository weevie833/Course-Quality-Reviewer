Overview

In this module, you will explore how AI can support one of the earliest and most critical project management deliverables: the project charter. You will examine how AI tools can streamline charter development, identify and engage stakeholders, and enhance decision-making at the project’s start. Drawing from Taylor’s AI and the Project Manager (2022), this module emphasizes both the efficiency gains and the ethical or organizational challenges that come with integrating AI at such an early phase.

This module builds directly on the foundations of Module 1, moving from general opportunities and challenges of AI to a specific application—early project initiation. The insights you gain here will prepare you for Module 3, where you will extend AI applications into work breakdown structures (WBS) and estimating.
