Overview

In this module, you'll explore how AI can support two foundational planning activities—developing a Work Breakdown Structure (WBS) and generating project estimates. You'll learn how AI tools help decompose complex deliverables into manageable tasks and enhance estimating accuracy by leveraging data from historical records, predictive models, and dataset analysis.

This builds directly on the charter-focused approach from Module 2, guiding you through the logical progression to task-level planning and costing. The skills you develop here will prepare you for Module 4, which focuses on scheduling and resource allocation.
