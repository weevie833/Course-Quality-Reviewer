Overview

In this module, you will apply AI tools to project scheduling and resource planning. You will explore how AI can optimize schedules by predicting task durations, identifying dependencies, and suggesting resource allocations using historical data and real-time project inputs.

This module builds directly on the WBS and estimating work from Module 3. Once the scope has been decomposed and tasks estimated, the next step is to integrate those elements into a schedule and resource plan. The skills gained here are essential as you prepare for the Midterm Portfolio, which consolidates your work from Modules 1–4.

    • Chapter 5: AI and Planning – approximately pp. 23–33.

    • Project Performance Domains:

      • Development Approach and Life Cycle (pp. 20–24)
    • Models, Methods, and Artifacts:
      • Schedule Management Models (pp. 223–225)
      • Critical Path Method (CPM) (pp. 227–229)

  • Case Study: Examining the Security of Artificial Intelligence in Project Management: A Case Study of AI-driven Project Scheduling and Resource Allocation in Information Systems Projects (11 pgs)

    • Author: Grace E. Egbedion, Middle Tennessee State University
    • Publication: April 2025 (IRE Journals)
    • Overview: This study examines how AI tools are integrated in IT project settings to enhance scheduling and resource allocation, while also considering security implications. It highlights efficiency and adaptability improvements that AI enables in complex, dynamic environments.
