Overview

This module explores how AI enhances risk identification, assessment, and response planning through predictive analytics. You will examine how AI systems process large datasets to identify emerging risks, calculate probabilities, and recommend mitigation strategies in real time. At the same time, you will reflect on the limitations and ethical challenges of predictive models, including issues of transparency, bias, and accountability.

Building on the financial lens of Module 5, this module shifts into uncertainty and risk—a central concern of all projects. The insights you gain here will prepare you for Module 7, where AI’s role in stakeholder engagement and communication strategies will be explored.
