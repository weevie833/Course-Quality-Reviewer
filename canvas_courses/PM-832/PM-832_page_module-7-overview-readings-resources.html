Overview

In this module, you will examine how AI can support stakeholder engagement by personalizing communication, analyzing sentiment, and improving collaboration. AI-enabled tools can tailor messages for diverse stakeholder groups, monitor communication effectiveness, and even predict potential conflicts.

Building on the predictive capabilities explored in Module 6, this module shifts the focus from managing risks to managing people — ensuring stakeholders remain engaged, informed, and supportive. The skills developed here will prepare you for Module 8, where you will evaluate the ethical, organizational, and leadership implications of using AI in project management.
