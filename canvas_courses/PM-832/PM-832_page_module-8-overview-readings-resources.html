Overview

This final module delves into the ethical, organizational, and leadership dimensions of integrating AI into project management. While AI brings significant advantages, it also raises concerns in areas such as data privacy, algorithmic bias, accountability, and workforce change. As emerging project leaders, you will explore how to balance innovation with responsibility.

Building on Module 7's focus on stakeholders, this module expands the lens toward organizational culture and leadership. You’ll consider how leaders can promote ethical AI adoption, establish governance frameworks, and maintain transparency. This module prepares you for your Final Reflective Paper and Final Project, where you'll demonstrate comprehensive mastery of course themes.
