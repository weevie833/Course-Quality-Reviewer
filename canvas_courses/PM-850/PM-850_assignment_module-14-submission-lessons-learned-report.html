Imagine at this point your Capstone Project has been completed, or maybe it has actually completed.  At this point, you need to capture any Lessons Learned from the project experienced by your team members and sponsor. These lessons should focus on the process and artifacts, not on personnel or team assignments. For every lesson recorded, be sure there is an appropriate action step identified. It could be "keep doing this" or it could be "stop doing this" or "change this to ….."  Be as succinct as you can; this report is intended to assist the next project to be better performed.
