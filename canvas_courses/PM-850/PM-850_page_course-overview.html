Welcome to PM 850, the Integrative Capstone Course, your final course before graduation!   

In this course, you will populate your Capstone ePortfolio within the PM850 course in Chalk and Wire.  Many of the required documents you will have already seen in other course work. In this course, you will have the opportunity to review those documents, IF they pertain to your Capstone Project, and ensure they represent your best possible work on your project. If the previous documents do not reflect your chosen project, then you will have time in this course to develop the missing artifacts.   

The culmination of this course is a presentation of your project work to the College, your final Capstone Project ePortfolio, and your Narrative.  This course is integrative as it permits you to pull together and integrate all the knowledge you gained within the program. And it permits you to get excited about your new knowledge!  

So, approach this course with a sense of excitement and celebration - you are just weeks away from graduating!!!
