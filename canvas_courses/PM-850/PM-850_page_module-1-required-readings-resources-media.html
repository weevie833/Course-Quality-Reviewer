Overview

This week we will focus on reviewing the Business Case and Scope Statement. You must formally gain approval from your project sponsor for the project you will manage in this course.  You will then prepare your Capstone Project’s WBS and Schedule. You will also identify your schedule's critical path, either highlighting the activities on that path or attaching a document that lists the critical path. You do not need to submit a network diagram, although creating one will be helpful in identifying your critical path.

You will also establish your Capstone ePortfolio in Chalk & Wire, using the provided illustrated PDF guides below for creating it.
