Overview

You will explore the tools available to you to assist in the controlling and monitoring of your capstone project. We will discuss baseline management and reporting against the baseline. You and your sponsor should have a conversation regarding how status reporting should be conducted on the project. You will then identify a status report template for your project and establish a schedule of status reporting from your team to you, and then on to the project sponsor.  The template and schedule will be shared with the team during your kick-off meeting. 

Organize and schedule a kick-off meeting for the project with your sponsor and team members.  The first agenda item should be Team Introductions. Review Charter, Schedule, Change Control, and Risk Management procedures during the meeting. There should be an agenda and minutes from the meeting. Review the project plans with meeting participants and address any questions that might arise.  After the meeting, be sure your team has the tools and accesses needed to perform the assigned work.

    • next week's plans
    • priority issues to be escalated
    • priority risks to be escalated
    • deliverables & milestones key dates
    • critical path tasks / status (can be a picture from msp schedule embedded)
