This week's assignment asks you to identify an organization of your choice that you are familiar with and describe the product(s) of that organization.

This selection serves as the foundation for the final paper of this course. Over the next 5 weeks, you will be developing an analysis of this organization, its primary product, and the associated supply chain.

The analysis will focus on the nature of the product offerings of the entity and articulate where product and service elements are found within the organization.  Relate that complex nature to the organization’s supply chain for that product.  Where do they source materials from?  How are they handling the related inventory and shipping?  What business and political issues threaten that supply chain?  How is the current supply chain functioning? 

Your final paper will be due Week 7, with an outline and an abstract due Week 3.  That final paper should be no more than 15 pages in length and compliant with APA 7 formatting, including reference pages.
