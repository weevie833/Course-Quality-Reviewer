Overview

Operations Management consists of the business processes and activities that, when performed, result in the production and delivery of a firm’s products and services to customers. In many cases, these are typically viewed as primary processes of an organization that are carefully measured, optimized, and improved. While operations vary greatly from one industry to another, ensuring continuity and productivity is key to achieving maximum continuity and operating profit. 

Learning Objectives 

  1. Evaluate how to improve the production environment 
  2. Explain the effects of technology on the production environment 
  3. Identify how technology has changed
