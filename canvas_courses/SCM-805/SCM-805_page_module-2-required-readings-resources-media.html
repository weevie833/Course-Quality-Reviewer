Overview

In the first part of this module, we will explore supply chain operations in a global environment. We will investigate various supply chain strategies used by organizations in the global economy, strategies ranging from total outsourcing to offshore procurement to reshoring.  The various competitive advantages associated with each strategy will be analyzed for effectiveness in varying scenarios.  We will also address the challenges associated with geopolitical issues and the impacts they have on a supply chain. 

The second half of this module is focused on forecasting. Forecasting drives many OM (operational management) decisions. In this section, we cover an array of forecasting techniques. In most cases, before using forecasting techniques, a manager must make two decisions, (1) what to forecast, and (2) what type of forecasting technique to select for different items. Forecasting consists of seven steps, steps we will review through the lens of global organizations such as Bath & Body Works and Lowes. We will look at the differences between quantitative and qualitative forecasting methods. Finally, we will review certain mathematical computations used when generating a forecast.
