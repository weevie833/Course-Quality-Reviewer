Overview

In the first part of this module, we will explore understanding the Spectrum of the Supply Chain. Supply chain management remains one of the hottest topics in business today, as companies continue to search for effective ways to drive costs out of the supply chain. Note, supply chain management can be really effective when cooperation among members of the chain, via close partnerships, optimize decisions and support each other’s core competency. Optimizing decisions for the entire chain versus individual partners bodes well for all participants. 

Learning Objectives 

  1. Explain the strategic drivers of the supply chain 
  2. Identify six strategic sourcing strategies 
  3. Explain major issues in logistics management 
  4. Describe the steps in supplier selection 

The second part of this module focuses on Supply Chain Analytics. Data, Data, and More Data. Supply chain management is a big topic with many different elements. Scholars have conducted a lot of quantitive aspects of research for many years. 

Learning Objectives 

  1. Explain and measure the bullwhip effect 
  2. Describe the factor-weighting approach to supplier evaluation 
  3. Evaluate the techniques for supply chains and explain why they are important
