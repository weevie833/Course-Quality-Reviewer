Overview

In the first part of this module, we will explore inventory management. Some suggest that inventory management is the heart and soul of operations management. Everyone stores inventory in their homes and workplaces, so they should be able to see the connection of inventory management and the importance and keep a balance on levels. 

Learning Objectives 

  1. Conduct an ABC analysis 
  2. Apply the production order quantity 
  3. Explain and use the quantity discount model 

The second part of this module focuses on Aggregate Planning and Sales and Operations Planning (S&OP). Aggregate planning is comprised of two segments, capacity options and demand options. Demand options are often implemented to smooth out the demand flow, which can enhance and make production planning much simpler and less costly. Capacity options are explored once demand options have been determined and demand patterns have been forecasted. 

Learning Objectives 

  1. Define sales and operations planning 
  2. Define aggregate planning 
  3. Identify optional strategies for developing an aggregate plan
