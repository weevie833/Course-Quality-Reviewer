Overview

MRP is primarily a planning tool, as opposed to an inventory or short-term dispatching technique. The ERP section describes how firms are trying to significantly extend MRP and create fully integrated business functions where all relevant information is easily obtainable as needed. 

Learning Objectives 

  1. Develop a product structure 
  2. Build a gross requirements plan 
  3. Build a net requirements plan 
  4. Describe MRP & ERP
