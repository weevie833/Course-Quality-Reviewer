Overview

In the world of operations management, some consider maintenance to be hidden in the scheme of the operations management strategy. Proper maintenance is vital to the success of companies because the costs of failure can be very large. Ideally, the reliability concept fits well in this chapter on maintenance, in particular because of preventative maintenance might improve the reliability on products and services. 

Learning Objectives 

  1. Describe how to improve system reliability 
  2. Compare preventative and breakdown maintenance costs 
  3. Describe and explain metrics on how to improve maintenance
