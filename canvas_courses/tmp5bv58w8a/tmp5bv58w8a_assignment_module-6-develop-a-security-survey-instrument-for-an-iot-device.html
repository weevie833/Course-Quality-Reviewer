Assignment Overview

The rapid expansion of Internet of Things (IoT) devices has raised important questions around security, privacy, and data protection. In this assignment, you will develop a security survey instrument designed to assess the security posture of a specific IoT device.

Your survey should reflect an understanding of current IoT vulnerabilities and best practices in cybersecurity. This assignment is an opportunity to demonstrate critical thinking, awareness of real-world threats, and your ability to develop tools for risk assessment.

Assignment Instructions

1 - Select an IoT Device
Choose a specific IoT device as the focus of your survey. Examples include:

  • Smart locks
  • Smart thermostats
  • Connected home appliances
  • Wearable health devices
  • Smart cameras or security systems
  • Industrial IoT sensors

2 - Research Common Security Risks
Identify the most common and relevant security risks associated with your chosen device. Consider aspects such as:

  • Data transmission and storage
  • Device authentication and access control
  • Software/firmware updates
  • Vulnerability to hacking or physical tampering
  • Privacy concerns

3 - Design the Security Survey Instrument
Develop a survey tool (approximately 10–15 questions) that could be used by IT professionals, auditors, or vendors to evaluate the security readiness of the selected device. Your survey may include:

  • Yes/No questions
  • Likert scale items
  • Short-answer questions
  • Multiple choice items

Each question should be clearly written, and you should briefly explain the purpose or rationale for including it.

4 - Analysis and Reflection
Conclude your paper with a short analysis (1–2 pages) discussing:

  • How your survey addresses key security concerns
  • How it could be used in real-world assessments
  • Potential limitations of the instrument

Formatting Guidelines

  • Paper length: 3–4 pages (not including title or reference pages)
  • Font: 12-point Times New Roman
  • Spacing: Double-spaced
  • APA 7th edition formatting and citations
  • Include at least two reputable sources to support your survey design
Note: This assignment is designated Level 1 - No AI. The assignment must be completed entirely without AI assistance. The assignment must be produced relying solely on conventional research and study methods. AI must not be used at any point during the production of the assignment.
