Ethical dilemmas are a common challenge in professional settings, requiring individuals to make difficult decisions that align with ethical standards and codes of conduct. In this discussion, you will apply the knowledge gained from the readings, media, and Codes of Ethics studied in this course to navigate a complex ethical scenario.

Now, imagine you are an IT Manager at a tech company developing a new software application. During a routine code review, you discover that one of your team members has implemented a feature that collects user data without explicit consent. This data collection significantly enhances the application's functionality and market competitiveness, but it violates both company policy and industry-standard ethical guidelines. 

  1. Reflect on the ethical principles and Codes of Ethics relevant to this situation.
  2. Use specific references from the course readings and media to support your analysis.
  3. Propose a course of action to address the ethical dilemma.
  4. Justify your decision based on ethical theories and guidelines discussed in the course.
  5. Consider the potential consequences of your chosen action for all stakeholders involved, including the team members, the company, and the users.
