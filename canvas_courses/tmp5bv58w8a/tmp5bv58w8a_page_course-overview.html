Welcome to CMPL 810 - Current and Emerging Technology

This course provides an in-depth exploration of current and emerging technologies that are transforming industries and society. Students will investigate the latest advancements in fields such as artificial intelligence, blockchain, Internet of Things (IoT), quantum computing, and cybersecurity. The course emphasizes critical analysis, ethical considerations, and the practical applications of these technologies in various sectors.  The students will demonstrate their knowledge of an emerging technology via a research paper due at the end of the term.
