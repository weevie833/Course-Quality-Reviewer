Overview

Building on the introduction, Week 2 dives into AI and ML, examining their principles and real-world applications. Participants will analyze successful business implementations, connecting AI/ML capabilities to business innovation. Participants will consider the ethical challenges associated with the adoption of an emerging technology and how these challenges are being addressed worldwide by vendors and government organizations, including the World Economic Forum and the UN. Reflections will help solidify understanding of how these technologies can drive efficiency and growth.

Module Objectives:

  • Respond to a situation-based ethical dilemma according to a Code of Ethics.
  • Locate and organize reliable sources for preparing a literature review related to your term project.

Readings

  • Read:  IEEE. (2019). Ethically Aligned Design: A Vision for Prioritizing Human Well-being with Autonomous and Intelligent Systems. (24 pages)
  • Read:  Prioritizing People and Planet as the Metrics for Responsible AI (26 pages)
