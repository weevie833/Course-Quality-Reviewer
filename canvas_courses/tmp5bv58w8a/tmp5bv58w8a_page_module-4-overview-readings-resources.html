Overview

Week 4 explores blockchain technology, its origins, and potential business applications. Participants will research specific industry uses, linking blockchain's decentralized nature to increased transparency and security. This week extends the discussion on AI/ML, emphasizing how different technologies address various business challenges.

Module Objectives:

  • Develop a perspective on the use of blockchain technologies and its implications for business.
  • Produce a draft of a literature review related to your term length project.

Readings

  • Read Chapters 7, 8, and 9, Vincent and Igou (40 pp)
  • Read: Bitcoin: A Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash System (8 pp)
