Overview

This week covers robotics automation, emphasizing its impact on business operations and efficiency. Participants will present on how robotics can transform various aspects of their business, linking to earlier discussions on AI and IoT. Reflections will consider the integration of robotics with other technologies for comprehensive automation strategies.

Module Objectives:

  • Develop a perspective on the use of robotics in a sector of society and its ethical implications.
  • Continue to work on your final research paper.
