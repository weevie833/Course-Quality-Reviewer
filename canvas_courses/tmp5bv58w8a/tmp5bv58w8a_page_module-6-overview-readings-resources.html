Overview

This week, the focus shifts to Quantum Computing and the IoT, exploring its integration into business processes. Participants will develop implementation plans for IoT solutions, learning how interconnected devices can optimize operations. The week's activities build on blockchain's impact, showing how combined technologies can further enhance business value.

Module Objectives:

  • Develop a position on the use of an IoT innovation for an organization.

Readings

  • Read:  What is IoT: The Internet of Things (10 pp)
  • OPTIONAL: Read:  The Internet of Things: Mapping the Value Beyond the Hype. (100 pp)
